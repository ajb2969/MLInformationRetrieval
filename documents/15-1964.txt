   Carboxynorspermidine decarboxylase      Carboxynorspermidine decarboxylase   Carboxynorspermidine decarboxylase (, carboxyspermidine decarboxylase , CANSDC , VC1623 (gene) ) is an enzyme with system name carboxynorspermidine carboxy-lyase (bis(3-aminopropyl)amine-forming) . 1 2 3 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) carboxynorspermidine    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   bis(3-aminopropyl)amine + CO 2   (2) carboxyspermidine    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    spermidine + CO 2    This enzyme contains pyridoxal 5'-phosphate.
