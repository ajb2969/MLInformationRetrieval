Carboxynorspermidine decarboxylase

Carboxynorspermidine decarboxylase (, carboxyspermidine decarboxylase, CANSDC, VC1623 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name carboxynorspermidine carboxy-lyase (bis(3-aminopropyl)amine-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) carboxynorspermidine

 
  bis(3-aminopropyl)amine + CO2

(2) carboxyspermidine

spermidine + CO2


This enzyme contains pyridoxal 5'-phosphate.
