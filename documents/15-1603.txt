Maltokinase

Maltokinase () is an enzyme with system name ATP:alpha-maltose 1-phosphotransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + maltose

 
  ADP + alpha-maltose 1-phosphate
 

This enzyme requires Mg2+ for activity.
