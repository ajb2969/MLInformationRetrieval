It is a papyrus  manuscript of the Acts of the Apostles , it contains Acts 4:31-37; 5:2-9; 6:1-6.8-15. The manuscript paleographically has been assigned to the 4th century. 1  The text is written in two columns per page, 25 lines per page. 2  The Greek text of this codex is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type .
