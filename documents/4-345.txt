   L-theory      L-theory   In mathematics algebraic L-theory is the K-theory of quadratic forms ; the term was coined by C. T. C. Wall , with L being used as the letter after K . Algebraic L -theory, also known as 'hermitian K -theory', is important in surgery theory .
