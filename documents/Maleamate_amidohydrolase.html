Maleamate amidohydrolase

Maleamate amidohydrolase (, NicF) is an enzyme with system name maleamate amidohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

maleamate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons maleate + NH3

The reaction is involved in the aerobic catabolism of nicotinic acid.
