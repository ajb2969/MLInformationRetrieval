   Real net output ratio      Real net output ratio   The Real Net Output Ratio (or Vertical Range of Manufacture ) describes in a value chain the fraction of the internal (company specific) production on the total production value of one company. The total production value of a company consists of internal production plus the sum of externally produced goods and services.
