Linalool dehydratase

Linalool dehydratase (, linalool hydro-lyase (myrcene-forming)) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-linalool hydro-lyase (myrcene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-linalool $\rightleftharpoons$ myrcene + H2O

In absence of oxygen this enzyme can also catalyse the isomerization of (3S)-linalool to geraniol.
