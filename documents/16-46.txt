(4S)-beta-phellandrene synthase (geranyl-diphosphate-cyclizing)

(4S)-beta-phellandrene synthase (geranyl-diphosphate-cyclizing) (, phellandrene synthase, (−)-beta-phellandrene synthase, (−)-(4S)-beta-phellandrene synthase) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing; (4S)-beta-phellandrene-forming).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate

(4S)-beta-phellandrene + diphosphate


This enzyme requires ion Mn2+.
