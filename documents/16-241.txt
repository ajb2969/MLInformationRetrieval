Tirucalladienol synthase

Tirucalladienol synthase (, PEN3) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, tirucalla-7,24-dien-3beta-ol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene 
 
 
 
  tirucalla-7,24-dien-3beta-ol
 

The product from Arabidopsis thaliana is tirucalla-7,24-dien-3beta-ol.
