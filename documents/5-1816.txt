   Light dressed state      Light dressed state   In the fields of atomic , molecular , and optical science, the term light dressed state refers to a quantum state of an atomic or molecular system interacting with a laser  light in terms of the Floquet picture , i.e. roughly like an atom or a molecule plus a photon . The Floquet picture is based on the Floquet theorem in differential equations with periodic coefficients.
