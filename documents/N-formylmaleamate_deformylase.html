N-formylmaleamate deformylase

N-formylmaleamate deformylase (, NicD) is an enzyme with system name N-formylmaleamic acid amidohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

N-formylmaleamic acid + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons maleamate + formate

The reaction is involved in the aerobic catabolism of nicotinic acid.
