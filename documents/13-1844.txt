   Arruda–Boyce model      Arruda–Boyce model   In continuum mechanics , an Arruda–Boyce model 1 is a hyperelastic  constitutive model used to describe the mechanical behavior of rubber and other polymeric substances. This model is based on the statistical mechanics of a material with a cubic representative volume element containing eight chains along the diagonal directions. The material is assumed to be incompressible .
