Tricyclene synthase

Tricyclene synthase (, TPS3) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing; tricyclene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate

tricyclene + diphosphate


The enzyme from Solanum lycopersicum (tomato) gives a mixture of tricyclene, camphene, beta-myrcene and limonene.
