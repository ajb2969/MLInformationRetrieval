   Violaxanthin de-epoxidase      Violaxanthin de-epoxidase   Violaxanthin de-epoxidase (, VDE ) is an enzyme with system name violaxanthin:ascorbate oxidoreductase . 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   violaxanthin + 2 L- ascorbate    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    zeaxanthin + 2 L- dehydroascorbate + 2 H 2 O (overall reaction)  (1a) violaxanthin + L-ascorbate   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   antheraxanthin + L-dehydroascorbate + H 2 O  (1b) antheraxanthin + L-ascorbate   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   zeaxanthin + L-dehydroascorbate + H 2 O   Violaxanthin de-epoxidase is a part of the xanthophyll (or violaxanthin) cycle for controlling the concentration of zeaxanthin in chloroplasts .
