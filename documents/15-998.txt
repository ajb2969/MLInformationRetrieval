Violaxanthin de-epoxidase

Violaxanthin de-epoxidase (, VDE) is an enzyme with system name violaxanthin:ascorbate oxidoreductase.1234567 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

violaxanthin + 2 L-ascorbate

zeaxanthin + 2 L-dehydroascorbate + 2 H2O (overall reaction)
 
(1a) violaxanthin + L-ascorbate 
 
 
 
 
  antheraxanthin + L-dehydroascorbate + H2O
 
(1b) antheraxanthin + L-ascorbate 
 
 
 
  zeaxanthin + L-dehydroascorbate + H2O
 

Violaxanthin de-epoxidase is a part of the xanthophyll (or violaxanthin) cycle for controlling the concentration of zeaxanthin in chloroplasts.
