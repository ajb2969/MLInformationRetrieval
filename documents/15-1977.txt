(6-4)DNA photolyase (, DNA photolyase, H64PRH, NF-10, phr (6-4), PL-(6-4), OtCPF1, (6-4) PHR, At64PHR) is an enzyme with system name (6-4) photoproduct pyrimidine-lyase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(6-4) photoproduct (in DNA) 
 
 
 
  2 pyrimidine residues (in DNA)
 

This enzyme is a flavoprotein.
