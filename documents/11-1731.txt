   Hanner's inequalities      Hanner's inequalities   In mathematics , Hanner's inequalities are results in the theory of L p spaces . Their proof was published in 1956 by Olof Hanner . They provide a simpler way of proving the uniform convexity of L p spaces for p ∈ (1, +∞) than the approach proposed by James A. Clarkson in 1936.
