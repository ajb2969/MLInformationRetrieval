Spherium

The "spherium" model consists of two electrons trapped on the surface of a sphere of radius R R R . It has been used by Berry and collaborators 1 to understand both weakly and strongly correlated systems and to suggest an "alternating" version of Hund's rule. Seidl studies this system in the context of density functional theory (DFT) to develop new correlation functionals within the adiabatic connection.2
