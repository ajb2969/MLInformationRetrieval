Copal-8-ol diphosphate hydratase

Copal-8-ol diphosphate hydratase (, CcCLS) is an enzyme with system name geranylgeranyl-diphosphate hydro-lyase ((13E)-8alpha-hydroxylabda-13-en-15-yl diphosphate forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(13E)-8alpha-hydroxylabda-13-en-15-yl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons geranylgeranyl diphosphate + H2O

This enzyme requires Mg2+.
