   Euclidean distance      Euclidean distance   In mathematics , the Euclidean distance or Euclidean metric is the "ordinary" (i.e. straight-line ) distance between two points in Euclidean space . With this distance, Euclidean space becomes a metric space . The associated norm is called the Euclidean norm . Older literature refers to the metric as Pythagorean metric . A generalized term for the Euclidean norm is the L 2 norm or L 2 distance.
