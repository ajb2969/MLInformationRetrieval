M7GpppX diphosphatase

M7GpppX diphosphatase (, DcpS, m7GpppX pyrophosphatase, m7GpppN m7GMP phosphohydrolase) is an enzyme with system name m7G5'ppp5'N m7GMP phosphohydrolase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) m7G5'ppp5'N(3'ppp5'N)n + H2O 
 
 
 
  7-methylguanosine 5'-phosphate + pp5'N(3'ppp5'N)n

(2) 7-methylguanosine 5'-diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
 
  7-methylguanosine 5'-phosphate + phosphate


Decapping is a process in the control of eukaryotic mRNA degradation.
