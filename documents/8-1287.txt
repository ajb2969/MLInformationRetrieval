Specification  Cumulative distribution function  The cumulative distribution function (cdf) of the half-logistic distribution is intimately related to the cdf of the logistic distribution. Formally, if F ( k ) is the cdf for the logistic distribution, then G ( k ) = 2 F ( k ) − 1 is the cdf of a half-logistic distribution.
