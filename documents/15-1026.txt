Gallate dioxygenase

Gallate dioxygenase (, GalA) is an enzyme with system name gallate:oxygen oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

gallate + O2

 
  (1E)-4-oxobut-1-ene-1,2,4-tricarboxylate
 

Gallate dioxygenase contains non-heme Fe2+.
