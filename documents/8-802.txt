   Chebyshev rational functions      Chebyshev rational functions    This article is not about the Chebyshev rational functions used in the design of elliptic filters . For those functions, see Elliptic rational functions .    In mathematics , the Chebyshev rational functions are a sequence of functions which are both rational and orthogonal . They are named after Pafnuty Chebyshev .
