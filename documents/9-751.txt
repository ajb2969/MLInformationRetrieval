Consequences  The name "forward measure" comes from the fact that under the forward measure, forward prices are martingales , a fact first observed by Geman (1989) (who is responsible for formally defining the measure). 3 Compare with futures prices, which are martingales under the risk neutral measure. Note that when interest rates are deterministic, this implies that forward prices and futures prices are the same.
