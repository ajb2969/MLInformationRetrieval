   List of equations in gravitation      List of equations in gravitation   This article summarizes equations in the theory of gravitation .  Definitions  Gravitational mass and inertia  A common misconception occurs between centre of mass and centre of gravity . They are defined in similar ways but are not exactly the same quantity. Centre of mass is the mathematical description of placing all the mass in the region considered to one position, centre of gravity is a real physical quantity, the point of a body where the gravitational force acts. They are equal if and only if the external gravitational field is uniform.        scope="col" Quantity (common name/s)   scope="col" (Common) symbol/s   scope="col" Defining equation   scope="col" SI units   scope="col" Dimension       Centre of gravity   r cog (symbols vary)   i th moment of mass     𝐦  i   =    𝐫  i    m  i         subscript  𝐦  i      subscript  𝐫  i    subscript  m  i      \mathbf{m}_{i}=\mathbf{r}_{i}m_{i}\,\!
