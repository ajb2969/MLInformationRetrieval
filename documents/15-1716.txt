Isotuberculosinol synthase

Isotuberculosinol synthase (, Rv3378c) is an enzyme with system name tuberculosinyl diphosphate diphosphohydrolase (isotuberculosinol forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

tuberculosinyl diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
 
  (13S)-isotuberculosinol + diphosphate


This enzyme is present in species of Mycobacterium that cause tuberculosis.
