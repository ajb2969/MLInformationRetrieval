   Bogoliubov causality condition      Bogoliubov causality condition   Bogoliubov causality condition is a causality condition for scattering matrix ( S -matrix) in axiomatic quantum field theory . The condition was introduced in axiomatic quantum field theory by Nikolay Bogolyubov in 1955.
