2,5-diketocamphane 1,2-monooxygenase

2,5-diketocamphane 1,2-monooxygenase (, 2,5-diketocamphane lactonizing enzyme, ketolactonase I, 2,5-diketocamphane 1,2-monooxygenase oxygenating component, 2,5-DKCMO, camP (gene), camphor 1,2-monooxygenase, camphor ketolactonase I) is an enzyme with system name (+)-bornane-2,5-dione,NADH:oxygen oxidoreductase (1,2-lactonizing).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(+)-bornane-2,5-dione + O2 + NADH + H+

 
  (+)-5-oxo-1,2-campholide + NAD+ + H2O
 

2,5-diketocamphane 1,2-monooxygenase is a flavoprotein (FMN) which requires Fe2+.
