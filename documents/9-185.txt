   Leray–Hirsch theorem      Leray–Hirsch theorem   In mathematics , the Leray–Hirsch theorem 1 is a basic result on the algebraic topology of fiber bundles . It is named after Jean Leray and Guy Hirsch , who independently proved it in the late 1940s. It can be thought of as a mild generalization of the Künneth formula , which computes the cohomology of a product space as a tensor product of the cohomologies of the direct factors.
