Glycine N-phenylacetyltransferase

Glycine N-phenylacetyltransferase (, arylacetyl-CoA N-acyltransferase, arylacetyltransferase, GAT (gene)) is an enzyme with system name phenylacetyl-CoA:glycine N-phenylacetyltransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

phenylacetyl-CoA + glycine

 
  CoA + phenylacetylglycine


This enzyme was purified from bovine liver mitochondria.
