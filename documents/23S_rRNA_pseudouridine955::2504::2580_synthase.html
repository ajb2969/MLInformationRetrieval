23S rRNA pseudouridine955/2504/2580 synthase

23S rRNA pseudouridine955/2504/2580 synthase (, RluC, pseudouridine synthase RluC) is an enzyme with system name 23S rRNA-uridine955/2504/2580 uracil mutase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

23S rRNA uridine955/uridine2504/uridine2580 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 23S rRNA pseudouridine955/pseudouridine2504/pseudouridine2580

The enzyme converts uridines at position 955, 2504 and 2580 of 23S rRNA to pseudouridines.
