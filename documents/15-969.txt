Zeeman conjecture

In mathematics, the Zeeman conjecture or Zeeman's collapsibility conjecture asks whether given a finite contractible 2-complex 
 
 
 
  the space 
 
 
 
  is collapsible.
The conjecture implies the Poincaré conjecture and the Andrews–Curtis conjecture.
