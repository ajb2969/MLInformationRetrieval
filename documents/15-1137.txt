Erythromycin 12 hydroxylase

Erythromycin 12 hydroxylase (, EryK) is an enzyme with system name ''erythromycin-D,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (12-hydroxylating) ''.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

erythromycin D + NADPH + H+ + O2

 
  erythromycin C + NADP+ + H2O
 

Erythromycin 12 hydroxylase is responsible for the C-12 hydroxylation of the macrolactone ring.
