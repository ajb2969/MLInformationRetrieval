   Heat kernel      Heat kernel   In the mathematical study of heat conduction and diffusion , a heat kernel is the fundamental solution to the heat equation on a specified domain with appropriate boundary conditions . It is also one of the main tools in the study of the spectrum of the Laplace operator , and is thus of some auxiliary importance throughout mathematical physics . The heat kernel represents the evolution of temperature in a region whose boundary is held fixed at a particular temperature (typically zero), such that an initial unit of heat energy is placed at a point at time t =Â 0.
