   Shintani zeta function      Shintani zeta function   In mathematics , a Shintani zeta function or Shintani L-function is a generalization of the Riemann zeta function . They were first studied by . They include Hurwitz zeta functions , Barnes zeta functions , and Witten zeta functions as special cases.
