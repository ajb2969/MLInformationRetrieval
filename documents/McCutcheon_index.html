McCutcheon index

The McCutcheon index or chemotactic ratio is the ratio of the net displacement of a bacterium to the length of its actual path.12

McCutcheon index = Displacement Distance . McCutcheon index Displacement Distance \text{McCutcheon index}={\text{Displacement}\over\text{Distance}}.

It is named after Morton McCutcheon who introduced it to describe chemotaxis in leukocytes.
