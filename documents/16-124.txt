(+)-sativene synthase

(+)-sativene synthase (, cop4) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, (+)-sativene-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  (+)-sativene + diphosphate


This enzyme is isolated from the fungus Coprinus cinereus.
