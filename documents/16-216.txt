Taraxerol synthase

Taraxerol synthase (, RsM2, (S)-2,3-epoxysqualene mutase (cyclizing, taraxerol-forming)) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, taraxerol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene 
 
 
taraxerol


The enzyme gives taraxerol, beta-amyrin and lupeol.
