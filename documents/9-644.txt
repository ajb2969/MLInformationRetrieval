   Yenice, Çanakkale      Yenice, Çanakkale   Yenice is a town and district of Çanakkale Province in the Marmara region of Turkey . According to the 2000 census, population of the district is 35,796 of which 6,903 live in the town of Yenice. 1 The district covers an area of , 2 and the town lies at an elevation of .
