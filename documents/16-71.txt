(+)-germacrene D synthase

(+)-germacrene D synthase () is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((+)-germacrene-D-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  (+)-germacrene D + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+, Mn2+, Ni2+ or Co2+.
