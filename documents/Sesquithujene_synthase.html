Sesquithujene synthase

Sesquithujene synthase (, TPS5-Del1) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (sesquithujene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons sesquithujene + diphosphate

The enzyme from Zea mays, variety Delprim, gives mainly sesquithujene.
