   Metric derivative      Metric derivative   In mathematics , the metric derivative is a notion of derivative appropriate to parametrized  paths in metric spaces . It generalizes the notion of "speed" or "absolute velocity" to spaces which have a notion of distance (i.e. metric spaces) but not direction (such as vector spaces ).
