   Methylenediurea deaminase      Methylenediurea deaminase   Methylenediurea deaminase (, methylenediurease ) is an enzyme with system name methylenediurea aminohydrolase . 1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   methylenediurea + 2 H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   N-(hydroxymethyl)urea + 2 NH 3 + CO 2 (overall reaction)  (1a) methylenediurea + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   N-(carboxyaminomethyl)urea + NH 3   (1b) N-(carboxyaminomethyl)urea   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   N-(aminomethyl)urea + CO 2 (spontaneous)  (1c) N-(aminomethyl)urea + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   N-(hydroxymethyl)urea + NH 3 (spontaneous)   Methylenediurea is hydrolysed and decarboxylated to give an aminated methylurea.
