   Xenon oxytetrafluoride      Xenon oxytetrafluoride   Xenon oxytetrafluoride ( Xe O F 4 ) is an inorganic  chemical compound . As are most xenon oxides, it is extremely reactive and unstable, and hydrolyses in water to give dangerously hazardous and corrosive products, including hydrogen fluoride :        2  X  e  O   F  4    +   4   H  2   O    ⟶    2  X  e   +   8  H  F   +   3   O  2        normal-⟶      2  normal-X  normal-e  normal-O   subscript  normal-F  4      4   subscript  normal-H  2   normal-O        2  normal-X  normal-e     8  normal-H  normal-F     3   subscript  normal-O  2       \rm 2XeOF_{4}+4H_{2}O\longrightarrow 2Xe+8HF+3O_{2}     In addition, some ozone and fluorine are also formed. The reaction is extremely dangerous, and xenon oxytetrafluoride should therefore be kept away from any trace of water or water vapour under all conditions.
