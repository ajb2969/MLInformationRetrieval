5-epi-alpha-selinene synthase

5-epi-alpha-selinene synthase (, 8a-epi-alpha-selinene synthase, NP1) is an enzyme with system name (2Z,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, 5-epi-alpha-selinene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  5-epi-alpha-selinene + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+.
