M7GpppN-mRNA hydrolase

M7GpppN-mRNA hydrolase (, DCP2, NUDT16, D10 protein, D9 protein, D10 decapping enzyme, decapping enzyme) is an enzyme with system name m7GpppN-mRNA m7GDP phosphohydrolase.1234567 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

m7G5'ppp5'-mRNA + H2O 
 
 
 
  m7GDP + 5'-phospho-mRNA
 

Decapping of mRNA is essential in eukaryotic mRNA turnover.
