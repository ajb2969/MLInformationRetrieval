   Woo–Lam      Woo–Lam  In [ [cryptography]], Woo–Lam refers to various computer network authentication protocols designed by Simon S. Lam and Thomas Woo. 1 2 The protocols enable two communicating parties to authenticate each other's identity and to exchange session keys, and involve the use of a trusted key distribution center (KDC) to negotiate between the parties. Both symmetric-key and public-key variants have been described.
