   Laguerre–Pólya class      Laguerre–Pólya class   The Laguerre–Pólya class is the class of entire functions consisting of those functions which are locally the limit of a series of polynomials whose roots are all real. 1 Any function of Laguerre–Pólya class is also of Pólya class .  The product of two functions in the class is also in the class, so the class constitutes a monoid under the operation of function multiplication.
