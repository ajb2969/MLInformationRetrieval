   Isoleucine N-monooxygenase      Isoleucine N-monooxygenase   Isoleucine N-monooxygenase (, CYP79D3 , CYP79D4 ) is an enzyme with system name L-isoleucine,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (N-hydroxylating) . 1 2 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   L-isoleucine + 2 O 2 + 2 NADPH + 2 H +    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (E)-2-methylbutanal oxime + 2 NADP + + CO 2 + 3 H 2 O (overall reaction)  (1a) L-isoleucine + O 2 + NADPH + H +    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   N-hydroxy-L-isoleucine + NADP + + H 2 O  (1b) N-hydroxy-L-isoleucine + O 2 + NADPH + H+   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   N,N-dihydroxy-L-isoleucine + NADP + + H 2 O  (1c) N,N-dihydroxy-L-isoleucine   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (E)-2-methylbutanal oxime + CO 2 + H 2 O (spontaneous reaction)   Isoleucine N-monooxygenase is a heme - thiolate protein (P-450).
