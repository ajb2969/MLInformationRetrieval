   Bilunabirotunda      Bilunabirotunda   In geometry , the bilunabirotunda is one of the Johnson solids ( J 91 ). It is one of the elementary Johnson solids that do not arise from "cut and paste" manipulations of the Platonic and Archimedean solids.
