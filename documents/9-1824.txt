Vol. 11 , p. L23 (1978)   Real form  By a simple linear combination of solid harmonics of ± m these functions are transformed into real functions. The real regular solid harmonics, expressed in cartesian coordinates, are homogeneous polynomials of order l in x , y , z . The explicit form of these polynomials is of some importance. They appear, for example, in the form of spherical atomic orbitals and real multipole moments . The explicit cartesian expression of the real regular harmonics will now be derived.
