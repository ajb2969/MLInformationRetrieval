   Pre-Lie algebra      Pre-Lie algebra   In mathematics , a pre-Lie algebra is an algebraic structure on a vector space , that describes some properties of objects such as rooted trees and vector fields on affine space .  The notion of pre-Lie algebra has been introduced by Murray Gerstenhaber in his work on deformations of algebras.  Pre-Lie algebras have been considered under some other names, among which one can cite left-symmetric algebras, right-symmetric algebras or Vinberg algebras.
