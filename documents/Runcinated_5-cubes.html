|- !Orthogonal projections in B5 Coxeter plane |} In five-dimensional geometry, a runcinated 5-cube is a convex uniform 5-polytope that is a runcination (a 3rd order truncation) of the regular 5-cube.

There are unique 8 degrees of runcinations of the 5-cube, along with permutations of truncations, and cantellations. Four are more simply constructed relative to the 5-orthoplex.
