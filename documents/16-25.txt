Levopimaradiene synthase

Levopimaradiene synthase (, PtTPS-LAS, LPS, copalyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase [abieta-8(14),12-diene-forming]) is an enzyme with system name (+)-copalyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (abieta-8(14),12-diene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(+)-copalyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  abieta-8(14),12-diene + diphosphate


In Ginkgo, the enzyme catalyses the initial cyclization step in the biosynthesis of ginkgolides.
