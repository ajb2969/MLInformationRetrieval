3-(cis-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dien-1-yl)propanoate dehydrogenase

3-(cis-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dien-1-yl)propanoate dehydrogenase (, hcaB (gene), cis-dihydrodiol dehydrogenase, 2,3-dihydroxy-2,3-dihydro-phenylpropionate dehydrogenase) is an enzyme with system name 3-(cis-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dien-1-yl)propanoate:NAD+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) 3-(cis-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dien-1-yl)propanoate + NAD+

 
  3-(2,3-dihydroxyphenyl)propanoate + NADH + H+

(2) (2E)-3-(cis-5,6-dihydroxycyclohexa-1,3-dien-1-yl)prop-2-enoate + NAD+

 
  (2E)-3-(2,3-dihydroxyphenyl)prop-2-enoate + NADH + H+


This enzyme catalyses a step in indegradation of phenylpropanoid compounds.
