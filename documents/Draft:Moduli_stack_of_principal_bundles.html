Draft:Moduli stack of principal bundles

In algebraic geometry, given a smooth projective curve over a finite field $\mathbf{F}_q$ and a smooth affine group scheme G over it, the moduli stack of principal bundles over X, denoted by $\operatorname{Bun}_G$, is an algebraic stack over the category of G-bundles on X that is given by:1 for any $\mathbf{F}_q$-algebra R,

$$R \mapsto$$ the category of (principal) G-bundles over the relative curve $X \times_{\mathbf{F}_q} \operatorname{Spec}R$. In particular, the category of $\mathbf{F}_q$-points of $\operatorname{Bun}_G$, that is, $\operatorname{Bun}_G(\mathbf{F}_q)$, is the category of G-bundles over X. Similarly, the moduli stack of bundles is defined when the curve X is over the field of complex numbers.

It is known that $\operatorname{Bun}_G$ is a smooth stack.

Behrend's formula

This is a version of Lefschetz trace formula when X is over a finite field.
