3-Succinoylsemialdehyde-pyridine dehydrogenase

3-Succinoylsemialdehyde-pyridine dehydrogenase () is an enzyme with system name 4-oxo-4-(pyridin-3-yl)butanal:NADP+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

4-oxo-4-(pyridin-3-yl)butanal + NADP+ + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 4-oxo-4-(pyridin-3-yl)butanoate + NADPH + H+

The enzyme has been characterized from the soil bacterium Pseudomonas sp. HZN6.
