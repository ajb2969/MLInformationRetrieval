   Monocyclic monoterpene ketone monooxygenase      Monocyclic monoterpene ketone monooxygenase   Monocyclic monoterpene ketone monooxygenase (, 1-hydroxy-2-oxolimonene 1,2-monooxygenase , dihydrocarvone 1,2-monooxygenase , MMKMO ) is an enzyme with system name (-)-menthone,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase . 1 2 3 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) (-)- menthone + NADPH + H + + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (4R,7S)-7-isopropyl-4-methyloxepan-2-one + NADP + + H 2 O  (2) dihydrocarvone + NADPH + H + + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   4-isopropenyl-7-methyloxepan-2-one + NADP + + H 2 O  (3) (iso)-dihydrocarvone + NADPH + H + + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   6-isopropenyl-3-methyloxepan-2-one + NADP + + H 2 O  (4a) 1-hydroxymenth-8-en-2-one + NADPH + H + + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   7-hydroxy-4-isopropenyl-7-methyloxepan-2-one + NADP + + H 2 O  (4b) 7-hydroxy-4-isopropenyl-7-methyloxepan-2-one   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   3-isopropenyl-6-oxoheptanoate (spontaneous)   Monocyclic monoterpene ketone monooxygenase is a flavoprotein (FAD).
