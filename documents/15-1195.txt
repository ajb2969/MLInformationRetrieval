Caffeine dehydrogenase

Caffeine dehydrogenase () is an enzyme with system name caffeine:ubiquinone oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

caffeine + ubiquinone (Q0 ox) + H2O

Caffeine dehydrogenase is isolated from the soil bacterium Pseudomonas sp. CBB1.
