Choice of norm  For Euclidean spaces , using the L 2 norm gives rise to the Euclidean metric in the product space; however, any other choice of p will lead to a topologically equivalent metric space. In the category of metric spaces (with Lipschitz maps having Lipschitz constant 1), the sup norm is used.  References    .   "  Category:Metric geometry
