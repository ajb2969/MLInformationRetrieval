23S rRNA pseudouridine2457 synthase

23S rRNA pseudouridine2457 synthase (, RluE, YmfC) is an enzyme with system name 23S rRNA-uridine2457 uracil mutase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

23S rRNA uridine2457

 
  23S rRNA pseudouridine2457


The enzyme modifies uridine2457 in a stem of 23S RNA in Escherichia coli.
