Cohen ring

In algebra, a Cohen ring is a field or a complete discrete valuation ring of mixed characteristic ( 0 , p ) 0 p (0,p) whose maximal ideal is generated by p. Cohen rings are used in the Cohen structure theorem for complete Noetherian local rings.
