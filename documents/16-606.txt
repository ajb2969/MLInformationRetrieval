   Weyl−Lewis−Papapetrou coordinates      Weyl−Lewis−Papapetrou coordinates   In general relativity , the Weyl−Lewis−Papapetrou coordinates are a set of coordinates , used in the solutions to the vacuum region surrounding an axisymmetric distribution of mass–energy . They are named for Hermann Weyl , T. Lewis , and Achilles Papapetrou .
