   Coherent states in mathematical physics      Coherent states in mathematical physics   Coherent states have been introduced in a physical context, first as quasi-classical states in quantum mechanics , then as the backbone of quantum optics and they are described in that spirit in the article Coherent states (see also 1 ). However, they have generated a huge variety of generalizations, which have led to a tremendous literature in mathematical physics . In this article, we sketch the main directions of research on this line.
