   Papyrus 19      Papyrus 19   Papyrus 19 (in the Gregory-Aland numbering ), signed by   𝔓   𝔓   \mathfrak{P}    19 , is an early copy of the New Testament in Greek . The manuscript paleographically has been assigned to the 4th or 5th century. 1  The papyrus is currently housed at the Bodleian Library , Gr. bibl. d. 6 (P) at the University of Oxford . 2 3  Description  Papyrus 19 is a papyrus  manuscript of the Gospel of Matthew , containing text for Matt 10:32-11:5. The leaf is complete at the top and bottom, but broken at the sides. 4  The Greek text of this codex is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type .
