Parkeol synthase

Parkeol synthase () is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, parkeol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons parkeol

The enzyme from rice (Oryza sativa) produces only parkeol.
