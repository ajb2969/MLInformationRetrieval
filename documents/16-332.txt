Enflo proved 6 that the class of Banach spaces that admit an equivalent uniformly convex norm coincides with the class of super-reflexive Banach spaces, introduced by Robert C. James. 7 As a space is super-reflexive if and only if its dual is super-reflexive, it follows that the class of Banach spaces that admit an equivalent uniformly convex norm coincides with the class of spaces that admit an equivalent uniformly smooth norm. If a normed space admits two equivalent norms, one uniformly convex and one uniformly smooth, the Asplund averaging technique 9 produces another equivalent norm that is both uniformly convex and uniformly smooth.
