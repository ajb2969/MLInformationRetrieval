   Hasse invariant of an algebra      Hasse invariant of an algebra   In mathematics , the Hasse invariant of an algebra is an invariant attached to a Brauer class of algebras over a field . The concept is named after Helmut Hasse . The invariant plays a role in local class field theory .  Local fields  Let K be a local field with valuation v and D a K -algebra. We may assume D is a division algebra with centre K of degree n .
