   Tensor derivative (continuum mechanics)      Tensor derivative (continuum mechanics)   The derivatives of scalars , vectors , and second-order tensors with respect to second-order tensors are of considerable use in continuum mechanics . These derivatives are used in the theories of nonlinear elasticity and plasticity , particularly in the design of algorithms for numerical simulations . 1  The directional derivative provides a systematic way of finding these derivatives. 2  Derivatives with respect to vectors and second-order tensors  The definitions of directional derivatives for various situations are given below. It is assumed that the functions are sufficiently smooth that derivatives can be taken.  Derivatives of scalar valued functions of vectors  Let f ( v ) be a real valued function of the vector v .
