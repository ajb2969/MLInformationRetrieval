   Prony's method      Prony's method   Prony analysis ( Prony's method ) was developed by Gaspard Riche de Prony in 1795. However, practical use of the method awaited the digital computer. 1 Similar to the Fourier transform , Prony's method extracts valuable information from a uniformly sampled signal and builds a series of damped complex exponentials or sinusoids. This allows for the estimation of frequency, amplitude, phase and damping components of a signal.
