Avermitilol synthase

Avermitilol synthase () is an enzyme with system name avermitilol hydrolase (cyclizing, avermitilol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
avermitilol + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+.
