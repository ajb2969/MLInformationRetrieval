Ascorbate ferrireductase (transmembrane)

Ascorbate ferrireductase (transmembrane) (, cytochrome b561) is an enzyme with system name Fe(III):ascorbate oxidorectuctase (electron-translocating).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction
(Figure)
Ascorbate ferrireductase (transmembrane).svg



ascorbate[in] + Fe(III)[out]

 
  monodehydroascorbate radical[in] + Fe(II)[out] + H+[in]
Ascorbate ferrireductase is a diheme cytochrome that acts on hexacyanoferrate(III) and other ferric chelates.
