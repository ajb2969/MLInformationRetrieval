GalNAc-alpha-(1-4)-GalNAc-alpha-(1-3)-diNAcBac-PP-undecaprenol alpha-1,4-N-acetyl-D-galactosaminyltransferase

GalNAc-alpha-(1->4)-GalNAc-alpha-(1->3)-diNAcBac-PP-undecaprenol alpha-1,4-N-acetyl-D-galactosaminyltransferase (, PglH) is an enzyme with system name UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine:GalNAc-alpha-(1->4)-GalNAc-alpha-(1->3)-diNAcBac-PP-tritrans,heptacis-undecaprenol 4-alpha-N-acetyl-D-galactosaminyltransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

3 UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine + GalNAc-alpha-(1->4)-GalNAc-alpha-(1->3)-diNAcBac-PP-tritrans,heptacis-undecaprenol 
 
 
 
  3 UDP + [GalNAc-alpha-(1->4)]4-GalNAc-alpha-(1->3)-diNAcBac-PP-tritrans,heptacis-undecaprenol
 

This enzyme is solated from Campylobacter jejuni.
