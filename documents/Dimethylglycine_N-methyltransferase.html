Dimethylglycine N-methyltransferase

Dimethylglycine N-methyltransferase (, BsmB, DMT) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:N,N-dimethylglycine N-methyltransferase (betaine-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-adenosyl-L-methionine + N,N-dimethylglycine ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + betaine

This enzyme is purified from the marine cyanobacterium Synechococcus sp. WH8102.
