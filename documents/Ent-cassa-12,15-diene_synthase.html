Ent-cassa-12,15-diene synthase

Ent-cassa-12,15-diene synthase (, OsDTC1, OsKS7) is an enzyme with system name ent-copalyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (ent-cassa-12,15-diene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ent-copalyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ent-cassa-12,15-diene + diphosphate

This class I diterpene cyclase produces ent-cassa-12,15-diene, a precursor of the rice phytoalexins (-)-phytocassanes A-E.
