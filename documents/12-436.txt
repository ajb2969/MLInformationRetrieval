   Sy Friedman      Sy Friedman   Sy-David Friedman (born on May 23, 1953 in Chicago ) is an American and Austrian  mathematician and a professor of mathematics at the University of Vienna and the director of the Kurt Gödel Research Center for Mathematical Logic. His main research interest lies in mathematical logic , in particular in set theory and recursion theory .  Friedman is the brother of mathematician Harvey Friedman .  Biography  He studied at Northwestern University and, from 1970, at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology . He received his Ph.D. in 1976 from MIT (his thesis Recursion on Inadmissible Ordinals was written under the supervision of Gerald E. Sacks ).  In 1979 Sy Friedman accepted a position at MIT, and in 1990 he became a full professor there. Since 1999 he has been a professor of mathematical logic at the University of Vienna.
