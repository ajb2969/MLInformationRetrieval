Strictosidine beta-glucosidase

3alpha(S)-strictosidine beta-glucosidase () is an enzyme with system name strictosidine beta-D-glucohydrolase.1 2 3 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

strictosidine + H2O 
 
 
 
  D-glucose + strictosidine aglycone
 

Strictosidine is a precursor of indole alkaloids.
