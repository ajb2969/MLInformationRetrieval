Ellipse Law

The '''Law of the Ellipse''', or '''[[Aurel Stodola|Stodola]]'s cone law''',Timot Veer, Andreas Ulvestad, Olav Bolland, [http://folk.ntnu.no/obo

lland/pdf/GT-04_53770.pdf FRAME, a Tool for Predicting Gas Turbine Condition as well as Reability, Availability Performance], ASME/IGTI TURBOEXPO Conference 2004 June 14–17, Vienna, Austria, GT-2004-537701 provides a method for calculating the highly nonlinear dependence of extraction pressures with a flow for multistage turbine with high backpressure, when the turbine nozzles are not choked.2 It is important in turbine off-design calculations.

For a given outlet pressure p 21 subscript p 21 \scriptstyle p_{21}\, , flow rates changes depending on the inlet pressure p 01 subscript p 01 \scriptstyle p_{01}\, as an arc of hyperbola in a plane parallel to m ˙ 01 0 p 01 subscript normal-˙ m 01 0 subscript p 01 \scriptstyle\dot{m}_{01}\,0\,p_{01}\, .
