Gamma-curcumene synthase

Gamma-curcumene synthase (, PatTpsA (gene)) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, gamma-curcumene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  gamma-curcumene + diphosphate


This enzyme is one of five sesquiterpenoid synthases in Pogostemon cablin (patchouli).
