2-haloacid dehalogenase (configuration-inverting)

2-haloacid dehalogenase (configuration-inverting) (, 2-haloalkanoic acid dehalogenase, 2-haloalkanoid acid halidohydrolase, DL-2-haloacid dehalogenase, DL-2-haloacid dehalogenase (inversion of configuration), DL-2-haloacid halidohydrolase (inversion of configuration), DL-DEXi, (R,S)-2-haloacid dehalogenase (configuration-inverting)) is an enzyme with system name (S)-2-haloacid dehalogenase (configuration-inverting).123456789 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) (S)-2-haloacid + H2O 
 
 
 
  (R)-2-hydroxyacid + halide

(2) (R)-2-haloacid + H2O 
 
 
 
  (S)-2-hydroxyacid + halide
 

This enzyme dehalogenates both (S)- and (R)-2-haloalkanoic acids.
