   Stein–Strömberg theorem      Stein–Strömberg theorem   In mathematics , the Stein–Strömberg theorem or Stein–Strömberg inequality is a result in measure theory concerning the Hardy–Littlewood maximal operator . The result is foundational in the study of the problem of differentiation of integrals . The result is named after the mathematicians  Elias M. Stein and Jan-Olov Strömberg .
