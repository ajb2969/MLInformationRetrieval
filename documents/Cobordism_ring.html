Cobordism ring

In mathematics, the oriented cobordism ring is a ring where elements are oriented cobordism classes1 of manifolds, the multiplication is given by the Cartesian product of manifolds and the addition is given as the disjoint union of manifolds. The ring is graded by dimensions of manifolds and is denoted by

One can also define an unoriented cobordism ring, denoted by Ω * O subscript superscript normal-Ω O \Omega^{O}_{*} . If O is replaced U, then one gets the complex cobordism ring, oriented or unoriented.

In general, one writes Ω * B subscript superscript normal-Ω B \Omega^{B}_{*} for the cobordism ring of manifolds with structure B.
