Valerena-4,7(11)-diene synthase

Valerena-4,7(11)-diene synthase (, VoTPS2) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, valerena-4,7(11)-diene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  valerena-4,7(11)-diene + diphosphate


This enzyme is isolated from the plant Valeriana officinalis (valerian).
