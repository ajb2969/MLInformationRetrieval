Cobalt-precorrin 5A hydrolase

Cobalt-precorrin 5A hydrolase (, CbiG (gene)) is an enzyme with system name cobalt-precorrin 5A acylhydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

cobalt-precorrin-5A + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons cobalt-precorrin-5B + acetaldehyde + 2 H+

This enzyme hydrolyses the ring A acetate delta-lactone of cobalt-precorrin-5A.
