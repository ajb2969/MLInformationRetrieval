Brahmagupta matrix

In mathematics, the following matrix was given by Indian mathematician Brahmagupta:1

B ( x , y ) = [ x y ± t y ± x ] . B x y x y plus-or-minus t y plus-or-minus x B(x,y)=\begin{bmatrix}x&y\\ \pm ty&\pm x\end{bmatrix}.

It satisfies

B ( x 1 , y 1 ) B ( x 2 , y 2 ) = B ( x 1 x 2 ± t y 1 y 2 , x 1 y 2 ± y 1 x 2 ) .
