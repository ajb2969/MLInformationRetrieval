(-)-camphene synthase

(-)-camphene synthase (, CS) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, (-)-camphene-forming).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate

 
  (-)-camphene + diphosphate


(-)-Camphene is the major product in Abies grandis (grand fir) with traces of other monoterpenoids.
