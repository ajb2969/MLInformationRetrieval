   Oscillatory integral      Oscillatory integral   In mathematical analysis an oscillatory integral is a type of distribution . Oscillatory integrals make rigorous many arguments that, on a naive level, appear to use divergent integrals. It is possible to represent approximate solution operators for many differential equations as oscillatory integrals.
