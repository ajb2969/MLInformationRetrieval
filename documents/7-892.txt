   Sudan function      Sudan function   In the theory of computation , the Sudan function is an example of a function that is recursive , but not primitive recursive . This is also true of the better-known Ackermann function . The Sudan function was the first function having this property to be published.  It was discovered (and published 1 ) in 1927 by Gabriel Sudan , a Romanian  mathematician who was a student of David Hilbert .
