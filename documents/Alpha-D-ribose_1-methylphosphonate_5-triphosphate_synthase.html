Alpha-D-ribose 1-methylphosphonate 5-triphosphate synthase

Alpha-D-ribose 1-methylphosphonate 5-triphosphate synthase () is an enzyme with system name ATP:methylphosphonate 5-triphosphoribosyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction
