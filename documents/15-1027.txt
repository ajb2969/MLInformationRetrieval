Linoleate 9S-lipoxygenase

Linoleate 9S-lipoxygenase (, 9-lipoxygenase, 9S-lipoxygenase, linoleate 9-lipoxygenase, LOX1 (gene), 9S-LOX) is an enzyme with system name linoleate:oxygen 9S-oxidoreductase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

linoleate + O2

 
  (9S,10E,12Z)-9-hydroperoxy-10,12-octadecadienoate
 

Linoleate 9S-lipoxygenase contains nonheme iron.
