   Two-vector      Two-vector   A two-vector is a tensor of type (2,0) and it is the dual of a two-form , meaning that it is a linear functional which maps two-forms to the real numbers (or more generally, to scalars).  The tensor product of a pair of vectors is a two-vector. Then, any two-form can be expressed as a linear combination of tensor products of pairs of vectors, especially a linear combination of tensor products of pairs of basis vectors.
