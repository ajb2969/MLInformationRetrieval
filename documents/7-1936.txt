   Molar solubility      Molar solubility   Molar solubility is the number of moles of a substance (the solute) that can be dissolved per liter of solution before the solution becomes saturated. It can be calculated from a substance's solubility product constant (K sp ) and stoichiometry . The units are mol/L, sometimes written as M .
