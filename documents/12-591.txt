   Coulomb wave function      Coulomb wave function   In mathematics , a Coulomb wave function is a solution of the Coulomb wave equation , named after Charles-Augustin de Coulomb . They are used to describe the behavior of charged particles in a Coulomb potential and can be written in terms of confluent hypergeometric functions or Whittaker functions of imaginary argument.
