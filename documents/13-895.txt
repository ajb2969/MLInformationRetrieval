Examples  Examples in two variables include Jacobi theta functions , the Weierstrass ℘ function , and Fourier–Jacobi coefficients of Siegel modular forms of genus 2. Examples with more than two variables include characters of some irreducible highest-weight representations of affine Kac–Moody algebras .
