Alpha-muurolene synthase

Alpha-muurolene synthase (, Cop3) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, alpha-muurolene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  alpha-muurolene + diphosphate


The enzyme has been characterized from the fungus Coprinus cinereus.
