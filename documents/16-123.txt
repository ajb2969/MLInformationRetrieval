Beta-cubebene synthase

Beta-cubebene synthase (, cop4, Mg25) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, beta-cubebene-forming).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
beta-cubebene + diphosphate


Isolated from the fungus Coprinus cinereus.
