TRNA pseudouridine13 synthase

TRNA pseudouridine13 synthase (, TruD, YgbO, tRNA PSI13 synthase, RNA:PSI-synthase Pus7p, Pus7p, RNA:pseudouridine-synthase Pus7p, Pus7 protein) is an enzyme with system name tRNA-uridine13 uracil mutase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

tRNA uridine13

 
  tRNA pseudouridine13


Pseudouridine synthase TruD from Escherichia coli specifically acts on uridine13 in tRNA.
