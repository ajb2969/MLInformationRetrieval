All-trans-phytoene synthase

All-trans-phytoene synthase (, prephytoene-diphosphate synthase, phytoene synthetase, PSase, geranylgeranyl-diphosphate geranylgeranyltransferase, 15-trans-phytoene synthase) is an enzyme with system name geranylgeranyl-diphosphate:geranylgeranyl-diphosphate geranylgeranyltransferase (all-trans-phytoene forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 geranylgeranyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons all-trans-phytoene + 2 diphosphate (overall reaction)
(1a) 2 geranylgeranyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons diphosphate + prephytoene diphosphate
(1b) prephytoene diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons all-trans-phytoene + diphosphate

This enzyme requires Mn2+ for activity.
