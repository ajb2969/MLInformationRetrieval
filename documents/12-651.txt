Light cone coordinates can also be generalized to curved spacetime in general relativity. Sometimes, calculations simplify using light cone coordinates. See Newman–Penrose formalism .  Light cone coordinates are sometimes used to describe relativistic collisions, especially if the relative velocity is very close to the speed of light.
