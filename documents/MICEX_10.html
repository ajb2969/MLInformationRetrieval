MICEX 10

The MICEX 10 Index () is an unweighted price index that tracks the ten most liquid Russian stocks listed on MICEX-RTS in Moscow, Russia. Composition of the index is assessed quarterly following the liquidity criterion.1 Components are assigned equal weight.

Calculation
