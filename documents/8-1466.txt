   De Branges space      De Branges space   In mathematics , a de Branges space (sometimes written De Branges space ) is a concept in functional analysis and is constructed from a de Branges function .  The concept is named after Louis de Branges who proved numerous results regarding these spaces, especially as Hilbert spaces , and used those results to prove the Bieberbach conjecture .
