   Sommerfeld expansion      Sommerfeld expansion   A Sommerfeld expansion is an approximation method developed by Arnold Sommerfeld for a certain class of integrals which are common in condensed matter and statistical physics . Physically, the integrals represent statistical averages using the Fermi–Dirac distribution .
