Sulfide-cytochrome-c reductase (flavocytochrome c)

Sulfide-cytochrome-c reductase (flavocytochrome c) () is an enzyme with system name hydrogen-sulfide:flavocytochrome c oxidoreductase.123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

hydrogen sulfide + 2 ferricytochrome c 
 
 
sulfur + 2 ferrocytochrome c + 2 H+


The enzyme from Allochromatium vinosum contains covalently bound FAD and c-type hemes.
