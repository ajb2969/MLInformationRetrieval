   Glaisher–Kinkelin constant      Glaisher–Kinkelin constant   In mathematics , the Glaisher–Kinkelin constant or Glaisher's constant , typically denoted A , is a mathematical constant , related to the K-function and the Barnes G-function . The constant appears in a number of sums and integrals , especially those involving Gamma functions and zeta functions . It is named after mathematicians  James Whitbread Lee Glaisher and Hermann Kinkelin .
