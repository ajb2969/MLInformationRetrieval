Arruda‚ÄìBoyce model

In continuum mechanics, an Arruda‚ÄìBoyce model1 is a hyperelastic constitutive model used to describe the mechanical behavior of rubber and other polymeric substances. This model is based on the statistical mechanics of a material with a cubic representative volume element containing eight chains along the diagonal directions. The material is assumed to be incompressible.
