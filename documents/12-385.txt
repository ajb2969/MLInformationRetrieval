   Kugel–Khomskii coupling      Kugel–Khomskii coupling   Kugel–Khomskii coupling describes a coupling between the spin and orbital  degrees of freedom in a solid; it is named after the Russian physicists Kliment I. Kugel (Климент Ильич Кугель) and Daniel I. Khomskii (Daniil I. Khomskii, Даниил Ильич Хомский).
