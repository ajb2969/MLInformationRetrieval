More generally, if A is a matrix polynomial of degree d , then one can convert the nonlinear eigenproblem into a linear (generalized) eigenproblem of d times the size.  Besides converting them to ordinary eigenproblems, which only works if A is polynomial, there are other methods of solving nonlinear eigenproblems based on the Jacobi-Davidson algorithm or based on Newton's method (related to inverse iteration ).
