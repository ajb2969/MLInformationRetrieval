   Cis-muuroladiene synthase      Cis-muuroladiene synthase   Cis-muuroladiene synthase (, MxpSS1 ) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cis-muuroladiene-forming) . 1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) (2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   cis-muurola-3,5-diene + diphosphate   (2) (2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   cis-muurola-4(14),5-diene + diphosphate   The recombinant enzyme from black peppermint produces a mixture of cis-muurola-3,5-diene and cis-muurola-4(14),5-diene.
