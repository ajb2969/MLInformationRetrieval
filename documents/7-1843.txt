   Hölder's theorem      Hölder's theorem   In mathematics , Hölder's theorem states that the gamma function does not satisfy any algebraic differential equation whose coefficients are rational functions . The result was first proved by Otto Hölder in 1887; several alternative proofs have subsequently been found. 1  The theorem also generalizes to the q-gamma function .
