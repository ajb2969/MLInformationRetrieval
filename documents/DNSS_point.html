Some references on the application of DNSS points are Caulkins et al.6 and Zeiler et al.7

History

Suresh P. Sethi identified such indifference points for the first time in 1977.8 Further, Skiba,9 Sethi,10 and Deckert and Nishimura11 explored these indifference points in economic models. The term DNSS (Deckert, Nishimura, Sethi, Skiba) points, introduced by Grass et al.,12 recognizes (alphabetically) the contributions of these authors.

These indifference points have been referred to earlier as Skiba points or DNS points in the literature.13
