Constructive function theory

In mathematical analysis, constructive function theory is a field which studies the connection between the smoothness of a function and its degree of approximation.12 It is closely related to approximation theory. The term was coined by Sergei Bernstein.

Example

Let f be a 2π-periodic function. Then f is α-Hölder for some 0 n'' of degree n such that
