Cyclohexanone monooxygenase

Cyclohexanone monooxygenase (, cyclohexanone 1,2-monooxygenase, cyclohexanone oxygenase, cyclohexanone:NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (6-hydroxylating, 1,2-lactonizing)) is an enzyme with system name cyclohexanone,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (lactone-forming).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

cyclohexanone + NADPH + H+ + O2

hexano-6-lactone + NADP+ + H2O
 

Cyclohexanone monooxygenase is a flavoprotein (FAD).
