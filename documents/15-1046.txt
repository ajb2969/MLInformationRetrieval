Chlorophyllide-a oxygenase

Chlorophyllide-a oxygenase (, chlorophyllide a oxygenase, cholorophyll-b synthase, CAO) is an enzyme with system name chlorophyllide-a:oxygen 7-oxidoreductase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) chlorophyllide a + O2 + NADPH + H+

 
  7-hydroxychlorophyllide a + H2O + NADP+

(2) 7-hydroxychlorophyllide a + O2 + NADPH + H+

chlorophyllide b + 2 H2O + NADP+


This enzyme contains a mononuclear iron centre.
