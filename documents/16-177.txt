2-hydroxymuconate tautomerase

2-hydroxymuconate tautomerase (, 4-oxalocrotonate tautomerase, 4-oxalocrotonate isomerase, cnbG (gene), praC (gene), xylH (gene)) is an enzyme with system name (2Z,4E)-2-hydroxyhexa-2,4-dienedioate keto-enol isomerase.123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2Z,4E)-2-hydroxyhexa-2,4-dienedioate 
 
 
 
  (3E)-2-oxohex-3-enedioate
 

Involved in the meta-cleavage pathway for the degradation of phenols, modified phenols and catechols.
