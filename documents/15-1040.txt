Carotenoid-9',10'-cleaving dioxygenase

Carotenoid-9',10'-cleaving dioxygenase (, BCO2 (gene), beta-carotene 9',10'-monooxygenase (misleading)) is an enzyme with system name all-trans-beta-carotene:O2 oxidoreductase (9',10'-cleaving).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

all-trans-beta-carotene + O2

 
  all-trans-10'-apo-beta-carotenal + beta-ionone


Carotenoid-9',10'-cleaving dioxygenase contains Fe2+.
