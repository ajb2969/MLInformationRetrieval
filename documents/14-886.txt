Isocitrate—homoisocitrate dehydrogenase

Isocitrate—homoisocitrate dehydrogenase is an enzyme that catalyzes the following two reactions:

isocitrate + NAD+

2-oxoglutarate + CO2 + NADH
homoisocitrate + NAD+

2-oxoadipate + CO2 + NADH + H+
