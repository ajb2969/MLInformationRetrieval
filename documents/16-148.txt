7-carboxy-7-deazaguanine synthase

7-carboxy-7-deazaguanine synthase (, 7-carboxy-7-carbaguanine synthase, queE (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 6-carboxy-5,6,7,8-tetrahydropterin ammonia-lyase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

6-carboxy-5,6,7,8-tetrahydropterin 
 
 
 
  7-carboxy-7-carbaguanine + NH3


The enzyme is a member of the superfamily of S-adenosyl-L-methionine-dependent radical enzymes.
