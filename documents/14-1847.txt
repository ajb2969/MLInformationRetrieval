   Skew gradient      Skew gradient   In mathematics , a skew gradient of a harmonic function over a simply connected domain with two real dimensions is a vector field that is everywhere orthogonal to the gradient of the function and that has the same magnitude as the gradient.  Definition  The skew gradient can be defined using complex analysis and the Cauchy–Riemann equations .
