Three dots

Three dots can refer to:

  • Ellipsis (... or . . .), indicates an intentional omission of a word
  • Asterism (⁂, sometimes *** or three dots), indicates a flourished section break in a document
  • Therefore sign ( ∴ therefore \therefore\!\, or ∴), shorthand form of the word "therefore" or "thus"
  • Because sign (), shorthand form of the word "because"
  • 3 Dots, A 2013 Malayalam film.
