5-epiaristolochene synthase

5-epiaristolochene synthase (, 5-epi-aristolochene synthase, tobacco epiaristolochene synthase, farnesyl pyrophosphate cyclase, EAS, TEAS) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((+)-5-epiaristolochene-forming).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  (+)-5-epiaristolochene + diphosphate


Initial cyclization gives (+)-germacrene A in an enzyme bound form.
