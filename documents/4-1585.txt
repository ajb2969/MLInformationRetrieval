   Cotton tensor      Cotton tensor   In differential geometry , the Cotton tensor on a (pseudo)- Riemannian manifold of dimension n is a third-order tensor concomitant of the metric , like the Weyl tensor . The vanishing of the Cotton tensor for n =3 is necessary and sufficient condition for the manifold to be conformally flat , as with the Weyl tensor for n ≥4.
