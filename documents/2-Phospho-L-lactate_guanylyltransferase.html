2-Phospho-L-lactate guanylyltransferase

2-Phospho-L-lactate guanylyltransferase (, CofC, MJ0887) is an enzyme with system name GTP:2-phospho-L-lactate guanylyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2S)-2-phospholactate + GTP ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (2S)-lactyl-2-diphospho-5'-guanosine + diphosphate

This enzyme is involved in the biosynthesis of coenzyme F420.
