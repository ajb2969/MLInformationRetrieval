   Fractional coordinates      Fractional coordinates   In crystallography , a fractional coordinate system is a coordinate system in which the edges of the unit cell are used as the basic vectors to describe the positions of atomic nuclei. The unit cell is a parallelepiped defined by the lengths of its edges a, b, c and angles between them α, β, γ as shown in the figure below.
