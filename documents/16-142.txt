L-lysine cyclodeaminase

L-lysine cyclodeaminase (, rapL (gene), fkbL (gene), tubZ (gene), visC (gene)) is an enzyme with system name L-lysine ammonia-lyase (cyclizing; ammonia-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

L-lysine

 
  L-pipecolate + NH3


This enzyme requires bound NAD+.
