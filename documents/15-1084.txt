   25-hydroxycholesterol 7α-hydroxylase      25-hydroxycholesterol 7α-hydroxylase   25-hydroxycholesterol 7alpha-hydroxylase (, 25-hydroxycholesterol 7alpha-monooxygenase , CYP7B1 , CYP7B1 oxysterol 7alpha-hydroxylase ) is an enzyme with system name cholest-5-ene-3beta,25-diol,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (7alpha-hydroxylating) . 1 2 3 4 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) cholest-5-ene-3beta,25-diol + NADPH + H + + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   cholest-5-ene-3beta,7alpha,25-triol + NADP + + H 2 O  (2) cholest-5-ene-3beta,27-diol + NADPH + H + + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   cholest-5-ene-3beta,7alpha,27-triol + NADP + + H 2 O   25-hydroxycholesterol 7α-hydroxylase is a heme-thiolate protein (P-450).
