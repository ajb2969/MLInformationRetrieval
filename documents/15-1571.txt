Methionine transaminase

Methionine transaminase (, methionine-oxo-acid transaminase) is an enzyme with system name L-methionine:2-oxo-acid aminotransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

L-methionine + 2-oxo carboxylate 
 
 
 
  2-oxo-4-methylthiobutanoate + L-amino acid
 

The enzyme is most active with L-methionine.
