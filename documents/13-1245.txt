   Balanced polygamma function      Balanced polygamma function   In mathematics, the generalized polygamma function or balanced negapolygamma function is a function introduced by Olivier Espinosa Aldunate and Victor H. Moll . 1  It generalizes the polygamma function to negative and fractional order, but remains equal to it for integer positive orders.
