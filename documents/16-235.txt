Friedelin synthase

Friedelin synthase () is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, friedelin-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene 
 
 
friedelin


The enzyme from Kalanchoe daigremontiana also gives traces of other triterpenoids.
