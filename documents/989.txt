   Mandel Q parameter      Mandel Q parameter   The Mandel Q parameter measures the departure of the occupation number distribution from Poissonian statistics . It was introduced in quantum optics by L. Mandel . 1 It is a convenient way to characterize non-classical states with negative values indicating a sub-Poissonian statistics, which have no classical analog.
