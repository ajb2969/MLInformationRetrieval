   Friedrichs' inequality      Friedrichs' inequality   In mathematics , Friedrichs' inequality is a theorem of functional analysis , due to Kurt Friedrichs . It places a bound on the L p norm of a function using L p bounds on the weak derivatives of the function and the geometry of the domain , and can be used to show that certain norms on Sobolev spaces are equivalent.  Statement of the inequality  Let Ω be a bounded subset of Euclidean space  R n with diameter  d .
