6₂ knot

In knot theory, the 62 knot is one of three prime knots with crossing number six, the others being the stevedore knot and the 63 knot. This knot is sometimes referred to as the Miller Institute knot,1 because it appears in the logo2 of the Miller Institute for Basic Research in Science at the University of California, Berkeley.
