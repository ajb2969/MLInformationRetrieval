Molybdenum cofactor sulfurtransferase

Molybdenum cofactor sulfurtransferase (, molybdenum cofactor sulfurase, ABA3, HMCS, MoCo sulfurase, MoCo sulfurtransferase) is an enzyme with system name L-cysteine:molybdenum cofactor sulfurtransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

molybdenum cofactor + L-cysteine + 2 H+

 
  thio-molybdenum cofactor + L-alanine + H2O
 

This enzyme contains pyridoxal phosphate.
