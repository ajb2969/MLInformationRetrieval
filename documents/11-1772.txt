   Dmrg of Heisenberg model      Dmrg of Heisenberg model   This example presents the infinite DMRG algorithm . It is about    S  =  1      S  1    S=1   antiferromagnetic Heisenberg chain , but the recipe can be applied for every translationally invariant one-dimensional lattice . DMRG is a renormalization-group technique because it offers an efficient truncation of the Hilbert space of one-dimensional quantum systems . The first is the Block site , the last the Universe-Block site and the remaining are the added sites , the right one is "added" to the Universe-Block site and the other to the Block site.
