Protostadienol synthase

Protostadienol synthase (, PdsA, (S)-2,3-epoxysqualene mutase [cyclizing, (17Z)-protosta-17(20),24-dien-3beta-ol-forming]) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, (17Z)-protosta-17(20),24-dien-3beta-ol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene 
 
 
 
  (17Z)-protosta-17(20),24-dien-3beta-ol
 

(17Z)-Protosta-17(20),24-dien-3beta-ol is a precursor of the steroidal antibiotic helvolic acid.
