In mathematics , the Riemann Xi function is a variant of the Riemann zeta function , and is defined so as to have a particularly simple functional equation . The function is named in honour of Bernhard Riemann .  Definition  Riemann's original lower-case xi-function, ξ, has been renamed with an upper-case Xi, Ξ, by Edmund Landau (see below).
