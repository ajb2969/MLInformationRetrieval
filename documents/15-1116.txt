Dimethyl-sulfide monooxygenase

Dimethyl-sulfide monooxygenase (, dimethylsulfide monooxygenase) is an enzyme with system name dimethyl sulfide,NADH:oxygen oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

dimethyl sulfide + O2 + NADH + H+

methanethiol + formaldehyde + NAD+ + H2O
 

Dimethyl-sulfide monooxygenase has lower activity with diethyl sulfide and other short-chain alkyl methyl sulfides.23
