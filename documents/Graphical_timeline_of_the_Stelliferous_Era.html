Graphical timeline of the Stelliferous Era

This is the timeline of the stelliferous era but also partly charts the primordial era, and charts more of the degenerate era of the heat death scenario.

The scale is 10 × log 10 year 10 subscript 10 year 10\times\log_{10}\mathrm{year} . Example one million years is 10 × log 10 1000000 = 10 × 6 = 60 10 subscript 10 1000000 10 6 60 10\times\log_{10}1000000=10\times 6=60 .
