Gamma-humulene synthase

Gamma-humulene synthase (, humulene cyclase) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (gamma-humulene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  gamma-humulene + diphosphate
