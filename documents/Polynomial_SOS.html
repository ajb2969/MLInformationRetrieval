Polynomial SOS

Matrix SOS

A matrix form F(x) (i.e., a matrix whose entries are forms) of dimension r and degree 2m in the real n-dimensional vector x is SOS if and only if there exist matrix forms G 1 ( x ) , … , G k ( x ) subscript G 1 x normal-… subscript G k x G_{1}(x),\ldots,G_{k}(x) of degree m such that
