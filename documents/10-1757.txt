Other definitions of the Q -function, all of which are simple transformations of the normal cumulative distribution function , are also used occasionally. 3  Because of its relation to the cumulative distribution function of the normal distribution, the Q -function can also be expressed in terms of the error function , which is an important function in applied mathematics and physics.
