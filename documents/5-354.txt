   Lerch zeta function      Lerch zeta function   In mathematics , the Lerch zeta-function , sometimes called the Hurwitz–Lerch zeta-function , is a special function that generalizes the Hurwitz zeta-function and the polylogarithm . It is named after the Czech mathematician Mathias Lerch  1 .
