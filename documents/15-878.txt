(-)-Isopiperitenone reductase

(-)-Isopiperitenone reductase () is an enzyme with system name (+)-cis-isopulegone:NADP+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(+)-cis-isopulegone + NADP+

 
  (-)-isopiperitenone + NADPH + H+


The reaction occurs in the opposite direction.
