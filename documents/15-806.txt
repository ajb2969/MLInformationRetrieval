L-galactose 1-dehydrogenase

L-galactose 1-dehydrogenase (, L-GalDH, L-galactose dehydrogenase) is an enzyme with system name L-galactose:NAD+ 1-oxidoreductase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

L-galactose + NAD+

 
  L-galactono-1,4-lactone + NADH + H+


The enzyme catalyses a step in the ascorbate biosynthesis in higher plants.
