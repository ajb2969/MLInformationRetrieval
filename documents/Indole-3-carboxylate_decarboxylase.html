Indole-3-carboxylate decarboxylase

Indole-3-carboxylate decarboxylase () is an enzyme with system name indole-3-carboxylate carboxy-lyase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

indole-3-carboxylate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons indole + CO2

This enzyme is activated by Zn2+, Mn2+ or Mg2+.
