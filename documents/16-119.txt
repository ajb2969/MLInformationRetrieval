2-deoxy-scyllo-inosose synthase

2-deoxy-scyllo-inosose synthase (, btrC (gene), neoC (gene), kanC (gene)) is an enzyme with system name D-glucose-6-phosphate phosphate-lyase (2-deoxy-scyllo-inosose-forming).1234567 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

D-glucose 6-phosphate

 
  2-deoxy-L-scyllo-inosose + phosphate


This enzyme requires Co2+.
