   Spherical cap      Spherical cap   (Figure)  The spherical cap is the purple section.   In geometry , a spherical cap or spherical dome is a portion of a sphere cut off by a plane . If the plane passes through the center of the sphere, so that the height of the cap is equal to the radius of the sphere, the spherical cap is called a hemisphere .
