   Non-specific polyamine oxidase      Non-specific polyamine oxidase   Non-specific polyamine oxidase (, polyamine oxidase , Fms1 , AtPAO3 ) is an enzyme with system name polyamine:oxygen oxidoreductase (3-aminopropanal or 3-acetamidopropanal-forming) . 1 2 3 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) spermine + O 2 + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    spermidine + 3-aminopropanal + H 2 O 2   (2) spermidine + O 2 + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    putrescine + 3-aminopropanal + H 2 O 2   (3) N 1 -acetylspermine + O 2 + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    spermidine + 3-acetamidopropanal + H 2 O 2   (4) N 1 -acetylspermidine + O 2 + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    putrescine + 3-acetamidopropanal + H 2 O 2    This enzyme is flavoprotein (FAD).
