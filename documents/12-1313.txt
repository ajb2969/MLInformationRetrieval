   Barnes integral      Barnes integral   In mathematics, a Barnes integral or Mellin –Barnes integral is a contour integral involving a product of gamma functions . They were introduced by . They are closely related to generalized hypergeometric series .  The integral is usually taken along a contour which is a deformation of the imaginary axis passing to the left of all poles of factors of the form Γ( a + s ) and to the right of all poles of factors of the form Γ( a − s ).
