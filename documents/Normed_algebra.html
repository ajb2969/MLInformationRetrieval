Normed algebra

In mathematics, a normed algebra A refers to an algebra over a field which has a sub-multiplicative norm:

∀ x , y ∈ A ∥ x y ∥ ≤ ∥ x ∥ ∥ y ∥ formulae-sequence for-all x y A norm x y norm x norm y \forall x,y\in A\qquad\|xy\|\leq\|x\|\|y\|

Optionally, it is sometimes required to have a multiplicative identity 1 A A {}_{A} such as ║1 A A {}_{A} ║ = 1.
