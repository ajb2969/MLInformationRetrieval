Sulfite reductase (ferredoxin)

Sulfite reductase (ferredoxin) (, ferredoxin-sulfite reductase) is an enzyme with system name hydrogen-sulfide:ferredoxin oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalises the following chemical reaction

hydrogen sulfide + 6 oxidized ferredoxin + 3 H2O 
 
 
sulfite + 6 reduced ferredoxin + 6 H+


This sulfite reductase is an iron protein.
