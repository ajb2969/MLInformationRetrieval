(3R,6E)-nerolidol synthase

(3R,6E)-nerolidol synthase (, terpene synthase 1) is an enzyme with system name ''(2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((3R,6E)-nerolidol-forming) ''.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
 
  (3R,6E)-nerolidol + diphosphate


The enzyme catalyses a step in the formation of (3E)-4,8-dimethylnona-1,3,7-triene.
