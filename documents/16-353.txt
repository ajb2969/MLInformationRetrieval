   Lie group action      Lie group action   In differential geometry, a Lie group action on a manifold M is a group action by a Lie group  G on M that is a differentiable map ; in particular, it is a continuous group action . Together with a Lie group action by G , M is called a G -manifold . The orbit types of G form a stratification of M and this can be used to understand the geometry of M .
