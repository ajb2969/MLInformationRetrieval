Historical origin of the parameter Îµ 0  As indicated above, the parameter Îµ 0 is a measurement-system constant. Its presence in the equations now used to define electromagnetic quantities is the result of the so-called "rationalization" process described below. But the method of allocating a value to it is a consequence of the result that Maxwell's equations predict that, in free space, electromagnetic waves move with the speed of light. Understanding why Îµ 0 has the value it does requires a brief understanding of the history.
