Geranyl-pyrophosphate—olivetolic acid geranyltransferase

Geranyl-pyrophosphate—olivetolic acid geranyltransferase (, GOT) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate:olivetolate geranyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate + 2,4-dihydroxy-6-pentylbenzoate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons diphosphate + cannabigerolate

Part of the cannabinoids biosynthetic pathway of the plant Cannabis sativa.
