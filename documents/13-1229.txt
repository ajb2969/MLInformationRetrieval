   Uncertainty theory      Uncertainty theory   Uncertainty theory is a branch of mathematics based on normality, monotonicity, self-duality, countable subadditivity, and product measure axioms. It was founded by Baoding Liu 1 in 2007 and refined in 2009. 2  Mathematical measures of the likelihood of an event being true include probability theory , capacity, fuzzy logic , possibility, and credibility, as well as uncertainty.
