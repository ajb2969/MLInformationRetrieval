Nitric oxide reductase (menaquinol)

Nitric oxide reductase (menaquinol) () is an enzyme.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 nitric oxide + menaquinol

nitrous oxide + menaquinone + H2O
 

Nitric oxide reductase contains copper.
