1,8-Cineole 2-endo-monooxygenase

1,8-Cineole 2-endo-monooxygenase (, P450cin, CYP176A, CYP176A1) is an enzyme with system name 1,8-cineole,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (2-endo-hydroxylating).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

1,8-cineole + NADPH + H+ + O2

 
  2-endo-hydroxy-1,8-cineole + NADP+ + H2O
 

1,8-Cineole 2-endo-monooxygenase is a heme-thiolate protein (P-450).
