Herbrand structure

In mathematics, for a language ℒ ℒ \mathcal{L} , define the Herbrand universe to be the set of ground terms of ℒ ℒ \mathcal{L} .

This fixes the domain of 𝔐 𝔐 \mathfrak{M} , and so each Herbrand structure can be identified with its interpretation.
