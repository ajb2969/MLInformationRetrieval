History

The manuscript was written by Theophylact, a monk.7 It once belonged to Colbert.8 The manuscript was examined by Paulin Martin.9 Formerly it was designated by 25a,10 in 1908 Gregory gave for it number 147,11 that number formerly belonged to Latin manuscript. Gregory saw it in 1885.12

The manuscript is cited in the critical editions of the Greek New Testament (UBS3,13 UBS414).
