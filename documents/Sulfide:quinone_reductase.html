Sulfide:quinone reductase

Sulfide:quinone reductase () is an enzyme with system name sulfide:quinone oxidoreductase.123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

n HS− + n quinone ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons polysulfide + n quinol

Sulfide:quinone reductase contains FAD. Ubiquinone, plastoquinone or menaquinone can act as acceptor in different species.
