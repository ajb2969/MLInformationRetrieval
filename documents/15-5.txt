   Gauss–Legendre method      Gauss–Legendre method   In numerical analysis and scientific computing , the Gauss–Legendre methods are a family of numerical methods for ordinary differential equations . Gauss–Legendre methods are implicit Runge–Kutta methods . More specifically, they are collocation methods based on the points of Gauss–Legendre quadrature . The Gauss–Legendre method based on s points has order 2 s . 1  All Gauss–Legendre methods are A-stable . 2  The Gauss–Legendre method of order two is the implicit midpoint rule .
