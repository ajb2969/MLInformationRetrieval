Biography
Balakram was born in Jalandhar, Punjab in 1876. He received his master's degree from Panjab University, Chandigarh and later passed the Mathematical Tripos from the St John's College, Cambridge.2
In 1899 he passed the Indian Civil Service examination. Among other posts in his career he was appointed a Bombay High Court judge in 1929 a month before his death.3 He also served as the president of Indian Mathematical Society from 1921 to 26.4
