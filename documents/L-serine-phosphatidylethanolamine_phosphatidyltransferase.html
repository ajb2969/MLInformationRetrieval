L-serine-phosphatidylethanolamine phosphatidyltransferase

L-serine-phosphatidylethanolamine phosphatidyltransferase (, phosphatidylserine synthase 2, serine-exchange enzyme II, PTDSS2 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name L-1-phosphatidylethanolamine:L-serine phosphatidyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

L-1-phosphatidylethanolamine + L-serine ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons L-1-phosphatidylserine + ethanolamine

This enzyme catalyses replacement of a polar head group of phosphatidylethanolamine with L-serine.
