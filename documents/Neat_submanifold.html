Neat submanifold

In differential topology, an area of mathematics, a neat submanifold of a manifold with boundary is a kind of "well-behaved" submanifold. More precisely, let M M M be a manifold with boundary, and A A A a submanifold of M M M . A is said to be a neat submanifold of M M M if it meets the following two conditions:1
