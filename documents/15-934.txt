


1-Pyrroline dehydrogenase




1-Pyrroline dehydrogenase

1-pyrroline dehydrogenase (, gamma-aminobutyraldehyde dehydrogenase, ABALDH, YdcW) is an enzyme with system name 1-pyrroline:NAD+ oxidoreductase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

1-pyrroline + NAD+ + 2 H2O 
 
 
4-aminobutanoate + NADH + 2 H+


This enzyme participates in the arginine-catabolism pathway.
