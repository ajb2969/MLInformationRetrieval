== Summary == This plot shows the locations of the zeros of the Jacobi polynomials of order 8 as a function of the Jacobi parameter, α = - 0.5 α 0.5 \alpha=-0.5 , on the left side. On the right is the corresponding Lebesgue constant for the grid. The dashed line indicates the special case α = - 0.5 α 0.5 \alpha=-0.5 which gives the Chebyshev polynomials of the first kind. The minimum Lebesgue number occurs very close to α = - 0.5 α 0.5 \alpha=-0.5 .
