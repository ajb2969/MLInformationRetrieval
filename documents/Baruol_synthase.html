Baruol synthase

Baruol synthase (, BARS1) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, baruol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons baruol

The enzyme from Arabidopsis thaliana also produces traces of 22 other triterpenoids.
