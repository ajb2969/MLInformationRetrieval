(+)-Pulegone reductase

(+)-pulegone reductase () is an enzyme with system name (-)-menthone:NADP+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalises the following chemical reaction

(1) (-)-menthone + NADP+

 
  (+)-pulegone + NADPH + H+

(2) (+)-isomenthone + NADP+

 
  (+)-pulegone + NADPH + H+


NADH cannot replace NADPH as reductant.
