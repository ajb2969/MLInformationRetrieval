Molybdopterin molybdotransferase

Molybdopterin molybdotransferase (, MoeA, Cnx1) is an enzyme with system name adenylyl-molybdopterin:molybdate molybdate transferase (AMP-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

adenylyl-molybdopterin + molybdate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons molybdenum cofactor + AMP

Catalyses the insertion of molybdenum into the ene-dithiol group of molybdopterin.
