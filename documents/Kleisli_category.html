Kleisli category

In category theory, a Kleisli category is a category naturally associated to any monad T. It is equivalent to the category of free T-algebras. The Kleisli category is one of two extremal solutions to the question Does every monad arise from an adjunction? The other extremal solution is the Eilenberg‚ÄìMoore category. Kleisli categories are named for the mathematician Heinrich Kleisli.
