Binary cyclic group

In mathematics, the binary cyclic group of the n-gon is the cyclic group of order 2n, C 2 n subscript C 2 n C_{2n} , thought of as an extension of the cyclic group C n subscript C n C_{n} by a cyclic group of order 2. It is the binary polyhedral group corresponding to the cyclic group.1
