   Classifying space for U(n)      Classifying space for U(n)   In mathematics , the classifying space for the unitary group U( n ) is a space BU( n ) together with a universal bundle EU( n ) such that any hermitian bundle on a paracompact space  X is the pull-back of EU( n ) by a map X ‚Üí BU( n ) unique up to homotopy.  This space with its universal fibration may be constructed as either   the Grassmannian of n -planes in an infinite-dimensional complex Hilbert space ; or,  the direct limit, with the induced topology, of Grassmannians of n planes.   Both constructions are detailed here.
