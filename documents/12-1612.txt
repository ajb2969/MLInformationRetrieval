   Papyrus 106      Papyrus 106   Papyrus 106 (in the Gregory-Aland numbering), designated by   𝔓   𝔓   \mathfrak{P}    106 , is a copy of the New Testament in Greek . It is a papyrus  manuscript of the Gospel of John , containing verses 1:29-35 & 1:40-46 in a fragmentary condition.
