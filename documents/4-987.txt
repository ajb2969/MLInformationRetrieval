   Strong generating set      Strong generating set   In abstract algebra , especially in the area of group theory , a strong generating set of a permutation group is a generating set that clearly exhibits the permutation structure as described by a stabilizer chain . A stabilizer chain is a sequence of subgroups , each containing the next and each stabilizing one more point.
