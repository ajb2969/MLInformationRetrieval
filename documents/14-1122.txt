The theorem provides a test for a given S-matrix to determine whether or not it can be constructed as a lossless rational two-port network .  Lossless implies that the network contains only inductances and capacitances - no resistances . Rational (meaning the driving point impedance  Z ( p ) is a rational function of p ) implies that the network consists solely of discrete elements ( inductors and capacitors only - no distributed elements ).
