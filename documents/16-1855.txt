Purpose
Convert redshift in values of km/s (ie kps) (kilometres per second) to light travel distance in terms of Mpc (megaparsecs)
Usage


(MANDATORY) value of redshift in terms of units in kilometers per second

(OPTIONAL) adds Mpc to the display

(OPTIONAL) define the Hubble Constant (H0) in terms of kps/Mpc if you do not wish to use the default of 71kps/Mpc
