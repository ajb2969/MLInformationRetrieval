   Barnes G-function      Barnes G-function   In mathematics , the Barnes G-function  G ( z ) is a function that is an extension of superfactorials to the complex numbers . It is related to the Gamma function , the K-function and the Glaisher–Kinkelin constant , and was named after mathematician  Ernest William Barnes . 1 Up to elementary factors, it is a special case of the double gamma function .
