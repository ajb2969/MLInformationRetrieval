Gingerbreadman map

 In dynamical systems theory, the Gingerbreadman map is a chaotic 2D map. It is given by the transformation:
$$\begin{cases}
 x_{n+1} = 1 - y_n + |x_n|\\
 y_{n+1} = x_n
 \end{cases}$$
