2,3-diketo-5-methylthiopentyl-1-phosphate enolase

2,3-diketo-5-methylthiopentyl-1-phosphate enolase (, DK-MTP-1-P enolase, MtnW, YkrW, RuBisCO-like protein, RLP) is an enzyme with system name 2,3-diketo-5-methylthiopentyl-1-phosphate keto-enol-isomerase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

5-(methylthio)-2,3-dioxopentyl phosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2-hydroxy-5-(methylthio)-3-oxopent-1-enyl phosphate

The enzyme participates in the methionine salvage pathway in Bacillus subtilis.
