   Current (mathematics)      Current (mathematics)   In mathematics , more particularly in functional analysis , differential topology , and geometric measure theory , a k-current in the sense of Georges de Rham is a functional on the space of compactly supported  differential k-forms , on a smooth manifold  M . Formally currents behave like Schwartz distributions on a space of differential forms. In a geometric setting, they can represent integration over a submanifold, generalizing the Dirac delta function , or more generally even directional derivatives of delta functions ( multipoles ) spread out along subsets of M .
