Deacetylisoipecoside synthase

In enzymology, a deacetylisoipecoside synthase () is an enzyme that catalyzes the chemical reaction

deacetylisoipecoside + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons dopamine + secologanin

Thus, the two substrates of this enzyme are deacetylisoipecoside and H2O, whereas its two products are dopamine and secologanin.

This enzyme belongs to the family of lyases, specifically amine lyases, which cleave carbon-nitrogen bonds. The systematic name of this enzyme class is deacetylisoipecoside dopamine-lyase (secologanin-forming). This enzyme is also called deacetylisoipecoside dopamine-lyase. This enzyme participates in indole and ipecac alkaloid biosynthesis.
