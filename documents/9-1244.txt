Nitrate reductase (NADH)

Nitrate reductase (NADH) (, assimilatory nitrate reductase, NADH-nitrate reductase, NADH-dependent nitrate reductase, assimilatory NADH: nitrate reductase, nitrate reductase (NADH2), NADH2:nitrate oxidoreductase) is an enzyme with system name nitrite:NAD+ oxidoreductase.12345 This enzyme catalises the following chemical reaction

nitrite + NAD+ + H2O 
 
 
nitrate + NADH + H+


Nitrate reductase us an iron-sulfur molybdenum flavoprotein.
