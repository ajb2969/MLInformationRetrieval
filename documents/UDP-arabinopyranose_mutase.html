UDP-arabinopyranose mutase

UDP-arabinopyranose mutase (, Os03g40270 protein, UAM1, UAM3, RGP1, RGP3, OsUAM1, OsUAM2, Os07g41360 protein) is an enzyme with system name UDP-arabinopyranose pyranomutase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

UDP-beta-L-arabinofuranose ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons UDP-beta-L-arabinopyranose

The reaction is reversible.
