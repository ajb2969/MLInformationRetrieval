   Vector radiative transfer      Vector radiative transfer   In spectroscopy and radiometry , vector radiative transfer (VRT) is a method of modelling the propagation of polarized electromagnetic radiation in low density media. In contrast to scalar  radiative transfer (RT), which models only the first Stokes component , the intensity, VRT models all four components through vector methods.
