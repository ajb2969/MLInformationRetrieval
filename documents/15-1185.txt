Pyrimidine oxygenase

Pyrimidine oxygenase (, RutA) is an enzyme with system name ''uracil,FMNH2:oxygen oxidoreductase (uracil hydroxylating, ring-opening) ''.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) uracil + FMNH2 + O2

 
  (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid + FMN
 
(2) thymine + FMNH2 + O2

 
  (Z)-2-methylureidoacrylate peracid + FMN
 

In vitro the product (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid is spontaneously reduced to ureidoacrylate.
