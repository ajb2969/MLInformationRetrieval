   Pyrimidine oxygenase      Pyrimidine oxygenase   Pyrimidine oxygenase (, RutA ) is an enzyme with system name ''uracil,FMNH 2 :oxygen oxidoreductase (uracil hydroxylating, ring-opening) ''. 1 2 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) uracil + FMNH 2 + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid + FMN  (2) thymine + FMNH 2 + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (Z)-2-methylureidoacrylate peracid + FMN   In vitro the product (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid is spontaneously reduced to ureidoacrylate .
