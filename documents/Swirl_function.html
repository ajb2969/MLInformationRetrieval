Swirl function

In mathematics, swirl functions are special functions defined as follows1：

S ( k , n , r , θ ) = sin ( k cos ( r ) - n θ ) S k n r θ k r n θ S(k,n,r,\theta)=\sin(k\cos(r)-n\theta)

where k, n are integers.

n is the number of blades, k is related to the shape of each blade.

Symmmetry

The function S(k,n,r,θ) satisifies the following relations:

mirror symmetry
  • f ( - k , n , r , θ ) = - f ( k , n , r , - θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(-k,n,r,\theta)=-f(k,n,r,-\theta)
  • f ( - k , n , r , θ ) = - f ( k , - n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(-k,n,r,\theta)=-f(k,-n,r,\theta)
  • f ( - k , - n , r , θ ) = - f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(-k,-n,r,\theta)=-f(k,n,r,\theta)
  • f ( - k , n , r , - θ ) = - f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(-k,n,r,-\theta)=-f(k,n,r,\theta)
  • f ( - k , n , r , θ ) = - f ( k , n , - r , - θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(-k,n,r,\theta)=-f(k,n,-r,-\theta)
  • f ( - k , n , - r , - θ ) = - f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(-k,n,-r,-\theta)=-f(k,n,r,\theta)
  • f ( - k , - n , - r , θ ) = - f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(-k,-n,-r,\theta)=-f(k,n,r,\theta)
  • f ( - k , n , - r , - θ ) = - f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(-k,n,-r,-\theta)=-f(k,n,r,\theta)
full symmetry
  • f ( k , - n , r , θ ) = f ( k , n , r , - θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(k,-n,r,\theta)=f(k,n,r,-\theta)
  • f ( k , - n , r , - θ ) = f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(k,-n,r,-\theta)=f(k,n,r,\theta)
  • f ( k , n , - r , θ ) = f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(k,n,-r,\theta)=f(k,n,r,\theta)
  • f ( k , n , - r , θ ) = f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(k,n,-r,\theta)=f(k,n,r,\theta)
  • f ( k , n , - r , θ ) = f ( k , - n , r , - θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(k,n,-r,\theta)=f(k,-n,r,-\theta)
  • f ( k , - n , - r , - θ ) = f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(k,-n,-r,-\theta)=f(k,n,r,\theta)
  • f ( k , n , - r , θ ) - f ( k , n , r , θ ) f k n r θ f k n r θ f(k,n,-r,\theta)-f(k,n,r,\theta)
rotation symmetry

S ( k , n , r , θ + 2 π n ) = S ( k , n , r , θ ) S k n r θ 2 π n S k n r θ S\left(k,n,r,\theta+\frac{2\pi}{n}\right)=S(k,n,r,\theta)
