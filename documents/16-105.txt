(+)-sabinene synthase

(+)-sabinene synthase (, SS) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, (+)-sabinene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate

 
  (+)-sabinene + diphosphate


This enzyme is isolated from Salvia officinalis (sage).
