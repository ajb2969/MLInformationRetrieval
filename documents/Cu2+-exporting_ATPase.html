Cu2+-exporting ATPase

Cu2+-exporting ATPase () is an enzyme with system name ATP phosphohydrolase (Cu2+-exporting).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + H2O + Cu2+in ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + phosphate + Cu2+out

This P-type ATPase undergoes covalent phosphorylation during the transport cycle.
