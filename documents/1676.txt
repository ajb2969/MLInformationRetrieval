   Block matrix pseudoinverse      Block matrix pseudoinverse   In mathematics, block matrix pseudoinverse is a formula of pseudoinverse of a partitioned matrix . This is useful for decomposing or approximating many algorithms updating parameters in signal processing , which are based on least squares method.
