   Elliptic hypergeometric series      Elliptic hypergeometric series   In mathematics, an elliptic hypergeometric series is a series Σ c n such that the ratio c n / c n −1 is an elliptic function of n , analogous to generalized hypergeometric series where the ratio is a rational function of n , and basic hypergeometric series where the ratio is a periodic function of the complex number n . They were introduced by  in their study of elliptic 6-j symbols .  For surveys of elliptic hypergeometric series see  or .
