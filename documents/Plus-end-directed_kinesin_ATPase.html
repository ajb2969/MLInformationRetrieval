Plus-end-directed kinesin ATPase

Plus-end-directed kinesin ATPase (, kinesin) is an enzyme with system name kinesin ATP phosphohydrolase (plus-end-directed).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + phosphate

This enzyme also hydrolyses GTP.
