   Tensor product of quadratic forms      Tensor product of quadratic forms   The tensor product of quadratic forms is most easily understood when one views the quadratic forms as quadratic spaces . So, if ( V , q 1 ) and ( W , q 2 ) are quadratic spaces, with V , W  vector spaces , then the tensor product is a quadratic form q on the tensor product of vector spaces  V ⊗ W .
