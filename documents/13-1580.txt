   Quadratic Lie algebra      Quadratic Lie algebra   A quadratic Lie algebra is a Lie algebra together with a compatible symmetric bilinear form. Compatibility means that it is invariant under the adjoint representation . Examples of such are semisimple Lie algebras , such as su(n) and sl(n, R ) .
