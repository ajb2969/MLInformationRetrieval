   Indifference price      Indifference price   In finance , indifference pricing is a method of pricing financial securities with regard to a utility function . The ' indifference price ' is also known as the reservation price or private valuation . In particular, the indifference price is the price at which an agent would have the same expected utility level by exercising a financial transaction as by not doing so (with optimal trading otherwise).
