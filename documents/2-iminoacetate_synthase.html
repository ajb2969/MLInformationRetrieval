2-iminoacetate synthase

2-iminoacetate synthase (, thiH (gene)) is an enzyme with system name L-tyrosine 4-methylphenol-lyase (2-iminoacetate-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

L-tyrosine + S-adenosyl-L-methionine + reduced acceptor ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2-iminoacetate + 4-methylphenol + 5'-deoxyadenosine + L-methionine + acceptor + 2 H+

This enzyme binds a 4Fe-4S cluster.
