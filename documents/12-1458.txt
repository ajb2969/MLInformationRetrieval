   Papyrus 43      Papyrus 43   Papyrus 43 (Gregory-Aland), signed by    𝔓   𝔓   \mathfrak{P}    43 , is an early copy of the New Testament in Greek . It is a papyrus  manuscript of the Book of Revelation , it contains Rev 2:12-13; 15:8-16:2. The manuscript paleographically has been assigned to the 6th or 7th century.  Description  The Greek text of this codex is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type .
