   Root system of a semi-simple Lie algebra      Root system of a semi-simple Lie algebra   In mathematics , there is a one-to-one correspondence between reduced crystallographic root systems and semi-simple Lie algebras . Here the construction of a root system of a semi-simple Lie algebra and, conversely, the construction of a semi-simple Lie algebra from a reduced crystallographic root systemare shown.
