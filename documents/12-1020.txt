   Continuant (mathematics)      Continuant (mathematics)   In algebra , the continuant is a multivariate polynomial representing the determinant of a tridiagonal matrix and having applications in generalized continued fractions . An extended definition takes the continuant with respect to three sequences a , b and c , so that K ( n ) is a polynomial of a 1 ,..., a n , b 1 ,..., b n −1 and c 1 ,..., c n −1 .
