   Dual wavelet      Dual wavelet   In mathematics , a dual wavelet is the dual to a wavelet . In general, the wavelet series generated by a square integrable  function will have a dual series, in the sense of the Riesz representation theorem . However, the dual series is not in general representable by a square integral function itself.
