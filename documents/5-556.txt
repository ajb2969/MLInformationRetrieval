   BF model      BF model   The BF model is a topological field theory , which when quantized , becomes a topological quantum field theory . BF stands for background field . B and F, as can be seen below, are also the variables appearing in the Lagrangian of the theory, which is helpful as a mnemonic device.  We have a 4-dimensional differentiable manifold M, a gauge group G, which has as "dynamical" fields a two-form  B taking values in the adjoint representation of G, and a connection form  A for G.
