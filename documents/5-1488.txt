   Weak topology (polar topology)      Weak topology (polar topology)   In functional analysis and related areas of mathematics the weak topology is the coarsest  polar topology , the topology with the fewest open sets , on a dual pair . The finest polar topology is called strong topology .  Under the weak topology the bounded sets coincide with the relatively compact sets which leads to the important Bourbaki–Alaoglu theorem .
