   (R)-amidase      (R)-amidase   (R)-amidase (, R-stereospecific amidase , R-amidase ) is an enzyme with system name (R)-piperazine-2-carboxamide amidohydrolase . 1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) (R)-piperazine-2-carboxamide + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (R)-piperazine-2-carboxylic acid + NH 3   (2) beta-alaninamide + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    beta-alanine + NH 3    This enzyme also hydrolyses (R)-piperidine-3-carboxamide to (R)-piperidine-3-carboxylic acid and NH3.
