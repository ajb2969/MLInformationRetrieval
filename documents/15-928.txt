Primary-amine oxidase () is an enzyme with system name primary-amine:oxygen oxidoreductase (deaminating).12345678910 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

RCH2NH2 + H2O + O2

 
  RCHO + NH3 + H2O2


These enzymes are copper quinoproteins (2,4,5-trihydroxyphenylalanine quinone).
