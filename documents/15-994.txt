O-aminophenol oxidase

O-aminophenol oxidase (, isophenoxazine synthase, o-aminophenol:O2 oxidoreductase, 2-aminophenol:O2 oxidoreductase, GriF) is an enzyme with system name 2-aminophenol:oxygen oxidoreductase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 2-aminophenol + O2 + acceptor 
 
 
 
  2-aminophenoxazin-3-one + reduced acceptor + 2 H2O (overall reaction)
 
(1a) 2 2-aminophenol + O2

 
  2 6-iminocyclohexa-2,4-dienone + 2 H2O
 
(1b) 2 6-iminocyclohexa-2,4-dienone + acceptor 
 
 
 
  2-aminophenoxazin-3-one + reduced acceptor (spontaneous)
 

O-aminophenol oxidase is a flavoprotein.
