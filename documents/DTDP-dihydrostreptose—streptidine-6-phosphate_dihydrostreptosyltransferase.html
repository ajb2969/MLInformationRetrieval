DTDP-dihydrostreptose—streptidine-6-phosphate dihydrostreptosyltransferase

DTDP-dihydrostreptose---streptidine-6-phosphate dihydrostreptosyltransferase (, thymidine diphosphodihydrostreptose-streptidine 6-phosphate dihydrostreptosyltransferase) is an enzyme with system name dTDP-L-dihydrostreptose:streptidine-6-phosphate dihydrostreptosyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

dTDP-L-dihydrostreptose + streptidine 6-phosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons dTDP + O-(1->4)-alpha-L-dihydrostreptosyl-streptidine 6-phosphate
