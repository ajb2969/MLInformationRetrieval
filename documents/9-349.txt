   Dirichlet algebra      Dirichlet algebra   In mathematics , a Dirichlet algebra is a particular type of algebra associated to a compact Hausdorff space X . It is a closed subalgebra of C ( X ), the uniform algebra of bounded continuous functions on X , whose real parts are dense in the algebra of bounded continuous real functions on X .
