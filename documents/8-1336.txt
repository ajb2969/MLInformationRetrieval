   Runge–Kutta method (SDE)      Runge–Kutta method (SDE)   In mathematics of stochastic systems, the Runge–Kutta method is a technique for the approximate numerical solution of a stochastic differential equation . It is a generalization of the Runge–Kutta method for ordinary differential equations to stochastic differential equations (SDEs). Importantly, the method does not involve knowing derivatives the coefficient functions in the SDEs.
