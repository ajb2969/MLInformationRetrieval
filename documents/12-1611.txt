It is a papyrus  manuscript of the Gospel of Matthew . The surviving texts of Matthew are verses 27:62-64; 28:2-5, they are in a fragmentary condition. The manuscript has been paleographically estimated to date back to the 5th or 6th century CE. 1   Text   The Greek text of the codex is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type .
