Aliphatic (R)-hydroxynitrile lyase

Aliphatic (R)-hydroxynitrile lyase (, (R)-HNL, (R)-oxynitrilase, (R)-hydroxynitrile lyase, LuHNL) is an enzyme with system name (2R)-2-hydroxy-2-methylbutanenitrile butan-2-one-lyase (cyanide forming).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2R)-2-hydroxy-2-methylbutanenitrile 
 
 
cyanide + butan-2-one
 

The enzyme contains Zn2+.
