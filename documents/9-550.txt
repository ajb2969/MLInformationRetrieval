   List of thermal conductivities      List of thermal conductivities   In heat transfer , the thermal conductivity of a substance, k, is an intensive property that indicates its ability to conduct heat .  Thermal conductivity is often measured with laser flash analysis . Alternative measurements are also established.  Mixtures may have variable thermal conductivities due to composition.  Note that this table shows thermal conductivity in units of watts per metre per Kelvin (W·m −1 ·K −1 ). This is the current metric unit of measurement. Prior to this, however, thermal conductivity was measured in terms of BTUs per foot per hour per degree Fahrenheit.
