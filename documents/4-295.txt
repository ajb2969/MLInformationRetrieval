   Batting average against      Batting average against  >In baseball statistics , batting average against (denoted by BAA or AVG ), or opponents' batting average (denoted by OBA ) is a statistic that measures a pitcher 's ability to prevent hits during official at bats . It can alternatively be described as the league's hitters' combined batting average against the pitcher.
