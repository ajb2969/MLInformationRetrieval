   Pentagonal orthocupolarotunda      Pentagonal orthocupolarotunda   In geometry , the pentagonal orthocupolarotunda is one of the Johnson solids ( J 32 ). As the name suggests, it can be constructed by joining a pentagonal cupola ( J 5 ) and a pentagonal rotunda ( J 6 ) along their decagonal bases, matching the pentagonal faces. A 36-degree rotation of one of the halves before the joining yields a pentagonal gyrocupolarotunda ( J 33 ).
