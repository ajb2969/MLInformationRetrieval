23S rRNA pseudouridine2605 synthase

23S rRNA pseudouridine2605 synthase (, RluB, YciL) is an enzyme with system name 23S rRNA-uridine2605 uracil mutase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

23S rRNA uridine2605

 
  23S rRNA pseudouridine2605


Pseudouridine synthase RluB converts uridine2605 of 23S rRNA to pseudouridine.
