Dimethylsulfone reductase

Dimethylsulfone reductase () is an enzyme.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

dimethyl sulfoxide + H2O + NAD+

 
  dimethyl sulfone + NADH + H+


Dimethylsulfone reductase is a molybdoprotein.
