Halimadienyl-diphosphate synthase

Halimadienyl-diphosphate synthase (, Rv3377c, halimadienyl diphosphate synthase, tuberculosinol diphosphate synthase, halima-5(6),13-dien-15-yl-diphosphate lyase (cyclizing)) is an enzyme with system name halima-5,13-dien-15-yl-diphosphate lyase (decyclizing).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranylgeranyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons tuberculosinyl diphosphate

This enzyme requires Mg2+ for activity.
