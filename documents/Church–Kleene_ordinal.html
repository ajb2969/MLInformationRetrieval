Church–Kleene ordinal

In mathematics, the Church–Kleene ordinal, ω 1 CK subscript superscript ω CK 1 \omega^{\mathrm{CK}}_{1} , named after Alonzo Church and S. C. Kleene, is a large countable ordinal. It is the set of all recursive ordinals and the smallest non-recursive ordinal. It is also the first ordinal which is not hyperarithmetical, and the first admissible ordinal after ω.
