Oxalate oxidoreductase

Oxalate oxidoreductase () is an enzyme with system name oxalate:ferredoxin oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

oxalate + oxidized ferredoxin 
 
 
 
  2 CO2 + reduced ferredoxin
 

This enzyme contains thiamine diphosphate and [4Fe-4S] clusters.
