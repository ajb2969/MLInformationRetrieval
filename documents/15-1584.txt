   HOSVD-based canonical form of TP functions and qLPV models      HOSVD-based canonical form of TP functions and qLPV models   Based on the key idea of higher-order singular value decomposition  1 (HOSVD) in tensor algebra Baranyi and Yam proposed the concept of HOSVD-based canonical form of TP functions and quasi-LPV system models. 2 3 Szeidl et al. 4 proved that the TP model transformation  5  6 is capable of numerically reconstructing this canonical form.  Related definitions (on TP functions, finite element TP functions, and TP models) can be found here . Details on the control theoretical background (i.e., the TP type polytopic Linear Parameter-Varying state-space model) can be found here .  A free MATLAB implementation of the TP model transformation can be downloaded at 1 or at MATLAB Central 2 .
