TRNAHis guanylyltransferase

TRNAHis guanylyltransferase (, histidine tRNA guanylyltransferase, Thg1p, Thg1) is an enzyme with system name p-tRNAHis:GTP guanylyltransferase (ATP-hydrolysing).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

p-tRNAHis + ATP + GTP 
 
 
 
  pppGp-tRNAHis + AMP + diphosphate (overall reaction)
 
(1a) p-tRNAHis + ATP 
 
 
 
  App-tRNAHis + diphosphate
 
(1b) App-tRNAHis + GTP 
 
 
 
  pppGp-tRNAHis + AMP
 

The enzyme requires a divalent cation for activity.
