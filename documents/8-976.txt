   Immanant of a matrix      Immanant of a matrix    Immanant redirects here; it should not be confused with the philosophical immanent .    In mathematics, the immanant of a matrix was defined by Dudley E. Littlewood and Archibald Read Richardson as a generalisation of the concepts of determinant and permanent .
