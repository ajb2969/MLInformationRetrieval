   Biorthogonal wavelet      Biorthogonal wavelet   A biorthogonal wavelet is a wavelet where the associated wavelet transform is invertible but not necessarily orthogonal . Designing biorthogonal wavelets allows more degrees of freedom than orthogonal wavelets . One additional degree of freedom is the possibility to construct symmetric wavelet functions.
