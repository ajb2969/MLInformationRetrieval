   Marchenko–Pastur distribution      Marchenko–Pastur distribution   In random matrix theory , the Marchenko–Pastur distribution , or Marchenko–Pastur law , describes the asymptotic behavior of singular values of large rectangular random matrices . The theorem is named after Ukrainian  mathematicians  Vladimir Marchenko and Leonid Pastur who proved this result in 1967.
