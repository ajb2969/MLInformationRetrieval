(+)-camphene synthase

(+)-camphene synthase () is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, (+)-camphene-forming).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate

 
  (+)-camphene + diphosphate


Cyclase I of Salvia officinalis (sage) gives about equal parts (+)-camphene and (+)-alpha-pinene.
