FMN reductase (NAD(P)H)

FMN reductase (NAD(P)H) (, FRG) is an enzyme with system name FMNH2:NAD(P)+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

FMNH2 + NAD(P)+

 
  FMN + NAD(P)H + H+


This enzyme contains FMN.
