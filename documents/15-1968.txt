Trans-o-hydroxybenzylidenepyruvate hydratase-aldolase

Trans-o-hydroxybenzylidenepyruvate hydratase-aldolase (, 2'-hydroxybenzalpyruvate aldolase, NsaE, tHBPA hydratase-aldolase) is an enzyme with system name (3E)-4-(2-hydroxyphenyl)-2-oxobut-3-enoate hydro-lyase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3E)-4-(2-hydroxyphenyl)-2-oxobut-3-enoate + H2O 
 
 
salicylaldehyde + pyruvate


This enzyme is involved in naphthalene degradation.
