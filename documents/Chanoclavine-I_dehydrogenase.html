Chanoclavine-I dehydrogenase

Chanoclavine-I dehydrogenase (, easD (gene), fgaDH (gene)) is an enzyme with system name chanoclavine-I:NAD+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalises the following chemical reaction

chanoclavine-I + NAD+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons chanoclavine-I aldehyde + NADH + H+

This enzyme catalyses a step in the pathway of ergot alkaloid biosynthesis in certain fungi.
