Dye decolorizing peroxidase

Dye decolorizing peroxidase (, DyP, DyP-type peroxidase) is an enzyme with system name Reactive-Blue-5:hydrogen-peroxide oxidoreductase.123456789 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

Reactive Blue 5 + H2O2

 
  phthalate + 2,2'-disulfonyl azobenzene + 3-[(4-amino-6-chloro-1,3,5-triazin-2-yl)amino]benzenesulfonate
 

These heme proteins are secreted by basidiomycetous fungi and eubacteria.
