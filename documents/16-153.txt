(2R)-sulfolactate sulfo-lyase

(2R)-sulfolactate sulfo-lyase (, Suy, SuyAB, 3-sulfolactate bisulfite-lyase, sulfolactate sulfo-lyase) is an enzyme with system name (2R)-3-sulfolactate bisulfite-lyase (pyruvate-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2R)-3-sulfolactate 
 
 
pyruvate + bisulfite


This enzyme requires iron(II).
