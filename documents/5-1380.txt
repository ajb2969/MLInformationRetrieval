   Dual pair      Dual pair   In functional analysis and related areas of mathematics a dual pair or dual system is a pair of vector spaces with an associated bilinear map to the base field .  A common method in functional analysis, when studying normed vector spaces , is to analyze the relationship of the space to its continuous dual , the vector space of all possible continuous linear forms on the original space. A dual pair generalizes this concept to arbitrary vector spaces, with the duality being expressed as a bilinear map. Using the bilinear map, semi norms can be constructed to define a polar topology on the vector spaces and turn them into locally convex spaces , generalizations of normed vector spaces.
