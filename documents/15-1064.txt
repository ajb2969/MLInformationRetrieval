Pheophorbide a oxygenase

Pheophorbide A oxygenase (, pheide a monooxygenase, pheide a oxygenase, PAO) is an enzyme with system name pheophorbide-a,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (biladiene-forming).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

Pheophorbide A + NADPH + H+ + O2

 
  red chlorophyll catabolite + NADP+


Pheophorbide A oxygenase participates in chlorophyll degradation.
