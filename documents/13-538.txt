Related topics  One can "globalize" the notion of determinantal varieties by considering the space of linear maps between two vector bundles on an algebraic variety. Then the determinantal varieties fall into the general study of degeneracy loci . An expression for the cohomology class of these degeneracy loci is given by the Thom-Porteous formula , see (Fulton-Pragacz).
