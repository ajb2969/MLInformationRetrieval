   Moore matrix      Moore matrix   In linear algebra , a Moore matrix , introduced by , is a matrix defined over a finite field . When it is a square matrix its determinant is called a Moore determinant (this is unrelated to the Moore determinant of a quaternionic Hermitian matrix ). In particular the Moore determinant vanishes if and only if the elements in the left hand column are linearly dependent over the finite field of order q . So it is analogous to the Wronskian of several functions.  Dickson used the Moore determinant in finding the modular invariants of the general linear group over a finite field.
