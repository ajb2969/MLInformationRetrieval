Inositol-polyphosphate multikinase

Inositol-polyphosphate multikinase (, IpK2, IP3/IP4 6-/3-kinase, IP3/IP4 dual-specificity 6-/3-kinase, IpmK, ArgRIII, AtIpk2alpha, AtIpk2beta, inositol polyphosphate 6-/3-/5-kinase) is an enzyme with system name ATP:1D-myo-inositol-1,4,5-trisphosphate 6-phosphotransferase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 ATP + 1D-myo-inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2 ADP + 1D-myo-inositol 1,3,4,5,6-pentakisphosphate (overall reaction)
(1a) ATP + 1D-myo-inositol 1,4,5-trisphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + 1D-myo-inositol 1,4,5,6-tetrakisphosphate
(1b) ATP + 1D-myo-inositol 1,4,5,6-tetrakisphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + 1D-myo-inositol 1,3,4,5,6-pentakisphosphate

This enzyme also phosphorylates Ins(1,4,5)P3 to Ins(1,3,4,5)P4, Ins(1,3,4,5)P4 to Ins(1,3,4,5,6)P5, and Ins(1,3,4,5,6)P4 to Ins(PP)P4, isomer unknown.
