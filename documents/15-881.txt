Crotonyl-CoA carboxylase/reductase

Crotonyl-CoA carboxylase/reductase (, CCR, crotonyl-CoA reductase (carboxylating)) is an enzyme with system name (2S)-ethylmalonyl-CoA:NADP+ oxidoreductase (decarboxylating).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2S)-ethylmalonyl-CoA + NADP+

 
  (E)-but-2-enoyl-CoA + CO2 + NADPH + H+


The reaction is catalysed in the reverse direction.
