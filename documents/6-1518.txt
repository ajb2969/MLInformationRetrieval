Artin's conjecture  It is a consequence of Chevalley's theorem that finite fields are quasi-algebraically closed . This had been conjectured by Emil Artin in 1935. The motivation behind Artin's conjecture was his observation that quasi-algebraically closed fields have trivial Brauer group , together with the fact that finite fields have trivial Brauer group by Wedderburn's theorem .
