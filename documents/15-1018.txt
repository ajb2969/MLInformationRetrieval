Catalase-peroxidase

Catalase-peroxidase (, katG (gene)) is an enzyme with system name donor:hydrogen-peroxide oxidoreductase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) donor + H2O2

 
  oxidized donor + 2 H2O
 
(2) 2 H2O2

 
  O2 + 2 H2O
 

This enzyme is a strong catalase with H2O2 as donor which releases O2.
