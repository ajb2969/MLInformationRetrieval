1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoyl-CoA hydrolase

1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoyl-CoA hydrolase () is an enzyme with system name 1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoyl-CoA hydrolase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoyl-CoA + H2O 
 
 
 
  1,4-dihydroxy-2-naphthoate + CoA
 

This enzyme participates in the biosynthesis of menaquinones, phyloquinone, and several plant pigments.
