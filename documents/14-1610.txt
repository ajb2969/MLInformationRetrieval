   Duffin–Kemmer–Petiau algebra      Duffin–Kemmer–Petiau algebra   In mathematical physics , the Duffin–Kemmer–Petiau algebra (DKP algebra) , introduced by R.J. Duffin , Nicholas Kemmer and G. Petiau, is the algebra which is generated by the Duffin–Kemmer–Petiau matrices. These matrices form part of the Duffin–Kemmer–Petiau equation that provides a relativistic description of spin-0 and spin-1 particles.
