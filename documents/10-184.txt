   Bochner identity      Bochner identity   In mathematics — specifically, differential geometry — the Bochner identity is an identity concerning harmonic maps between Riemannian manifolds . The identity is named after the American  mathematician  Salomon Bochner .  Statement of the result  Let M and N be Riemannian manifolds and let u : M → N be a harmonic map. Let d u denote the derivative (pushforward) of u , ∇ the gradient , Δ the Laplace–Beltrami operator , Riem N the Riemann curvature tensor on N and Ric M the Ricci curvature tensor on M .
