   Hierarchical RBF      Hierarchical RBF   In computer graphics , a hierarchical RBF is an interpolation method based on Radial basis functions (RBF). Hierarchical RBF interpolation has applications in the construction of shape models in 3D computer graphics (see Stanford Bunny image below), treatment of results from a 3D scanner , terrain reconstruction and others.
