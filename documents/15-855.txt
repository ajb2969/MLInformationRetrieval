Alcohol dehydrogenase (nicotinoprotein)

Alcohol dehydrogenase (nicotinoprotein) (, NDMA-dependent alcohol dehydrogenase, nicotinoprotein alcohol dehydrogenase, np-ADH, ethanol:N,N-dimethyl-4-nitrosoaniline oxidoreductase) is an enzyme with system name ethanol:acceptor oxidoreductase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ethanol + acceptor 
 
 
acetaldehyde + reduced acceptor
 

This enzyme contains Zn2+.
