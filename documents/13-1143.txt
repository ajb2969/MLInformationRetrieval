   Hans Werner Ballmann      Hans Werner Ballmann    Hans Werner Ballmann (known as Werner Ballmann ; born 1951) is a German  mathematician . His area of research is differential geometry with focus on geodesic flows , spaces of negative curvature as well as spectral theory of Dirac operators  Ballmann earned his doctorate from the University of Bonn in 1979, under the supervision of Wilhelm Klingenberg . 1 He currently is a professor at the University of Bonn, and the managing director of the Max Planck Institute for Mathematics in Bonn , Germany , since 2007. 2 He has advised 16 doctoral students at Bonn, including Christian Bär and Anna Wienhard . 3  He is a member of the German Academy of Sciences Leopoldina since 2007, 4 and a member of the scientific committee of the Mathematical Research Institute of Oberwolfach since 2004.
