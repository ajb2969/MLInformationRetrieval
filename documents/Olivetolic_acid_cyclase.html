Olivetolic acid cyclase

Olivetolic acid cyclase (, OAC) is an enzyme with system name 3,5,7-trioxododecanoyl-CoA CoA-lyase (2,4-dihydroxy-6-pentylbenzoate-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

3,5,7-trioxododecanoyl-CoA ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons CoA + 2,4-dihydroxy-6-pentylbenzoate

This enzymes participates in the cannabinoids biosynthesis in the plant Cannabis sativa.
