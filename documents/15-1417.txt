Ribostamycin:4-(gamma-L-glutamylamino)-(S)-2-hydroxybutanoyl-(BtrI acyl-carrier protein) 4-(gamma-L-glutamylamino)-(S)-2-hydroxybutanoate transferase (, btrH (gene)) is an enzyme with system name ribostamycin:4-(gamma-L-glutamylamino)-(S)-2-hydroxybutanoyl-(BtrI acyl-carrier protein) 4-(gamma-L-glutamylamino)-(S)-2-hydroxybutanoate transferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

4-(gamma-L-glutamylamino)-(S)-2-hydroxybutanoyl-[BtrI acyl-carrier protein] + ribostamycin

 
  gamma-L-glutamyl-butirosin B + BtrI acyl-carrier protein
 

The enzyme attaches the side chain of the aminoglycoside antibiotics of the butirosin family.
