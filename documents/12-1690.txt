   Petersson trace formula      Petersson trace formula   In analytic number theory , the Petersson trace formula is a kind of orthogonality relation between coefficients of a holomorphic modular form . It is a specialization of the more general Kuznetsov trace formula .  In its simplest form the Petersson trace formula is as follows.
