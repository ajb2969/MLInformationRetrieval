21S rRNA pseudouridine2819 synthase

21S rRNA pseudouridine2819 synthase (, Pus5p) is an enzyme with system name 21S rRNA-uridine2819 uracil mutase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

21S rRNA uridine2819 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 21S rRNA pseudouridine2819

The enzyme specifically acts on uridine2819 in 21S rRNA.
