The intersection of a ray of light with each plane is used to produce an image of the surface. In vision-based 3D reconstruction, a subfield of computer vision, depth values are commonly measured by so-called triangulation method, which finds the intersection between light plane and ray reflected toward camera.  The algorithm can be generalised to cover intersection with other planar figures, in particular, the intersection of a polyhedron with a line .
