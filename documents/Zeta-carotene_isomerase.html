Zeta-carotene isomerase

Zeta-carotene isomerase (, Z-ISO, 15-cis-zeta-carotene isomerase) is an enzyme with system name 9,15,9'-tricis-zeta-carotene cis-trans-isomerase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

9,15,9'-tricis-zeta-carotene ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 9,9'-dicis-zeta-carotene

This enzyme is involved in carotenoid biosynthesis.
