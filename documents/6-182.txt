   Gegenbauer polynomials      Gegenbauer polynomials   In mathematics , Gegenbauer polynomials or ultraspherical polynomials  C ( x ) are orthogonal polynomials on the interval [−1,1] with respect to the weight function (1 − x 2 ) α –1/2 . They generalize Legendre polynomials and Chebyshev polynomials , and are special cases of Jacobi polynomials . They are named after Leopold Gegenbauer .
