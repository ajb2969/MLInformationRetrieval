   Elongated pentagonal orthobicupola      Elongated pentagonal orthobicupola   In geometry , the elongated pentagonal orthobicupola is one of the Johnson solids ( J 38 ). 1 As the name suggests, it can be constructed by elongating a pentagonal orthobicupola ( J 30 ) by inserting a decagonal prism between its two congruent halves. Rotating one of the cupolae through 36 degrees before inserting the prism yields an elongated pentagonal gyrobicupola ( J 39 ).
