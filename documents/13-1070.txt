It is a generalization of the two-parameter gamma distribution . Since many distributions commonly used for parametric models in survival analysis (such as the Weibull distribution and the log-normal distribution ) are special cases of the generalized gamma, it is sometimes used to determine which parametric model is appropriate for a given set of data. 1  Characteristics  The generalized gamma has three parameters    a  >  0      a  0    a>0   ,    d  >  0      d  0    d>0   , and    p  >  0      p  0    p>0   .
