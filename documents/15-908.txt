   4,4'-Diapophytoene desaturase      4,4'-Diapophytoene desaturase   4,4'-Diapophytoene desaturase (, dehydrosqualene desaturase , CrtN , 4,4'-diapophytoene:FAD oxidoreductase ) is an enzyme with system name 15-cis-4,4'-diapophytoene:FAD oxidoreductase . 1 2 3 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   15-cis-4,4'-diapophytoene + 4 FAD   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   all-trans-4,4'-diapolycopene + 4 FADH2 (overall reaction)  (1a) 15-cis-4,4'-diapophytoene + FAD   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   all-trans-4,4'-diapophytofluene + FADH2  (1b) all-trans-4,4'-diapophytofluene + FAD   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   all-trans-4,4'-diapo-zeta-carotene + FADH2  (1c) all-trans-4,4'-diapo-zeta-carotene + FAD   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   all-trans-4,4'-diaponeurosporene + FADH2  (1d) all-trans-4,4'-diaponeurosporene + FAD   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   all-trans-4,4'-diapolycopene + FADH2   This enzyme is typical of Staphylococcus aureus and some other bacteria such as Heliobacillus sp .
