Ent-isokaurene synthase

Ent-isokaurene synthase (, OsKSL5i, OsKSL6) is an enzyme with system name ent-copalyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, ent-isokaurene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ent-copalyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ent-isokaurene + diphosphate

Two enzymes of the rice sub-species Oryza sativa ssp. indica, OsKSL5 and OsKSL6, produce ent-isokaurene.
