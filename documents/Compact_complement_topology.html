Compact complement topology

In mathematics, the compact complement topology is a topology defined on the set ℝ ℝ \scriptstyle\mathbb{R} of real numbers, defined by declaring a subset X ⊆ ℝ X ℝ \scriptstyle X\subseteq\mathbb{R} open if and only if it is either empty or its complement ℝ ∖ X ℝ X \scriptstyle\mathbb{R}\setminus X is compact in the standard Euclidean topology on ℝ ℝ \scriptstyle\mathbb{R} .
