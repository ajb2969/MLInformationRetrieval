Phenylalanine/tyrosine ammonia-lyase

Phenylalanine/tyrosine ammonia-lyase (, PTAL, bifunctional PAL) is an enzyme with system name L-phenylalanine(or L-tyrosine):trans-cinnamate(or trans-p-hydroxycinnamate) ammonia-lyase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) L-phenylalanine

trans-cinnamate + NH3

(2) L-tyrosine

 
  trans-p-hydroxycinnamate + NH3


This enzyme is a member of the aromatic amino acid lyase family.
