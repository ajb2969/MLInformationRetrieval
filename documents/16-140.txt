   Phenylalanine/tyrosine ammonia-lyase      Phenylalanine/tyrosine ammonia-lyase   Phenylalanine/tyrosine ammonia-lyase (, PTAL , bifunctional PAL ) is an enzyme with system name L-phenylalanine(or L-tyrosine):trans-cinnamate(or trans-p-hydroxycinnamate) ammonia-lyase . 1 2 3 4 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) L- phenylalanine    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    trans-cinnamate + NH 3   (2) L- tyrosine    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   trans-p-hydroxycinnamate + NH 3    This enzyme is a member of the aromatic amino acid lyase family.
