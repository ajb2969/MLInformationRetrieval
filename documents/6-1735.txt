   Fredholm alternative      Fredholm alternative   In mathematics , the Fredholm alternative , named after Ivar Fredholm , is one of Fredholm's theorems and is a result in Fredholm theory . It may be expressed in several ways, as a theorem of linear algebra , a theorem of integral equations , or as a theorem on Fredholm operators . Part of the result states that a non-zero complex number in the spectrum of a compact operator is an eigenvalue.
