Optimal level and weight  The strong form of Serre's conjecture describes the level and weight of the modular form.  The optimal level is the Artin conductor of the representation, with the power of l removed.  Proof  A proof of the level 1 and small weight cases of the conjecture was obtained during 2004 by Chandrashekhar Khare and Jean-Pierre Wintenberger , 3 and by Luis Dieulefait , 4 independently.
