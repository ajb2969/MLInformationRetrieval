Cubebol synthase

Cubebol synthase (, Cop4) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, cubebol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
 
  cubebol + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+.
