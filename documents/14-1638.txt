   Esscher transform      Esscher transform   In actuarial science , the Esscher transform is a transform that takes a probability density  f ( x ) and transforms it to a new probability density f ( x ; h ) with a parameter h . It was introduced by F. Esscher in 1932 .  Definition  Let f ( x ) be a probability density.
