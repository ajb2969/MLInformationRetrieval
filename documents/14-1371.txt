   Sieved ultraspherical polynomials      Sieved ultraspherical polynomials   In mathematics, the two families c ( x ; k ) and B ( x ; k ) of sieved ultraspherical polynomials , introduced by Waleed Al-Salam , W.R. Allaway and Richard Askey in 1984, are the archetypal examples of sieved orthogonal polynomials . Their recurrence relations are a modified (or "sieved") version of the recurrence relations for ultraspherical polynomials .
