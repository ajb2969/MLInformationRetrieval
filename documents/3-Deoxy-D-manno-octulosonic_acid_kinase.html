3-Deoxy-D-manno-octulosonic acid kinase

3-deoxy-D-manno-octulosonic acid kinase (, kdkA (gene), Kdo kinase) is an enzyme with system name ATP:(KDO)-lipid IVA 3-deoxy-alpha-D-manno-oct-2-ulopyranose 4-phosphotransferase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

alpha-Kdo-(2->6)-lipid IVA + ATP ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 4-O-phospho-alpha-Kdo-(2->6)-lipid IVA + ADP

The enzyme phosphorylates the 4-OH position of KDO in (KDO)-lipid IVA.
