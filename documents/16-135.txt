Cis-abienol synthase

Cis-abienol synthase (, Z-abienol synthase, CAS, ABS) is an enzyme with system name (13E)-8alpha-hydroxylabd-13-en-15-yl-diphosphate-lyase (cis-abienol forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(13E)-8alpha-hydroxylabd-13-en-15-yl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  cis-abienol + diphosphate


This enzyme is isolated from the plants Abies balsamea (balsam fir) and Nicotiana tabacum (tobacco).
