5-Exo-hydroxycamphor dehydrogenase

5-exo-hydroxycamphor dehydrogenase (, F-dehydrogenase, FdeH) is an enzyme with system name 5-exo-hydroxycamphor:NAD+ oxidoreductase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

5-exo-hydroxycamphor + NAD+ $\rightleftharpoons$ bornane-2,5-dione + NADH + H+

This enzyme contains Zn2+. It is isolated from Pseudomonas putida.
