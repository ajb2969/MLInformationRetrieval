   Pyrrole-2-carboxylate decarboxylase      Pyrrole-2-carboxylate decarboxylase   Pyrrole-2-carboxylate decarboxylase () is an enzyme with system name pyrrole-2-carboxylate carboxy-lyase . 1 2 3 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) pyrrole-2-carboxylate   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons    pyrrole + CO 2   (2) pyrrole-2-carboxylate + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   pyrrole + HCO 3 −    The enzyme catalyses both the carboxylation and decarboxylation reactions.
