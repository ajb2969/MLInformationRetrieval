Pyrrole-2-carboxylate decarboxylase

Pyrrole-2-carboxylate decarboxylase () is an enzyme with system name pyrrole-2-carboxylate carboxy-lyase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) pyrrole-2-carboxylate 
 
 
pyrrole + CO2

(2) pyrrole-2-carboxylate + H2O 
 
 
 
  pyrrole + HCO3−


The enzyme catalyses both the carboxylation and decarboxylation reactions.
