Aminopyrimidine aminohydrolase

Aminopyrimidine aminohydrolase (, thiaminase, thiaminase II, tenA (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 4-amino-5-aminomethyl-2-methylpyrimidine aminohydrolase.1234567 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) 4-amino-5-aminomethyl-2-methylpyrimidine + H2O 
 
 
 
  4-amino-5-hydroxymethyl-2-methylpyrimidine + ammonia

(2) thiamine + H2O 
 
 
 
  4-amino-5-hydroxymethyl-2-methylpyrimidine + 5-(2-hydroxyethyl)-4-methylthiazole
 

This enzyme was previously known as thiaminase II.
