   Template:Oiint/doc      Template:Oiint/doc   This template is used to include the double closed integral, usually in a mathematical formula. It overcomes a limitation of the TeX rendering which cannot display this symbol without clumsy workarounds.  Arguments   preintegral the text or formula immediately before the integral  intsubscpt the subscript below the integral  integrand the text or formula immediately after the formula   All parameters are optional.
