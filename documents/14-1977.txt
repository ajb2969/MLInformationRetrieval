   Eckert IV projection      Eckert IV projection   (Figure)  Eckert IV projection of the world.   The Eckert IV projection is an equal-area  pseudocylindrical map projection . The length of polar line is half that of the equator, and lines of longitude are semiellipses, or portions of ellipses .
