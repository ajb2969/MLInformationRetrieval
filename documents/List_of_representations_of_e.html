List of representations of e

The mathematical constant e e e can be represented in a variety of ways as a real number. Since e e e is an irrational number (see proof that e is irrational), it cannot be represented as a fraction, but it can be represented as a continued fraction. Using calculus, e e e may also be represented as an infinite series, infinite product, or other sort of limit of a sequence.
