TRNA-dihydrouridine20 synthase (NAD(P)+)

TRNA-dihydrouridine20 synthase (NAD(P)+) (, Dus2p, tRNA-dihydrouridine synthase 2) is an enzyme with system name tRNA-5,6-dihydrouracil20:NAD(P)+ oxidoreductase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

5,6-dihydrouracil20 in tRNA + NAD(P)+

 
  uracil20 in tRNA + NAD(P)H + H+


This enzyme specifically modifies uracil20 in tRNA.
