Otherwise, when B is larger, the frequency resolution is better.  We can choose specified B to decide time resolution and frequency resolution.  Advantage and disadvantage   Compare with the Fourier transform   Advantage The instantaneous frequency can be observed.  Disadvantage Higher complexity of computation.   Compared with other types of time-frequency analysis:   The rec-STFT has an advantage of the least computation time for digital implementation, but its performance is worse than other types of time-frequency analysis.
