Neoabietadiene synthase

Neoabietadiene synthase (, AgAS, PtTPS-LAS) is an enzyme with system name (+)-copaly-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, neoabietadiene-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(+)-copalyl diphosphate 
 
 
neoabietadiene + diphosphate


This enzyme is isolated from Abies grandis (grand fir).
