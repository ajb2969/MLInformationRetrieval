   Komlós–Major–Tusnády approximation      Komlós–Major–Tusnády approximation   In theory of probability , the Komlós–Major–Tusnády approximation (also known as the KMT approximation , the KMT embedding , or the Hungarian embedding ) is an approximation of the empirical process by a Gaussian process constructed on the same probability space . It is named after Hungarian mathematicians János Komlós , Gábor Tusnády , and Péter Major .
