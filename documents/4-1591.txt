   Polyakov action      Polyakov action   In physics , the Polyakov action is the two-dimensional action of a conformal field theory describing the worldsheet of a string in string theory . It was introduced by Stanley Deser and Bruno Zumino and independently by L. Brink, P. Di Vecchia and P. S. Howe (in Physics Letters B65, pages 369 and 471 respectively), and has become associated with Alexander Polyakov after he made use of it in quantizing the string.
