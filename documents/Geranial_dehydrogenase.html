Geranial dehydrogenase

Geranial dehydrogenase (, GaDH, geoB (gene)) is an enzyme with system name geranial:NAD+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranial + H2O + NAD+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons geranate + NADH + H+

Does not act on neral.
