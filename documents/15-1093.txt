Limonene 1,2-monooxygenase () is an enzyme with system name limonene,NAD(P)H:oxygen oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) (S)-limonene + NAD(P)H + H+ + O2

 
  1,2-epoxymenth-8-ene + NAD(P)+ + H2O
 
(2) (R)-limonene + NAD(P)H + H+ + O2
