3-dehydroshikimate dehydratase

3-dehydroshikimate dehydratase () is an enzyme with system name 3-dehydroshikimate hydro-lyase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

3-dehydro-shikimate 
 
 
 
  protocatechuate + H2O
 

This enzyme catalyses an early step in the biosynthesis of petrobactin.
