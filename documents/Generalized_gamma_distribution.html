It is a generalization of the two-parameter gamma distribution. Since many distributions commonly used for parametric models in survival analysis (such as the Weibull distribution and the log-normal distribution) are special cases of the generalized gamma, it is sometimes used to determine which parametric model is appropriate for a given set of data.1
