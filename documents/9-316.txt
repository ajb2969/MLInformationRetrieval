   Kautz filter      Kautz filter   In signal processing, the Kautz filter , named after William H. Kautz, is a fixed- pole traversal filter , published in 1954. 1 2  Like Laguerre filters , Kautz filters can be implemented using a cascade of all-pass filters , with a one-pole lowpass filter at each tap between the all-pass sections.
