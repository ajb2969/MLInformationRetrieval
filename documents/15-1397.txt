Acetoacetyl-CoA synthase

Acetoacetyl-CoA synthase (, NphT7) is an enzyme with system name acetyl-CoA:malonyl-CoA C-acetyltransferase (decarboxylating).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

acetyl-CoA + malonyl-CoA

acetoacetyl-CoA + CoA + CO2


The enzyme from the soil bacterium Streptomyces sp. CL190 produces acetoacetyl-CoA.
