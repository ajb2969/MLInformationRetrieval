Characterizations  Nuclearity admits the following equivalent characterizations:   A C*-algebra is nuclear if the identity map, as a completely positive map , approximately factors through matrix algebras. Abelian C*-algebras are nuclear. One might say that nuclearity means that the C*-algebra admits noncommutative " partitions of unity ."  A C*-algebra is nuclear if and only if its enveloping von Neumann algebra is injective.
