Remark

The rationale behind twisting arguments does not change when parameters are vectors, though some complication arises from the management of joint inequalities. Instead, the difficulty of dealing with a vector of parameters proved to be the Achilles heel of Fisher's approach to the fiducial distribution of parameters . Also Fraser‚Äôs constructive probabilities devised for the same purpose do not treat this point completely.
