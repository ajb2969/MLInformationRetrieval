   Burgers' equation      Burgers' equation   Burgers' equation is a fundamental partial differential equation occuring in various areas of applied mathematics , such as fluid mechanics , 1  nonlinear acoustics , 2  gas dynamics , traffic flow . It is named for Johannes Martinus Burgers (1895–1981). If the characteristics do intersect, then a classical solution to the PDE does not exist.
