   Pentachlorophenol monooxygenase      Pentachlorophenol monooxygenase   Pentachlorophenol monooxygenase (, pentachlorophenol dechlorinase , pentachlorophenol dehalogenase , pentachlorophenol 4-monooxygenase , PCP hydroxylase , pentachlorophenol hydroxylase , PcpB , PCB 4-monooxygenase , PCB4MO ) is an enzyme with system name pentachlorophenol,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (hydroxylating, dechlorinating) . 1 2 3 4 5 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) pentachlorophenol + 2 NADPH + H + + O 2   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   2,3,5,6-tetrachlorohydroquinone + 2 NADP + + chloride + H 2 O   (2) 2,3,5,6-tetrachlorophenol + NADPH + H + + O2   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   2,3,5,6-tetrachlorohydroquinone + NADP + + H 2 O   Pentachlorophenol monooxygenase is a flavoprotein (FAD).
