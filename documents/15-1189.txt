Iron:rusticyanin reductase

Iron:rusticyanin reductase (, Cyc2) is an enzyme with system name Fe(II):rusticyanin oxidoreductase.1234567 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

Fe(II) + rusticyanin

 
  Fe(III) + reduced rusticyanin
 

Rusticyanin reductase contains c-type heme.
