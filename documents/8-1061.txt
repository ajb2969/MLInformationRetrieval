   Wrapping (graphics)      Wrapping (graphics)   In computer graphics, wrapping is the process of limiting a position to an area. A good example of wrapping is wallpaper , a single pattern repeated indefinitely over a wall . Wrapping is used in 3D computer graphics to repeat a texture over a polygon , eliminating the need for large textures or multiple polygons.
