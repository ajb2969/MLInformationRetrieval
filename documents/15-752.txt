   Affine term structure model      Affine term structure model   An affine term structure model is a financial model that relates zero-coupon bond prices (i.e. the discount curve) to a spot rate model. It is particularly useful for inverting the yield curve – the process of determining spot rate model inputs from observable bond market data.
