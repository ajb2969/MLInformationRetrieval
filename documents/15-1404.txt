UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-glucuronate N-acetyltransferase

UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-glucuronate N-acetyltransferase (, WbpD, WlbB) is an enzyme with system name acetyl-CoA:UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucuronate N-acetyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

acetyl-CoA + UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucuronate 
 
 
 
  CoA + UDP-2,3-diacetamido-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucuronate
 

This enzyme participates in the biosynthetic pathway for UDP-alpha-D-ManNAc3NAcA (UDP-2,3-diacetamido-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-mannuronic acid).
