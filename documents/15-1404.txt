   UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-glucuronate N-acetyltransferase      UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-glucuronate N-acetyltransferase   UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-glucuronate N-acetyltransferase (, WbpD , WlbB ) is an enzyme with system name acetyl-CoA:UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucuronate N-acetyltransferase . 1 2 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   acetyl-CoA + UDP-2-acetamido-3-amino-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucuronate   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   CoA + UDP-2,3-diacetamido-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucuronate   This enzyme participates in the biosynthetic pathway for UDP-alpha-D-ManNAc3NAcA (UDP-2,3-diacetamido-2,3-dideoxy-alpha-D-mannuronic acid).
