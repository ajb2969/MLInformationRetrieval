   Trudinger's theorem      Trudinger's theorem   In mathematical analysis , Trudinger's theorem or the Trudinger inequality (also sometimes called the Moser–Trudinger inequality ) is a result of functional analysis on Sobolev spaces . It is named after Neil Trudinger (and Jürgen Moser ).  It provides an inequality between a certain Sobolev space norm and an Orlicz space norm of a function.
