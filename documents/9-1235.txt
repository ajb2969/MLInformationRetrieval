Dynein ATPase

Dynein ATPase (, dynein adenosine 5'-triphosphatase) is an enzyme with system name ATP phosphohydrolase (tubulin-translocating).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + H2O 
 
 
 
  ADP + phosphate


This enzyme is a multisubunit protein complex associated with microtubules.
