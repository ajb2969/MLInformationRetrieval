UDP-GlcNAc:ribostamycin N-acetylglucosaminyltransferase

UDP-GlcNAc:ribostamycin N-acetylglucosaminyltransferase (, neoK (gene)) is an enzyme with system name UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosamine:ribostamycin N-acetylglucosaminyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosamine + ribostamycin

 
  UDP + 2'''-acetyl-6'''-hydroxyneomycin C
 

Involved in biosynthesis of the aminoglycoside antibiotic neomycin.
