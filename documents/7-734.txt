   Time dependent vector field      Time dependent vector field   In mathematics , a time dependent vector field is a construction in vector calculus which generalizes the concept of vector fields . It can be thought of as a vector field which moves as time passes. For every instant of time, it associates a vector to every point in a Euclidean space or in a manifold .
