   7₁ knot      7₁ knot   In knot theory , the 7 1 knot , also known as the septoil knot , the septafoil knot , or the (7, 2)-torus knot , is one of seven prime knots with crossing number seven. It is the simplest torus knot after the trefoil and cinquefoil .  The 7 1 knot is invertible but not amphichiral .
