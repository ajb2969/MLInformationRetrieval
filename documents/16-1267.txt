   Lambda2 method      Lambda2 method   The Lambda2 method , or Lambda2 vortex criterion , is a detection algorithm that can adequately identify vortices from a three-dimensional velocity field . 1 The Lambda2 method is Galilean invariant , which means it produces the same results when a uniform velocity field is added to the existing velocity field or when the field is translated .  Definition  The Lambda2 method consists of several steps.
