Ubiquinol oxidase (H+-transporting)

Ubiquinol oxidase (H+-transporting) (, cytochrome bb3 oxidase, cytochrome bo oxidase, cytochrome bd-I oxidase) is an enzyme with system name ubiquinol:O2 oxidoreductase (H+-transporting).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 ubiquinol + O2 + n H+in 
 
 
 
  2 ubiquinone + 2 H2O + n H+out
 

Ubiquinol oxidase contains a dinuclear centre comprising two hemes, or heme and copper.
