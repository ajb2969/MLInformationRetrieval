   Lozanić's triangle      Lozanić's triangle   Lozanić's triangle (sometimes called Losanitsch's triangle ) is a triangular array of binomial coefficients in a manner very similar to that of Pascal's triangle . It is named after the Serbian chemist Sima Lozanić , who researched it in his investigation into the symmetries exhibited by rows of paraffins (archaic term for alkanes ).
