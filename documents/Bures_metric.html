Bures metric

In mathematics, in the area of quantum information geometry, the Bures metric1 or Helstrom metric2 defines an infinitesimal distance between density matrix operators defining quantum states. It is a quantum generalization of the Fisher information metric, and is identical to the Fubini‚ÄìStudy metric3 when restricted to the pure states alone.
