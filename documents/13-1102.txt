   Complex normal distribution      Complex normal distribution   In probability theory , the family of complex normal distributions characterizes complex random variables whose real and imaginary parts are jointly normal . 1 The complex normal family has three parameters: location parameter μ , covariance matrix Γ, and the relation matrix C . The standard complex normal is the univariate distribution with μ = 0, Γ = 1, and C = 0.
