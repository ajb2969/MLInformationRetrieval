(+)-alpha-pinene synthase

(+)-alpha-pinene synthase (, (+)-alpha-pinene cyclase, cyclase I) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, (+)-alpha-pinene-forming).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate

 
  (+)-alpha-pinene + diphosphate


Cyclase I of Salvia officinalis (sage) gives about equal parts (+)-alpha-pinene and (+)-camphene.
