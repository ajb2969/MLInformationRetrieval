   Mingarelli identity      Mingarelli identity   In the field of ordinary differential equations , the Mingarelli identity (coined by Philip Hartman 1 ) is a theorem that provides criteria for the oscillation and non-oscillation of solutions of some linear differential equations in the real domain. It extends the Picone identity from two to three or more differential equations of the second order. Its most basic form appears here.
