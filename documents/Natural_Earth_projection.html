Natural Earth projection

The Natural Earth projection is a pseudocylindrical map projection designed by Tom Patterson. It is neither conformal nor equal-area.

Definition

The Natural Earth is defined by the following formulas:

x = 0.8707 × l ( φ ) × λ x 0.8707 l φ λ x=0.8707\times l(\varphi)\times\lambda ,

y = 0.8707 × 0.52 × d ( φ ) × π y 0.8707 0.52 d φ π y=0.8707\times 0.52\times d(\varphi)\times\pi ,

where

  • x x x , y y y are the Cartesian coordinates;
  • λ λ \lambda is the longitude from the central meridian;
  • φ φ \varphi is the latitude;
  • l ( φ ) l φ l(\varphi) is the length of the parallel at latitude φ φ \varphi ;
  • d ( φ ) d φ d(\varphi) is the distance of the parallel from the equator at latitude φ φ \varphi .
