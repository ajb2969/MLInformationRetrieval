7-Methylxanthine demethylase

7-Methylxanthine demethylase () is an enzyme with system name 7-methylxanthine:oxygen oxidoreductase (demethylating).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

7-methylxanthine + O2 + NAD(P)H + H+

 
  xanthine + NAD(P)+ + H2O + formaldehyde


7-Methylxanthine demethylase is a non-heme iron oxygenase.
