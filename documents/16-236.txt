Baccharis oxide synthase

Baccharis oxide synthase () is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, baccharis-oxide-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene 
 
 
 
  baccharis oxide
 

The enzyme from Stevia rebaudiana also gives traces of other triterpenoids.
