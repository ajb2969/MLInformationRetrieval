   Fibonacci polynomials      Fibonacci polynomials   In mathematics , the Fibonacci polynomials are a polynomial sequence which can be considered as a generalization of the Fibonacci numbers . The polynomials generated in a similar way from the Lucas numbers are called Lucas polynomials .
