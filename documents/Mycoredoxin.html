Mycoredoxin

Mycoredoxin (, Mrx1, MrxI) is an enzyme with system name arseno-mycothiol:mycoredoxin oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

arseno-mycothiol + mycoredoxin ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons arsenite + mycothiol-mycoredoxin disulfide

Reduction of arsenate is part of a defense mechanism of the cell against toxic arsenate.
