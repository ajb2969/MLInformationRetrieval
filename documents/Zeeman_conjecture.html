Zeeman conjecture

In mathematics, the Zeeman conjecture or Zeeman's collapsibility conjecture asks whether given a finite contractible 2-complex K K K the space K × [ 0 , 1 ] K 0 1 K\times[0,1] is collapsible.

The conjecture implies the Poincaré conjecture and the Andrews–Curtis conjecture.
