   Strength of a graph      Strength of a graph   In the branch of mathematics called graph theory , the strength of an undirected graph corresponds to the minimum ratio edges removed / components created in a decomposition of the graph in question. It is a method to compute partitions of the set of vertices and detect zones of high concentration of edges.
