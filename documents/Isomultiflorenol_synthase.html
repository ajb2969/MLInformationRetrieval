Isomultiflorenol synthase

Isomultiflorenol synthase (, LcIMS1, (S)-2,3-epoxysqualene mutase (cyclizing, isomultiflorenol-forming)) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualenee mutase (cyclizing, isomultiflorenol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons isomultiflorenol
