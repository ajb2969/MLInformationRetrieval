   Crocco's theorem      Crocco's theorem   Crocco's theorem is a fluid dynamics theorem relating the flow velocity , vorticity , and stagnation pressure (or entropy ) of a potential flow . Crocco's theorem gives the relation between the thermodynamics and fluid kinematics.
