Skoda–El Mir theorem

The Skoda–El Mir theorem is a theorem of complex geometry, stated as follows:

Theorem (Skoda,1 El Mir,2 Sibony 3). Let X be a complex manifold, and E a closed complete pluripolar set in X. Consider a closed positive current Θ normal-Θ \Theta on X \ E normal-\ X E X\backslash E which is locally integrable around E. Then the trivial extension of Θ normal-Θ \Theta to X is closed on X.
