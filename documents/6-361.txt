   Poisson kernel      Poisson kernel   In potential theory , the Poisson kernel is an integral kernel , used for solving the two-dimensional Laplace equation , given Dirichlet boundary conditions on the unit disc . The kernel can be understood as the derivative of the Green's function for the Laplace equation. It is named for SimÃ©on Poisson .  Poisson kernels commonly find applications in control theory and two-dimensional problems in electrostatics . In practice, the definition of Poisson kernels are often extended to n -dimensional problems.
