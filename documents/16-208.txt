TRNA pseudouridine32 synthase

TRNA pseudouridine32 synthase (, RluA, pseudouridine synthase RluA, Pus9p, Rib2/Pus8p) is an enzyme with system name tRNA-uridine32 uracil mutase.123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

tRNA uridine32

 
  tRNA pseudouridine32


The dual enzyme from Escherichia coli also catalyses the formation of pseudouridine746 in 23S rRNA.
