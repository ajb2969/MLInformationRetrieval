Cyclic alcohol dehydrogenase (quinone)

Cyclic alcohol dehydrogenase (quinone) (, cyclic alcohol dehydrogenase, MCAD) is an enzyme with system name cyclic alcohol:quinone oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

cyclic alcohol + quinone

 
  cyclic ketone + quinol


This enzyme oxidizes a wide variety of cyclic alcohols.
