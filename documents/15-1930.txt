2,6-dihydroxypseudooxynicotine hydrolase

2,6-dihydroxypseudooxynicotine hydrolase () is an enzyme with system name 1-(2,6-dihydroxypyridin-3-yl)-4-(methylamino)butan-1-one hydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

1-(2,6-dihydroxypyridin-3-yl)-4-(methylamino)butan-1-one + H2O 
 
 
 
  2,6-dihydroxypyridine + 4-methylaminobutanoate
 

The enzyme is present in the soil bacterium Arthrobacter nicotinovorans.
