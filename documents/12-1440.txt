Description  The codex contains almost complete text of the Acts of the Apostles and the Epistles on 282 parchment leaves ({{Ã—|18|12.6}}), with some lacunae (Acts 4:8-7:17; 17:28-23:9 - 297 verses). Lectionary markings were added on the margin by a later hand (for liturgical use). 4 5  Text  The Greek text of the codex, is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type , with some the Byzantine readings.
