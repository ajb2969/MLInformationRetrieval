   Dirichlet beta function      Dirichlet beta function   In mathematics , the Dirichlet beta function (also known as the Catalan beta function ) is a special function , closely related to the Riemann zeta function . It is a particular Dirichlet L-function , the L-function for the alternating character of period four.
