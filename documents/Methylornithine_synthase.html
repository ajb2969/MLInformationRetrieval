Methylornithine synthase

Methylornithine synthase (, PylB) is an enzyme with system name L-lysine carboxy-aminomethylmutase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

L-lysine ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (3R)-3-methyl-D-ornithine

The enzyme is a member of the superfamily of S-adenosyl-L-methionine-dependent radical enzymes.
