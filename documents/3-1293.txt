   Shooting method      Shooting method   In numerical analysis , the shooting method is a method for solving a boundary value problem by reducing it to the solution of an initial value problem . The following exposition may be clarified by this illustration of the shooting method .  For a boundary value problem of a second-order ordinary differential equation , the method is stated as follows.
