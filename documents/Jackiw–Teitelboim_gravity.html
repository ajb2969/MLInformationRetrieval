Jackiw–Teitelboim gravity

The R=T model,1 also known as Jackiw–Teitelboim gravity is a theory of gravity with dilaton coupling in one spatial and one time dimension. It should not be confused23 with the CGHS model or Liouville gravity. The action is given by
