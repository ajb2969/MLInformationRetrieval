4-Methylaminobutanoate oxidase (formaldehyde-forming)

4-methylaminobutanoate oxidase (formaldehyde-forming) (, mabO (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 4-methylaminobutanoate:oxygen oxidoreductase (formaldehyde-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

4-methylaminobutanoate + O2 + H2O 
 
 
 
  4-aminobutanoate + formaldehyde + H2O2


This enzyme is a flavoprotein (FAD).
