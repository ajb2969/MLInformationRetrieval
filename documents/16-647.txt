   Rubidium hydrogen sulfate      Rubidium hydrogen sulfate   Rubidium hydrogen sulfate is the rubidium  salt of sulfuric acid . It has the formula RbHSO 4 .  Synthesis  It may be synthesised with water and a stoichiometric amount of rubidium disulfate .
