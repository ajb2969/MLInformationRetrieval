9-cis-beta-carotene 9',10'-cleaving dioxygenase

9-cis-beta-carotene 9',10'-cleaving dioxygenase (, CCD7 (gene), MAX3 (gene), NCED7 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 9-cis-beta-carotene:O2 oxidoreductase (9',10'-cleaving).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

9-cis-beta-carotene + O2

 
  9-cis-10'-apo-beta-carotenal + beta-ionone
 

9-cis-beta-carotene 9',10'-cleaving dioxygenase contains Fe2+.
