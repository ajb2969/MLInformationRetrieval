Eugenol synthase

Eugenol synthase (, LtCES1, EGS1, EGS2) is an enzyme with system name eugenol:NADP+ oxidoreductase (coniferyl ester reducing).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction: eugenol + a carboxylate + NADP+

 
  a coniferyl ester + NADPH + H+
The enzyme acts in the reverse direction.
