   Axiality and rhombicity      Axiality and rhombicity   In physics and mathematics , axiality and rhombicity are two characteristics of a symmetric second-rank tensor in three-dimensional Euclidean space , describing its directional asymmetry.  Let A denote a second-rank tensor in R 3 , which can be represented by a 3-by-3 matrix . We assume that A is symmetric.
