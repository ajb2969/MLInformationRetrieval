Purpose
Convert redshift in values of km/s (ie kps) (kilometres per second) to light travel distance in terms of Mly (millions of light years)
Usage


(MANDATORY) value of redshift in terms of units in kilometers per second

(OPTIONAL) adds Mly to the display

(OPTIONAL) define the Hubble Constant (H0) in terms of kps/Mly if you do not wish to use the default of 20kps/Mly
