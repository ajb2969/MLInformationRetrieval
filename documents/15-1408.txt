Fumigaclavine B O-acetyltransferase

Fumigaclavine B O-acetyltransferase (, FgaAT) is an enzyme with system name acetyl-CoA:fumigaclavine B O-acetyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

acetyl-CoA + fumigaclavine B

 
  CoA + fumigaclavine A


The enzyme participates in the biosynthesis of fumigaclavine C, an ergot alkaloid.
