TRNA pseudouridine31 synthase

TRNA pseudouridine31 synthase (, Pus6p) is an enzyme with system name tRNA-uridine31 uracil mutase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

tRNA uridine31 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons tRNA pseudouridine31

The enzyme specifically acts on uridine31 in tRNA.
