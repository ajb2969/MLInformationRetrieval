D-threonine aldolase

D-threonine aldolase (, D-TA, DTA, low specificity D-TA, low specificity D-threonine aldolase) is an enzyme with system name D-threonine acetaldehyde-lyase (glycine-forming).123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) D-threonine ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons glycine + acetaldehyde
(2) D-allothreonine ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons glycine + acetaldehyde

This pyridoxal-phosphate protein is activated by divalent metal cations (e.g. Co2+, Ni2+, Mn2+ or Mg2+).
