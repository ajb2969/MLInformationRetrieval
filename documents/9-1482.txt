   Brascamp–Lieb inequality      Brascamp–Lieb inequality   In mathematics , the Brascamp–Lieb inequality is a result in geometry concerning integrable functions on n - dimensional  Euclidean space  R n . It generalizes the Loomis–Whitney inequality and Hölder's inequality , and is named after Herm Jan Brascamp and Elliott H. Lieb .
