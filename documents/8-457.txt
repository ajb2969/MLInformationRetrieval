Phosphoenolpyruvate carboxykinase (ATP)

Phosphoenolpyruvate carboxykinase (ATP) (, phosphopyruvate carboxylase (ATP), phosphoenolpyruvate carboxylase, phosphoenolpyruvate carboxykinase, phosphopyruvate carboxykinase (adenosine triphosphate), PEP carboxylase, PEP carboxykinase, PEPCK (ATP), PEPK, PEPCK, phosphoenolpyruvic carboxylase, phosphoenolpyruvic carboxykinase, phosphoenolpyruvate carboxylase (ATP), phosphopyruvate carboxykinase, ATP:oxaloacetate carboxy-lyase (transphosphorylating)) is an enzyme with system name ATP:oxaloacetate carboxy-lyase (transphosphorylating; phosphoenolpyruvate-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + oxaloacetate

 
  ADP + phosphoenolpyruvate + CO2
