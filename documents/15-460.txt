   Weyl metrics      Weyl metrics   In general relativity , the Weyl metrics (named after the German-American mathematician Hermann Weyl ) refer to the class of static and axisymmetric solutions to Einstein's field equation . Three members in the renowned Kerr–Newman family solutions, namely the Schwarzschild , nonextremal Reissner–Nordström and extremal Reissner–Nordström metrics, can be identified as Weyl-type metrics.
