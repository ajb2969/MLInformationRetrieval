Conformal Killing equation

In conformal geometry, the conformal Killing equation on a manifold of space-dimension n with metric g g g describes those vector fields X X X which preserve g g g up to scale, i.e.

Vector fields that satisfy the conformal Killing equation are exactly those vector fields whose flow preserves the conformal structure of the manifold. The name Killing refers to Wilhelm Killing, who first investigated the Killing equation for vector fields that preserve a Riemannian metric.
