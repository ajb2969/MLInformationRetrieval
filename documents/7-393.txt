   Sidney Lau romanisation      Sidney Lau romanisation   Sidney Lau is a system of romanisation for Cantonese , developed by Sidney Lau for teaching Cantonese. It was developed for use in Cantonese radio, and is essentially an adaptation of the Meyer-Wempe romanisation.
