Ent-sandaracopimaradiene synthase

Ent-sandaracopimaradiene synthase (, OsKS10, ent-sandaracopimara-8(14),15-diene synthase) is an enzyme with system name ent-copalyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (ent-sandaracopimara-8(14),15-diene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ent-copalyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ent-sandaracopimara-8(14),15-diene + diphosphate

ent-Sandaracopimaradiene is a precursor of the rice oryzalexins A-F.
