Ent-cassa-12,15-diene 11-hydroxylase

Ent-cassa-12,15-diene 11-hydroxylase (, ent-cassadiene C11alpha-hydroxylase, CYP76M7) is an enzyme with system name ent-cassa-12,15-diene,NADPH:oxygen 11-oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ent-cassa-12,15-diene + O2 + NADPH + H+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ent-11beta-hydroxycassa-12,15-diene + NADP+ + H2O

Ent-cassa-12,15-diene 11-hydroxylase requires cytochrome P450.
