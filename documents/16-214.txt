Cucurbitadienol synthase

Cucurbitadienol synthase (, CPQ (gene), (S)-2,3-epoxysqualene mutase (cyclizing, cucurbitadienol-forming)) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, cucurbitadienol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene 
 
 
cucurbitadienol
