Low-specificity L-threonine aldolase

Low-specificity L-threonine aldolase (, LtaE) is an enzyme with system name L-threonine/L-allo-threonine acetaldehyde-lyase (glycine-forming).12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) L-threonine

glycine + acetaldehyde

(2) L-allo-threonine 
 
 
 
  glycine + acetaldehyde
 

This enzyme requires pyridoxal phosphate.
