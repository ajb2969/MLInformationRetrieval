   Peroxyureidoacrylate/ureidoacrylate amidohydrolase      Peroxyureidoacrylate/ureidoacrylate amidohydrolase   Peroxyureidoacrylate/ureidoacrylate amidohydrolase (, RutB ) is an enzyme with system name (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid amidohydrolase . 1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (Z)-3-peroxyaminoacrylate + CO 2 + NH 3 (overall reaction)  (1a) (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (Z)-3-peroxyaminoacrylate + carbamate   (1b) carbamate   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   CO 2 + NH 3 (spontaneous)    (2) (Z)-2-methylureidoacrylate peracid + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (Z)-2-methylperoxyaminoacrylate + CO 2 + NH 3 (overall reaction)  (2a) (Z)-2-methylureidoacrylate peracid + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   (Z)-2-methylperoxyaminoacrylate + carbamate  (2b) carbamate   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   CO 2 + NH 3 (spontaneous)     The enzyme also shows activity towards ureidoacrylate .
