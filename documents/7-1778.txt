   Balian–Low theorem      Balian–Low theorem   In mathematics , the Balian–Low theorem in Fourier analysis is named for Roger Balian and Francis E. Low . The theorem states that there is no well-localized window function (or Gabor atom ) g either in time or frequency for an exact Gabor frame (Riesz Basis).
