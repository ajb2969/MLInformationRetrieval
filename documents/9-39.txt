   Oriented projective geometry      Oriented projective geometry   Oriented projective geometry is an oriented version of real projective geometry .  Whereas the real projective plane describes the set of all unoriented lines through the origin in R 3 , the oriented projective plane describes lines with a given orientation. There are applications in computer graphics and computer vision where it is necessary to distinguish between rays light being emitted or absorbed by a point.  Elements in an oriented projective space are defined using signed homogeneous coordinates .
