   Flip (mathematics)      Flip (mathematics)   In algebraic geometry , flips and flops are codimension-2 surgery operations arising in the minimal model program , given by blowing up along a relative canonical ring . In dimension 3 flips are used to construct minimal models, and any two birationally equivalent minimal models are connected by a sequence of flops. It is conjectured that the same is true in higher dimensions.
