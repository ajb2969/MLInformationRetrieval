Miltiradiene synthase

Miltiradiene synthase (, SmMDS, SmiKSL) is an enzyme with system name (+)-copaly-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, miltiradiene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(+)-copalyl diphosphate 
 
 
miltiradiene + diphosphate


This enzyme is isolated from the plant Selaginella moellendorffii.
