Rothe–Hagen identity

In mathematics, the Rothe–Hagen identity is a mathematical identity valid for all complex numbers ( x , y , z x y z x,y,z ) except where the denominators vanish:

It is a generalization of Vandermonde's identity, and is named after Heinrich August Rothe and Johann Georg Hagen.
