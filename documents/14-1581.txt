   Fréchet-Kolmogorov theorem      Fréchet-Kolmogorov theorem   In functional analysis , the Fréchet - Kolmogorov theorem (the names of Riesz or Weil are sometimes added as well) gives a necessary and sufficient condition for a set of functions to be relatively compact in an L p space . It can be thought of as an L p version of the Arzelà-Ascoli theorem , from which it can be deduced.
