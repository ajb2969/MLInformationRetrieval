Acetylajmaline esterase

Acetylajmaline esterase (, AAE, 2beta(R)-17-O-acetylajmalan:acetylesterase, acetylajmalan esterase) is an enzyme with system name 17-O-acetylajmaline O-acetylhydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) 17-O-acetylajmaline + H2O 
 
 
ajmaline + acetate

(2) 17-O-acetylnorajmaline + H2O 
 
 
norajmaline + acetate
 

This plant enzyme mediates the last stages in the biosynthesis of the indole alkaloid ajmaline.
