   't Hooft symbol      't Hooft symbol   The 't Hooft η symbol is a symbol which allows one to express the generators of the SU(2) Lie algebra in terms of the generators of Lorentz algebra. The symbol is a blend between the Kronecker delta and the Levi-Civita symbol . It was introduced by Gerard 't Hooft .
