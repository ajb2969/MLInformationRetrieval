PGL2

For the group PGL 2 subscript PGL 2 \mathrm{PGL}_{2} in mathematics, see projective linear group and modular group.

PGL2 is a gene that is one cause of familial paraganglioma, also known as glomus tumors. Its locus is at 11q13.1.
