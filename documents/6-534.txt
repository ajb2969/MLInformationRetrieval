   Ramanujan theta function      Ramanujan theta function   In mathematics , particularly q-analog theory, the Ramanujan theta function generalizes the form of the Jacobi theta functions , while capturing their general properties. In particular, the Jacobi triple product takes on a particularly elegant form when written in terms of the Ramanujan theta. The function is named after Srinivasa Ramanujan .
