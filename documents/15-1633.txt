Molybdenum cofactor guanylyltransferase

Molybdenum cofactor guanylyltransferase (, MobA, MoCo guanylyltransferase) is an enzyme with system name GTP:molybdenum cofactor guanylyltransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

GTP + molybdenum cofactor

diphosphate + guanylyl molybdenum cofactor
 

Catalyses the guanylation of the molybdenum cofactor.
