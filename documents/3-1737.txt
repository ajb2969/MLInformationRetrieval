In mathematics , an image is the subset of a function 's codomain which is the output of the function on a subset of its domain . Precisely evaluating the function at each element of a subset X of the domain produces a set called the image of X under or through the function. The inverse image or preimage of a particular subset S of the codomain of a function is the set of all elements of the domain that map to the members of S .  Image and inverse image may also be defined for general binary relations , not just functions.  Definition  The word "image" is used in three related ways.
