   Hurwitz quaternion order      Hurwitz quaternion order   The Hurwitz quaternion order is a specific order in a quaternion algebra over a suitable number field . The order is of particular importance in Riemann surface theory, in connection with surfaces with maximal symmetry , namely the Hurwitz surfaces . 1 The Hurwitz quaternion order was studied in 1967 by Goro Shimura , 2 but first explicitly described by Noam Elkies in 1998. 3 For an alternative use of the term, see Hurwitz quaternion (both usages are current in the literature).
