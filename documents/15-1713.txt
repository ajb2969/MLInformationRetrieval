Farnesyl diphosphatase

Farnesyl diphosphatase (, FPP phosphatase) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphohydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
 
  (2E,6E)-farnesol + diphosphate


The enzyme is involved in the biosynthesis of acyclic sesquiterpenoids.
