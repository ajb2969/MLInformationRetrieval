Decaprenylphospho-beta-D-ribofuranose 2-oxidase

Decaprenylphospho-beta-D-ribofuranose 2-oxidase (, decaprenylphosphoryl-beta-D-ribofuranose 2'-epimerase, Rv3790, DprE1) is an enzyme with system name trans,octacis-decaprenylphospho-beta-D-ribofuranose:FAD 2-oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

trans,octacis-decaprenylphospho-beta-D-ribofuranose + FAD 
 
 
 
  trans,octacis-decaprenylphospho-beta-D-erythro-pentofuranosid-2-ulose + FADH2


This enzyme from the bacterium Mycobacterium smegmatis participates in epimerization of trans,octacis-decaprenylphospho-beta-D-ribofuranose to trans,octacis-decaprenylphospho-beta-D-arabinoofuranose.
