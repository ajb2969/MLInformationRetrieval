In spectroscopy , this distribution, with frequency as the dependent variable, is known as a van der Waals profile . 1 It is a special case of the inverse-gamma distribution .  It is one of the few distributions that are stable and that have probability density functions that can be expressed analytically, the others being the normal distribution and the Cauchy distribution .
