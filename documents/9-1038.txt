   Moisture advection      Moisture advection   Moisture advection is the horizontal transport of water vapor by the wind . Measurement and knowledge of atmospheric water vapor, or "moisture", is crucial in the prediction of all weather elements, especially clouds , fog , temperature , humidity thermal comfort indices and precipitation .
