   Basis (universal algebra)      Basis (universal algebra)   In universal algebra a basis is a structure inside of some (universal) algebras, which are called free algebras . It generates all algebra elements from its own elements by the algebra operations in an independent manner. It also represents the endomorphisms of an algebra by certain indexings of algebra elements, which can correspond to the usual matrices when the free algebra is a vector space .
