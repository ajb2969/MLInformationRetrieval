   Superconformal algebra      Superconformal algebra   In theoretical physics , the superconformal algebra is a graded Lie algebra or superalgebra that combines the conformal algebra and supersymmetry . It generates the superconformal group in some cases (In two Euclidean dimensions, the Lie superalgebra does not generate any Lie supergroup .).  In two dimensions, the superconformal algebra is infinite-dimensional. In higher dimensions, there is a finite number of known examples of superconformal algebras.
