   Chandrasekhar number      Chandrasekhar number   The Chandrasekhar number is a dimensionless quantity used in magnetic convection to represent ratio of the Lorentz force to the viscosity . It is named after the Indian  astrophysicist  Subrahmanyan Chandrasekhar .  The number's main function is as a measure of the magnetic field , being proportional to the square of a characteristic magnetic field in a system.
