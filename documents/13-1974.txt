   Oregonator      Oregonator   The Oregonator is a theoretical model for a type of autocatalytic reaction . The Oregonator (Orygunator) is the simplest realistic model of the chemical dynamics of the oscillatory Belousov-Zhabotinsky reaction . 1 It was created by Richard Field and Richard M. Noyes at the University of Oregon . 2 It is a portmanteau of Oregon and oscillator .  Earlier, the Brusselator model was proposed by Ilya Prigogine and his collaborators at the Free University of Brussels 3 as a portmanteau of Brussels and oscillator .
