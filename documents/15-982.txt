Hydrazine oxidoreductase (, HAO (ambiguous)) is an enzyme with system name hydrazine:acceptor oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

hydrazine + acceptor 
 
 
 
  N2 + reduced acceptor
 

Hydroxylamine oxidoreductase is involved in the pathway of anaerobic ammonium oxidation in anammox bacteria.
