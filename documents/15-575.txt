   Consistency (suspension)      Consistency (suspension)   Consistency is a term used in the pulp and paper  making industry.  The consistency of a suspension is defined as:      C  =   x  /   (   x  +  y   )        C    x    x  y      C=x/(x+y)     Where C is consistency, x is the mass of pulp, and y is the mass of the rest of the suspension (usually mainly composed of water).
