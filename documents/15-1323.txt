16S rRNA (cytidine1409-2'-O)-methyltransferase

16S rRNA (cytidine1409-2'-O)-methyltransferase (, TlyA) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:16S rRNA (cytidine1409-2'-O)-methyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-adenosyl-L-methionine + cytidine1409 in 16S rRNA 
 
 
S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + 2'-O-methylcytidine1409 in 16S rRNA
 

The bifunctional enzyme from Mycobacterium tuberculosis.
