   Adjugate matrix      Adjugate matrix   In linear algebra , the adjugate , classical adjoint , or adjunct of a square matrix is the transpose of the cofactor matrix .  The adjugate has sometimes been called the "adjoint", but today the "adjoint" of a matrix normally refers to its corresponding adjoint operator , which is its conjugate transpose .
