   Linalool dehydratase      Linalool dehydratase   Linalool dehydratase (, linalool hydro-lyase (myrcene-forming) ) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-linalool hydro-lyase (myrcene-forming) . 1 2 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (3S)- linalool  $\rightleftharpoons$  myrcene + H 2 O   In absence of oxygen this enzyme can also catalyse the isomerization of (3S)-linalool to geraniol .
