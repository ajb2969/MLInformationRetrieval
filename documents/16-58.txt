(+)-epicubenol synthase

(+)-epicubenol synthase (, farnesyl pyrophosphate cyclase) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((+)-epicubenol-forming).12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
 
  (+)-epicubenol + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+.
