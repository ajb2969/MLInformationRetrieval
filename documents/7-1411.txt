   Mountain pass theorem      Mountain pass theorem   The mountain pass theorem is an existence theorem from the calculus of variations . Given certain conditions on a function, the theorem demonstrates the existence of a saddle point . The theorem is unusual in that there are many other theorems regarding the existence of extrema , but few regarding saddle points.
