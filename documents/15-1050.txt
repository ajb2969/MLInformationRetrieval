Dinoflagellate luciferase

Dinoflagellate luciferase (, Gonyaulax luciferase) is a specific luciferase, an enzyme with system name dinoflagellate-luciferin:oxygen 132-oxidoreductase.123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

dinoflagellate luciferin + O2

 
  oxidized dinoflagellate luciferin + H2O + hnu
 

Dinoflagellate luciferase is a single protein with three luciferase domains.
