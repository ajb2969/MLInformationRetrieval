Minimal surfaces  A minimal surface is a surface which has zero mean curvature at all points. Classic examples include the catenoid , helicoid and Enneper surface . Recent discoveries include Costa's minimal surface and the Gyroid .  An extension of the idea of a minimal surface are surfaces of constant mean curvature .
