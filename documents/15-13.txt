   Negativity (quantum mechanics)      Negativity (quantum mechanics)  In [[quantum mechanics]], '''negativity''' is a measure of [[quantum entanglement]] which is easy to compute. It is a measure deriving fr om the PPT criterion for separability . 1 It has shown to be an entanglement monotone  2 3 and hence a proper measure of entanglement.
