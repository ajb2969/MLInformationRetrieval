Nitrate reductase (cytochrome)

Nitrate reductase (cytochrome) (, respiratory nitrate reductase, benzyl viologen-nitrate reductase) is an enzyme with system name ferrocytochrome:nitrate oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalises the following chemical reaction

2 ferrocytochrome + 2 H+ + nitrate 
 
 
 
  2 ferricytochrome + nitrite
