4-hydroxybutanoyl-CoA dehydratase

4-hydroxybutanoyl-CoA dehydratase () is an enzyme with system name 4-hydroxybutanoyl-CoA hydro-lyase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

4-hydroxybutanoyl-CoA 
 
 
 
  but-3-enoyl-CoA + H2O
 

This enzyme contains FAD and a [4Fe-4S] iron-sulfur cluster.
