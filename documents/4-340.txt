   Dirac adjoint      Dirac adjoint   In quantum field theory , the Dirac adjoint defines the dual operation of a Dirac spinor . The Dirac adjoint is motivated by the need to form well-behaved, measurable quantities out of Dirac spinors. Since the usual Hermitian adjoint lacks the Lorentz symmetry of the system, the Dirac adjoint must be used instead.
