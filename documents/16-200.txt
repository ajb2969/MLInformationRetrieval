23S rRNA pseudouridine2604 synthase

23S rRNA pseudouridine2604 synthase (, RluF, YjbC) is an enzyme with system name 23S rRNA-uridine2604 uracil mutase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

23S rRNA uridine2604

 
  23S rRNA pseudouridine2604


The enzyme can, to a small extent, also react with uridine2605.
