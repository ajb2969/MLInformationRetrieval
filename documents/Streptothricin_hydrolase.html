Streptothricin hydrolase

Streptothricin hydrolase (, sttH (gene)) is an enzyme with system name streptothricin-F hydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

streptothricin-F + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons streptothricin-F acid

The enzyme also catalyses the hydrolysis of streptothricin-D to streptothricin-D acid.
