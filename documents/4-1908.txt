   Elongated pentagonal gyrobicupola      Elongated pentagonal gyrobicupola   In geometry , the elongated pentagonal gyrobicupola is one of the Johnson solids ( J 39 ). As the name suggests, it can be constructed by elongating a pentagonal gyrobicupola ( J 31 ) by inserting a decagonal prism between its congruent halves. Rotating one of the pentagonal cupolae ( J 5 ) through 36 degrees before inserting the prism yields an elongated pentagonal orthobicupola ( J 38 ).
