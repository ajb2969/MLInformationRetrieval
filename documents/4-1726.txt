   Linearization      Linearization   In mathematics  linearization refers to finding the linear approximation to a function at a given point. In the study of dynamical systems , linearization is a method for assessing the local stability of an equilibrium point of a system of nonlinear  differential equations or discrete dynamical systems . 1 This method is used in fields such as engineering , physics , economics , and ecology .
