Introduction  The study of lengths of organisms, devices, materials, etc., is of major importance in the biological and engineering sciences. In general, the lifetime of a device is expected to exhibit decreasing failure rate (DFR) when its behavior over time is characterized by 'work-hardening' (in engineering terms) or 'immunity' (in biological terms).  The exponential-logarithmic model, together with its various properties, are studied by Tahmasbi and Rezaei (2008) 1 This model is obtained under the concept of population heterogeneity (through the process of compounding).
