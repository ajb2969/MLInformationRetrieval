   N = 2 superconformal algebra      N = 2 superconformal algebra   In mathematical physics , the N = 2 superconformal algebra is an infinite-dimensional Lie superalgebra , related to supersymmetry , that occurs in string theory and conformal field theory . It has important applications in mirror symmetry . It was introduced by  as a gauge algebra of the U(1) fermionic string.
