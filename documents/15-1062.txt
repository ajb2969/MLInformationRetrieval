1-Deoxypentalenic acid 11beta-hydroxylase

1-deoxypentalenic acid 11beta-hydroxylase (, PTLH (gene), SAV2991 (gene), PNTH (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 1-deoxypentalenic acid,2-oxoglutarate:oxygen oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

1-deoxypentalenate + 2-oxoglutarate + O2

 
  1-deoxy-11beta-hydroxypentalenate + succinate + CO2


1-Deoxypentalenic acid 11beta-hydroxylase contains Fe(II) and ascorbate.
