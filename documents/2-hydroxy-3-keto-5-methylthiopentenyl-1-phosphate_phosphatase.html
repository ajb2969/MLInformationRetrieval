2-hydroxy-3-keto-5-methylthiopentenyl-1-phosphate phosphatase

2-hydroxy-3-keto-5-methylthiopentenyl-1-phosphate phosphatase (, HK-MTPenyl-1-P phosphatase, MtnX, YkrX) is an enzyme with system name 2-hydroxy-5-(methylthio)-3-oxopent-1-enyl phosphate phosphohydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-hydroxy-5-(methylthio)-3-oxopent-1-enyl phosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 1,2-dihydroxy-5-(methylthio)pent-1-en-3-one + phosphate

The enzyme participates in the methionine salvage pathway in Bacillus subtilis.
