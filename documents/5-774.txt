Applications  ARPES  In angle-resolved photo-emission spectroscopy ( ARPES ), irradiating light on a crystal sample results in the ejection of an electron away from the crystal. Throughout the course of the interaction, one is allowed to conflate the two concepts of crystal and true momentum and thereby gain direct knowledge of a crystal's band structure.
