Protein-methionine-S-oxide reductase

Protein-methionine-S-oxide reductase (, methionine sulfoxide (protein) reductase, methionine sulfoxide peptide reductase, protein (methionine sulfoxide) reductase, Met(O)-peptide reductase, peptide methionine sulfoxide reductase, protein-L-methionine:oxidized-thioredoxin S-oxidoreductase) is an enzyme with system name protein-L-methionine:thioredoxin-disulfide S-oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

protein L-methionine + thioredoxin disulfide ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons protein L-methionine S-oxide + thioredoxin

Dithiothreitol can replace a reduced thioredoxin in the reverse reaction.
