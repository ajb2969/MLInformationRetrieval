7-Chloro-L-tryptophan oxidase

7-Chloro-L-tryptophan oxidase (, RebO) is an enzyme with system name 7-chloro-L-tryptophan:oxygen oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

7-chloro-L-tryptophan + O2

 
  2-imino-3-(7-chloroindol-3-yl)propanoate + H2O2


This enzyme contains a noncovalently bound FAD.
