Arsenate-mycothiol transferase

Arsenate-mycothiol transferase (, ArsC1, ArsC2, mycothiol:arsenate transferase) is an enzyme with system name mycothiol:arsenate S-arsenotransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction
(Figure)
Arsenate-mycothiol transferase.svg


mycothiol + arsenate

 
  arseno-mycothiol + H2O

Reduction of arsenate is part of a defence mechanism of the cell against toxic arsenate.
