Perakine reductase

Perakine reductase () is an enzyme with system name raucaffrinoline:NADP+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

raucaffrinoline + NADP+

 
  perakine + NADPH + H+


The biosynthesis of raucaffrinoline from perakine is a side route of the ajmaline biosynthesis pathway.
