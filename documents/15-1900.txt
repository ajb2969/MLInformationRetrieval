8-oxo-dGTP diphosphatase

8-oxo-dGTP diphosphatase (, MutT, 7,8-dihydro-8-oxoguanine triphosphatase, 8-oxo-dGTPase, 7,8-dihydro-8-oxo-dGTP pyrophosphohydrolase) is an enzyme with system name 8-oxo-dGTP diphosphohydrolase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

8-oxo-dGTP + H2O 
 
 
 
  8-oxo-dGMP + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+.
