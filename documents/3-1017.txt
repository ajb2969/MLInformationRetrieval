The bicommutant is particularly useful in operator theory , due to the von Neumann double commutant theorem , which relates the algebraic and analytic structures of operator algebras . Specifically, it shows that if M is a unital, self-adjoint operator algebra in the C*-algebra  B(H) , for some Hilbert space  H , then the weak closure , strong closure and bicommutant of M are equal.
