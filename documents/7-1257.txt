   Poisson–Lie group      Poisson–Lie group   In mathematics , a Poisson–Lie group is a Poisson manifold that is also a Lie group , with the group multiplication being compatible with the Poisson algebra structure on the manifold. The algebra of a Poisson–Lie group is a Lie bialgebra .
