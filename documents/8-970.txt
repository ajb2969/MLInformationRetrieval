   Mixed complementarity problem      Mixed complementarity problem   Mixed Complementarity Problem ( MCP ) is a problem formulation in mathematical programming . Many well-known problem types are special cases of, or may be reduced to MCP. It is a generalization of Nonlinear complementarity problem (NCP).
