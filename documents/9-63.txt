   Z-channel (information theory)      Z-channel (information theory)   A Z-channel is a communications channel used in coding theory and information theory to model the behaviour of some data storage systems.  Definition  A Z-channel (or a binary asymmetric channel ) is a channel with binary input and binary output where the crossover 1 → 0 occurs with nonnegative probability p , whereas the crossover 0 → 1 never occurs.
