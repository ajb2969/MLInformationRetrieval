Beta-santalene synthase

Beta-santalene synthase () is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate lyase (cyclizing, (-)-beta-santalene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  (-)-beta-santalene + diphosphate


The enzyme synthesizes a mixture of sesquiterpenoids from (2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate.
