UDP-N-acetylgalactosamine diphosphorylase

UDP-N-acetylgalactosamine diphosphorylase () is an enzyme with system name UTP:N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine-1-phosphate uridylyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

UTP + N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine 1-phosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons diphosphate + UDP-N-acetyl-alpha-D-galactosamine

The enzyme from plants and animals also acts on N-acetyl-alpha-D-glucosamine 1-phosphate.
