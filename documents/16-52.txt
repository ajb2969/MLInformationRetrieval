Longifolene synthase

Longifolene synthase () is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (longifolene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
longifolene + diphosphate


This enzyme forms alpha-longipinene, longicyclene and traces of other sesquiterpenoids.
