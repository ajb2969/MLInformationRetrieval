D-lactate dehydratase

D-lactate dehydratase (, glyoxylase III) is an enzyme with system name (R)-lactate hydro-lyase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(R)-lactate

methylglyoxal + H2O
 

The enzyme converts methylglyoxal to D-lactate.
