Squalene—-hopanol cyclase

Squalene—-hopanol cyclase (, squalene—-hopene cyclase) is an enzyme with system name hopan-22-ol hydro-lyase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

hopan-22-ol ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons squalene + H2O

The enzyme produces the cyclization products hopene and hopanol.
