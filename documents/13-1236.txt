It is a papyrus  manuscript of the First Epistle of Peter . 1  Description  To the present day survived only pieces from one leaf of the codex. The surviving texts of 1 Peter are verses 1:23-2:5.7-12, they are in a fragmentary condition. The manuscript palaeographically has been assigned to the 3rd or 4th century ( INTF ). The text is written in one column per page, 30 lines per page. 2 The Greek text of this codex probably is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type . It was published by D. Obdink in 2009.
