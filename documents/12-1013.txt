It is the earliest known manuscript of the Epistle to Philemon . The surviving texts of Philemon are verses 13-15, 24-25.  The manuscript paleographically has been assigned to the early 3rd century (or late 2nd century).  The Greek text of this codex is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type (or proto-Alexandrian).
