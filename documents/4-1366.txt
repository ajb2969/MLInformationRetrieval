   Newmark-beta method      Newmark-beta method   The Newmark-beta method is a method of numerical integration used to solve differential equations . It is widely used in numerical evaluation of the dynamic response of structures and solids such as in finite element analysis to model dynamic systems. The method is named after Nathan M. Newmark , 1 former Professor of Civil Engineering at the University of Illinois, who developed it in 1959 for use in structural dynamics .
