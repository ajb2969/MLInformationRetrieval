   Prosolvable group      Prosolvable group   In mathematics , more precisely in algebra , a prosolvable group (less common: prosoluble group ) is a group that is isomorphic to the inverse limit of an inverse system of solvable groups . Equivalently, a group is called prosolvable , if, viewed as a topological group , every open neighborhood of the identity contains a normal subgroup whose corresponding quotient group is a solvable group.
