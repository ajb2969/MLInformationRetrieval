Fumonisin B1 esterase

Fumonisin B1 esterase (, fumD (gene)) is an enzyme with a system name fumonisin B1 acylhydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

fumonisin B1 + 2 H2O 
 
 
aminopentol + 2 propane-1,2,3-tricarboxylate
 

The enzyme is involved in degradation of fumonisin B1.
