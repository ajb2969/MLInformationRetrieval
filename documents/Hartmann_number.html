Hartmann number

Hartmann number (Ha) is the ratio of electromagnetic force to the viscous force first introduced by Hartmann.1 It is defined by:

Ha = B L σ μ Ha B L σ μ \mathrm{Ha}=BL\sqrt{\frac{\sigma}{\mu}} where

  • B is the magnetic field
  • L is the characteristic length scale
  • σ is the electrical conductivity
  • μ is the dynamic viscosity
