Threo-3-hydroxy-D-aspartate ammonia-lyase

Threo-3-hydroxy-D-aspartate ammonia-lyase (, D-threo-3-hydroxyaspartate dehydratase) is an enzyme with system name threo-3-hydroxy-D-aspartate ammonia-lyase (oxaloacetate-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

threo-3-hydroxy-D-aspartate 
 
 
oxaloacetate + NH3


This enzyme is a pyridoxal-phosphate protein.
