   Etemadi's inequality      Etemadi's inequality   In probability theory , Etemadi's inequality is a so-called "maximal inequality", an inequality that gives a bound on the probability that the partial sums of a finite collection of independent random variables exceed some specified bound. The result is due to Nasrollah Etemadi .  Statement of the inequality  Let X 1 , ..., X n be independent real-valued random variables defined on some common probability space , and let α ≥ 0.
