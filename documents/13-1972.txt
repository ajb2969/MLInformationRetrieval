11 Lacertae

11 Lacertae (11 Lac) is a star in the constellation Lacerta. 11 Lacertae is a giant star with a stellar classification of K2III, an apparent magnitude of 4.50 and absolute magnitude −0.33.1 It lies at a distance of about 300 light years.
