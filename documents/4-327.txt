   Fejér kernel      Fejér kernel   In mathematics , the Fejér kernel is a summability kernel used to express the effect of Cesàro summation on Fourier series . It is a non-negative kernel, giving rise to an approximate identity . It is named after the Hungarian mathematician Lipót Fejér (1880–1959).
