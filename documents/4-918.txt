   Jacobi field      Jacobi field   In Riemannian geometry , a Jacobi field is a vector field along a geodesic    γ   γ   \gamma   in a Riemannian manifold describing the difference between the geodesic and an "infinitesimally close" geodesic. In other words, the Jacobi fields along a geodesic form the tangent space to the geodesic in the space of all geodesics. They are named after Carl Jacobi .
