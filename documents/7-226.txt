   Orthogonal wavelet      Orthogonal wavelet   An orthogonal wavelet is a wavelet whose associated wavelet transform is orthogonal . That is, the inverse wavelet transform is the adjoint of the wavelet transform. If this condition is weakened one may end up with biorthogonal wavelets .
