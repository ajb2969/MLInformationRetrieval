Arabidiol synthase

Arabidiol synthase (, PEN1 (gene), (S)-squalene-2,3-epoxide hydro-lyase (arabidiol forming)) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene hydro-lyase (arabidiol forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

arabidiol ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene + H2O

The reaction occurs in the reverse direction.
