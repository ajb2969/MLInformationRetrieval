Alpha-guaiene synthase

Alpha-guaiene synthase (, PatTps177 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name (2Z,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, alpha-guaiene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  alpha-guaiene + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+.
