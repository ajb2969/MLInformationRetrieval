Alpha-humulene 10-hydroxylase

Alpha-humulene 10-hydroxylase (, CYP71BA1) is an enzyme with system name alpha-humulene,NADPH:oxygen 10-oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

alpha-humulene + O2 + NADPH + H+

 
  10-hydroxy-alpha-humulene + NADP+ + H2O
 

Alpha-humulene 10-hydroxylase requires cytochrome P-450.
