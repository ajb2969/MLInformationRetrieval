Quarter hypercubic honeycomb

In geometry, the quarter hypercube honeycomb (or quarter n-cubic honeycomb) is a dimensional infinite series of honeycombs, based on the hypercube honeycomb. It is given a Schläfli symbol q{4,3...3,4} or Coxeter symbol qδ4 representing the regular form with quarter the vertices removed and containing the symmetry of Coxeter group D ~ n - 1 subscript normal-~ D n 1 {\tilde{D}}_{n-1} for n ≥ 5, with D ~ 4 subscript normal-~ D 4 {\tilde{D}}_{4} = A ~ 4 subscript normal-~ A 4 {\tilde{A}}_{4} and for quarter n-cubic honeycombs D ~ 5 subscript normal-~ D 5 {\tilde{D}}_{5} = B ~ 5 subscript normal-~ B 5 {\tilde{B}}_{5} .1
