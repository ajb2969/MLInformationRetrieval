   Singularity function      Singularity function   Singularity functions are a class of discontinuous functions that contain singularities , i.e. they are discontinuous at its singular points. Singularity functions have been heavily studied in the field of mathematics under the alternative names of generalized functions and distribution theory . 1 2 3 The functions are notated with brackets, as     ⟨   x  -  a   ⟩   n     superscript   delimited-⟨⟩    x  a    n    \langle x-a\rangle^{n}   where n is an integer.
