   Braided Hopf algebra      Braided Hopf algebra   In mathematics , a braided Hopf algebra is a Hopf algebra in a braided monoidal category . The most common braided Hopf algebras are objects in a Yetter–Drinfeld category of a Hopf algebra H , particurlarly the Nichols algebra of a braided vectorspace in that category.  The notion should not be confused with quasitriangular Hopf algebra .  Definition  Let H be a Hopf algebra over a field k , and assume that the antipode of H is bijective.
