Fumigaclavine A dimethylallyltransferase

Fumigaclavine A dimethylallyltransferase (, FgaPT1) is an enzyme with system name dimethylallyl-diphosphate:fumigaclavine A dimethylallyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

fumigaclavine A + dimethylallyl diphosphate 
 
 
fumigaclavine C + diphosphate
 

Fumigaclavine C is an ergot alkaloid produced by some fungi of the Trichocomaceae family.
