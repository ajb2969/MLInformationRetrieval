   Redheffer matrix      Redheffer matrix   In mathematics, a Redheffer matrix , studied by , is a (0,1) matrix whose entries a ij are 1 if i divides j or if j = 1; otherwise, a ij = 0.  The determinant of the n x n  square Redheffer matrix is given by the Mertens function  M ( n ).  Example  The matrix below is the 12 × 12 Redheffer matrix.
