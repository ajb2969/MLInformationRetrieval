Thebaine 6-O-demethylase

Thebaine 6-O-demethylase (, T6ODM) is an enzyme with system name thebaine,2-oxoglutarate:oxygen oxidoreductase (6-O-demethylating).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

thebaine + 2-oxoglutarate + O2

neopinone + formaldehyde + succinate + CO2


Thebaine 6-O-demethylase contains Fe2+.
