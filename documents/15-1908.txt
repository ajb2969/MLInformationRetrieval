   Diadenosine hexaphosphate hydrolase (ATP-forming)      Diadenosine hexaphosphate hydrolase (ATP-forming)   Diadenosine hexaphosphate hydrolase (ATP-forming) (, Ndx1 ) is an enzyme with system name P1,P6-bis(5'-adenosyl)hexaphosphate nucleotidohydrolase (ATP-forming) . 1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) P1,P6-bis(5'-adenosyl)hexaphosphate + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   2 ATP  (2) P1,P5-bis(5'-adenosyl)pentaphosphate + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   ATP + ADP  (3) P1,P4-bis(5'-adenosyl)tetraphosphate + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   ATP + AMP   The enzyme requires the presence of the divalent cations (Mn 2+ , Mg 2+ , Zn 2+ , and Co 2+ ).
