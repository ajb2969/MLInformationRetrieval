Legendre functions of fractional degree

Legendre functions of fractional degree exist and follow from insertion of fractional derivatives as defined by fractional calculus and non-integer factorials (defined by the gamma function) into the Rodrigues' formula. The resulting functions continue to satisfy the Legendre differential equation throughout (‚àí1,1), but are no longer regular at the endpoints. The fractional degree Legendre function Pn agrees with the associated Legendre polynomial P.
