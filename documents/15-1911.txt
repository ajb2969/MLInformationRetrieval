   Petkovšek's algorithm      Petkovšek's algorithm   Petkovšek's algorithm is a computer algebra algorithm that computes a basis of hypergeometric terms solution of its input linear recurrence equation with polynomial coefficients. Equivalently, it computes a first order right factor of linear difference operators with polynomial coefficients. This algorithm is implemented in all the major computer algebra systems.
