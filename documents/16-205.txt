TRNA pseudouridine65 synthase

TRNA pseudouridine65 synthase (, TruC, YqcB) is an enzyme with system name tRNA-uridine65 uracil mutase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

tRNA uridine65

 
  tRNA pseudouridine65


TruC specifically modifies uridines at positions 65 in tRNA.
