This ensures that an image of the input of formed at the output with a magnification m . That would be the case of vertical line r A  r B in R I transformed to vertical line r A  r B in R O .  In nonimaging optics , the goal is not to form an image but simply to transfer all light from the input aperture to the output aperture. This is accomplished by transforming the edge rays âˆ‚ R I of R I to edge rays âˆ‚ R O of R O . This is known as the edge ray principle .
