Low level injection

Low level injection conditions for a P-N junction refers to the state where the number of minority carriers generated are small compared to the majority carriers of the material. Essentially, the semiconductor's majority carrier concentration will remain (relatively) unchanged, while the minority carrier concentration sees a large increase. In this condition minority carrier recombination rates are linear.1
