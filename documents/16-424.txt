   Versor (physics)      Versor (physics)     In geometry and physics , the versor of an axis or of a vector is a unit vector indicating its direction .  The versor of a Cartesian axis is also known as a standard basis vector . The versor of a vector is also known as a normalized vector .  Versors of a Cartesian coordinate system  The versors of the axes of a Cartesian coordinate system are the unit vectors codirectional with the axes of that system. Every Euclidean vector  a in a n -dimensional Euclidean space ( ) can be represented as a linear combination of the n versors of the corresponding Cartesian coordinate system.
