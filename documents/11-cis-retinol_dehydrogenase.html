11-cis-retinol dehydrogenase

11-cis-retinol dehydrogenase (, RDH5 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 11-cis-retinol:NAD+ oxidoreductase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

11-cis-retinol---[retinal-binding-protein] + NAD+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 11-cis-retinal---[retinol-binding-protein] + NADH + H+

This enzyme from retinal pigment epithelium, catalyses the reduction of 11-cis-retinol to 11-cis-retinal.
