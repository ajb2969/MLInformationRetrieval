   Ehrenfest model      Ehrenfest model   The Ehrenfest model (or dog-flea model 1 ) of diffusion was proposed by Tatiana and Paul Ehrenfest to explain the second law of thermodynamics . The model considers N particles in two containers. Particles independently change container at a rate λ .
