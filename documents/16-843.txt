   Draft:Exact couple      Draft:Exact couple   In mathematics, an exact couple , due to , is a general source of spectral sequences . It is common especially in algebraic topology ; for example, Serre spectral sequence can be constructed by first constructing an exact couple.  For the definition of an exact sequence and the construction of a spectral sequence from it (which is immediate), see spectral sequence#Exact couples . For a basic example, see Bockstein spectral sequence . The present article covers additional materials.  Exact couple of a filtered complex  Let R be a ring, which is fixed throughout the discussion. Note if R is Z , then modules over R are the same thing as abelian groups .  Each filtered chain complex of modules determines an exact couple, which in turn determines a spectral sequence, as follows.
