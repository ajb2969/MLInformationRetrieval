Tubulin GTPase

Tubulin GTPase () is an enzyme with system name GTP phosphohydrolase (microtubule-releasing).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

GTP + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons GDP + phosphate

This enzyme participates in tubulin folding and division plane formation.
