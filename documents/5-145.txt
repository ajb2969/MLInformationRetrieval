   Volume integral      Volume integral   In mathematics —in particular, in multivariable calculus —a volume integral refers to an integral over a 3- dimensional domain, that is, it is a special case of multiple integrals . Volume integrals are especially important in physics for many applications, for example, to calculate flux densities.
