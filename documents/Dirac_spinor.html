Dirac spinors and the Dirac algebra

The Dirac matrices are a set of four 4√ó4 matrices that are used as spin and charge operators.

The Dirac matrices are typically written as Œ≥ Œº superscript Œ≥ Œº \scriptstyle\gamma^{\mu} where Œº Œº \scriptstyle\mu runs from 0 to 3.
