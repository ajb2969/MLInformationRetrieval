   Volume viscosity      Volume viscosity   Volume viscosity (also called second viscosity or bulk viscosity ) becomes important only for such effects where fluid compressibility is essential. Examples would include shock waves and sound propagation. It appears in the Stokes' law (sound attenuation) that describes propagation of sound in Newtonian liquid .
