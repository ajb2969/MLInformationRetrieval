D-alanine—D-serine ligase

D-alanine—D-serine ligase (, VanC, VanE, VanG) is an enzyme with system name D-alanine:D-serine ligase (ADP-forming).12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

D-alanine + D-serine + ATP ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons D-alanyl-D-serine + ADP + phosphate

The product of this enzyme, D-alanyl-D-serine, can be incorporated into the peptidoglycan pentapeptide instead of the usual D-alanyl-D-alanine dipeptide.
