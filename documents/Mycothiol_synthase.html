Mycothiol synthase

Mycothiol synthase (, MshD) is an enzyme with system name acetyl-CoA:desacetylmycothiol O-acetyltransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

desacetylmycothiol + acetyl-CoA ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons CoA + mycothiol

This enzyme catalyses the last step in the biosynthesis of mycothiol
