Alcohol dehydrogenase (quinone)

Alcohol dehydrogenase (quinone) (, type III ADH, membrane associated quinohaemoprotein alcohol dehydrogenase) is an enzyme with system name alcohol:quinone oxidoreductase.123456789 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ethanol + ubiquinone 
 
 
 
  acetaldehyde + ubiquinol
 

This enzyme is present in acetic acid bacteria where it is involved in acetic acid production.
