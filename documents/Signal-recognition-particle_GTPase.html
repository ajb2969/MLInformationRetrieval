Signal-recognition-particle GTPase

Signal-recognition-particle GTPase () is an enzyme with system name GTP phosphohydrolase (protein-synthesis-assisting).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

GTP + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons GDP + phosphate

Enzyme activity is associated with the signal-recognition particle.
