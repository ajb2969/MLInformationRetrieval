   Bockstein spectral sequence      Bockstein spectral sequence   In mathematics, the Bockstein spectral sequence is a spectral sequence relating the homology with mod p coefficients and the homology reduced mod p . It is named after Meyer Bockstein .  Definition  Let C be a chain complex of torsion-free abelian groups and p a prime number .
