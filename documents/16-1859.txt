Purpose
Convert light travel distance in terms of Mly (millions of light years) to redshift in values of km/s (ie kps) (kilometres per second)
Usage


(MANDATORY) value of light travel distance in terms of millions of light years

(OPTIONAL) adds km/s to the display

(OPTIONAL) define the Hubble Constant (H0) in terms of kps/Mly if you do not wish to use the default of 20kps/Mly
