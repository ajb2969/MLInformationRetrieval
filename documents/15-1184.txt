Carotene epsilon-monooxygenase

Carotene epsilon-monooxygenase (, CYP97C1, LUT1) is an enzyme with system name alpha-carotene:oxygen oxidoreductase (3-hydroxylating).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

alpha-carotene + O2 + AH2

 
  alpha-cryptoxanthin + A + H2O
 

Carotene epsilon-monooxygenase is a heme-thiolate protein (P450).
