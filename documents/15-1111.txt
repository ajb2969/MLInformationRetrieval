   Vitamin D3 24-hydroxylase      Vitamin D3 24-hydroxylase   Vitamin D3 24-hydroxylase (, CYP24A1 ) is an enzyme with system name calcitriol,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (24-hydroxylating) . 1 2 3 4 5 6 7 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) calcitriol + NADPH + H + + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   calcitetrol + NADP + + H 2 O  (2) calcidiol + NADPH + H + + O 2    ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   secalciferol + NADP + + H 2 O   Vitamin D3 24-hydroxylase is a heme - thiolate enzyme (P-450).
