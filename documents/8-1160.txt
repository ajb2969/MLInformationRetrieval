   Malliavin derivative      Malliavin derivative   In mathematics , the Malliavin derivative is a notion of derivative in the Malliavin calculus . Intuitively, it is the notion of derivative appropriate to paths in classical Wiener space , which are "usually" not differentiable in the usual sense.
