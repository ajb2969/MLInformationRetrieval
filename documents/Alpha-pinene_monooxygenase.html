Alpha-pinene monooxygenase

Alpha-pinene monooxygenase () is an enzyme with system name (-)-alpha-pinene,NADH:oxygen oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(-)-alpha-pinene + NADH + H+ + O2 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons alpha-pinene oxide + NAD+ + H2O

Alpha-pinene monooxygenase takes part in catabolism of alpha-pinene.
