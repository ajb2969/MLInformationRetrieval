   Minkowski–Steiner formula      Minkowski–Steiner formula   In mathematics , the Minkowski–Steiner formula is a formula relating the surface area and volume of compact  subsets of Euclidean space . More precisely, it defines the surface area as the "derivative" of enclosed volume in an appropriate sense.  The Minkowski–Steiner formula is used, together with the Brunn–Minkowski theorem , to prove the isoperimetric inequality . It is named after Hermann Minkowski and Jakob Steiner .
