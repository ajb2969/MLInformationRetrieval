Motivation  In measure theory, we are not interested in semi-rings and rings themselves, but rather in œÉ-algebras generated by them. The idea is that it is possible to build a pre-measure on a semi-ring S (for example Stieltjes measures ), which can then be extended to a pre-measure on R(S) , which can finally be extended to a measure on a œÉ-algebra through Caratheodory's extension theorem. As œÉ-algebras generated by semi-rings and rings are the same, the difference does not really matter (in the measure theory context at least).
