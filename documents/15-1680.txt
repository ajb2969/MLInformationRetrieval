Protein phosphatase methylesterase-1

Protein phosphatase methylesterase-1 (, PME-1, PPME1) is an enzyme with system name (phosphatase 2A protein)-leucine ester acylhydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

[phosphatase 2A protein]-leucine methyl ester + H2O 
 
 
 
  [phosphatase 2A protein]-leucine + methanol


A key regulator of protein phosphatase 2A.
