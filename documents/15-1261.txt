23S rRNA (adenine2503-C2)-methyltransferase

23S rRNA (adenine2503-C2)-methyltransferase (, RlmN, YfgB, Cfr) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:23S rRNA (adenine2503-C2)-methyltransferase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 S-adenosyl-L-methionine + adenine2503 in 23S rRNA

S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + L-methionine + 5'-deoxyadenosine + 2-methyladenine2503 in 23S rRNA
 

23S rRNA (adenine2503-C2)-methyltransferase contains an [4Fe-4S] cluster.
