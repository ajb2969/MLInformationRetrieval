(+)-cubenene synthase

(+)-cubenene synthase () is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((+)-cubenene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  (+)-cubenene + diphosphate


This enzyme requires presence of Mg2+.
