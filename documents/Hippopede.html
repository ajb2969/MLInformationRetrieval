Hippopede

In geometry, a hippopede (from Ancient Greek ἱπποπέδη, "horse fetter") is a plane curve determined by an equation of the form

Hippopedes are bicircular rational algebraic curves of degree 4 and symmetric with respect to both the x and y axes.
