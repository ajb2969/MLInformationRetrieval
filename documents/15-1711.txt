Sclareol cyclase

Sclareol cyclase (, geranylgeranyl pyrophosphate:sclareol cyclase, geranylgeranyl pyrophosphate-sclareol cyclase, GGPP:sclareol cyclase) is an enzyme with system name geranylgeranyl-diphosphate diphosphohydrolase (sclareol-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranylgeranyl diphosphate + 2 H2O 
 
 
sclareol + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+ or Mn2+ for activity.
