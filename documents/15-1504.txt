   Lipid IVA 4-amino-4-deoxy-L-arabinosyltransferase      Lipid IVA 4-amino-4-deoxy-L-arabinosyltransferase   Lipid IVA 4-amino-4-deoxy-L-arabinosyltransferase (, undecaprenyl phosphate-alpha-L-Ara4N transferase , 4-amino-4-deoxy-L-arabinose lipid A transferase , polymyxin resistance protein PmrK , arnT (gene) ) is an enzyme with system name 4-amino-4-deoxy-alpha-L-arabinopyranosyl ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl phosphate:lipid IVA 4-amino-4-deoxy-L-arabinopyranosyltransferase . 1 2 3 4 5 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction   (1) 4-amino-4-deoxy-alpha-L-arabinopyranosyl ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl phosphate + alpha-Kdo-(2->4)-alpha-Kdo-(2->6)-lipid A   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   alpha-Kdo-(2->4)-alpha-Kdo-(2->6)-[4-P-L-Ara4N]-lipid A + ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl phosphate  (2) 4-amino-4-deoxy-alpha-L-arabinopyranosyl ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl phosphate + lipid IVA   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   lipid IIA + ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl phosphate  (3) 4-amino-4-deoxy-alpha-L-arabinopyranosyl ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl phosphate + alpha-Kdo-(2->4)-alpha-Kdo-(2->6)-lipid IVA   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   4'-alpha-L-Ara4N-alpha-Kdo-(2->4)-alpha-Kdo-(2->6)-lipid IVA + ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl phosphate   This integral membrane protein is present in the inner membrane of certain Gram negative  endobacteria .
