Syn-copalyl-diphosphate synthase

Syn-copalyl-diphosphate synthase (, OsCyc1, OsCPSsyn, syn-CPP synthase, syn-copalyl diphosphate synthase) is an enzyme with system name 9alpha-copalyl-diphosphate lyase (decyclizing).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranylgeranyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 9alpha-copalyl diphosphate

This enzyme requires a divalent metal ion, preferably Mg2+, for activity.
