D-amino acid dehydrogenase (quinone)

D-amino acid dehydrogenase (quinone) (, DadA) is an enzyme with system name D-amino acid:quinone oxidoreductase (deaminating).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

D-amino acid + H2O + quinone

 
  2-oxo carboxylate + NH3 + quinol


This enzyme is iron-sulfur flavoprotein.
