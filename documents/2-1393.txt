   Generalized Fourier series      Generalized Fourier series   In mathematical analysis , many generalizations of Fourier series have proved to be useful. They are all special cases of decompositions over an orthonormal basis of an inner product space . Here we consider that of square-integrable functions defined on an interval of the real line , which is important, among others, for interpolation theory.
