A vector bundle together with an orientation is called an oriented bundle . Just as a real vector bundle is classified by the real infinite Grassmannian , oriented bundles are classified by the infinite Grassmannian of oriented real vector spaces.  The basic invariant of an oriented bundle is the Euler class . The multiplication (that is, cup product) by the Euler class of an oriented bundle gives rise to a Gysin sequence .
