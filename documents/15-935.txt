Flavin reductase (NADH)

Flavin reductase (NADH) (, NADH-dependent flavin reductase, flavin:NADH oxidoreductase) is an enzyme with system name flavin:NAD+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

reduced flavin + NAD+

 
  flavin + NADH + H+


The Escherichia coli enzyme reduces free flavins by NADH.
