L-proline amide hydrolase

L-proline amide hydrolase (, S-stereoselective piperazine-2-tert-butylcarboxamide hydrolase, LaaA, L-amino acid amidase) is an enzyme with system name (S)-piperidine-2-carboxamide amidohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) (S)-piperidine-2-carboxamide + H2O 
 
 
 
  (S)-piperidine-2-carboxylic acid + NH3

(2) L-prolinamide + H2O 
 
 
 
  L-proline + NH3
