   Conjugacy class sum      Conjugacy class sum   In abstract algebra , a conjugacy class sum , or simply class sum , is a function defined for each conjugacy class of a finite group  G as the sum of the elements in that conjugacy class. The class sums of a group form a basis for the center of the associated group algebra .  Definition  Let G be a finite group, and let C 1 ,...,C k be the distinct conjugacy classes of G .
