Taxoid 7beta-hydroxylase

Taxoid 7beta-hydroxylase () is an enzyme with system name taxusin,NADPH:oxygen 7-oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

taxusin + O2 + NADPH + H+

 
  7beta-hydroxytaxusin + NADP+ + H2O
 

Taxoid 7beta-hydroxylase requires cytochrome P450.
