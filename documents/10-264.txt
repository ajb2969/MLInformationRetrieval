   Clarkson's inequalities      Clarkson's inequalities   In mathematics , Clarkson's inequalities , named after James A. Clarkson , are results in the theory of L p spaces . They give bounds for the L p - norms of the sum and difference of two measurable functions in L p in terms of the L p -norms of those functions individually.  Statement of the inequalities  Let ( X , Σ, μ ) be a measure space ; let f , g : X → R be measurable functions in L p .
