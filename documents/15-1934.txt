   Relaxed intersection      Relaxed intersection   The relaxed intersection of m sets corresponds to the classical intersection between sets except that it is allowed to relax few sets in order to avoid an empty intersection. This notion can be used to solve Constraints Satisfaction Problems that are inconsistent by relaxing a small number of constraints . When a bounded-error approach is considered for parameter estimation , the relaxed intersection makes it possible to be robust with respect to some outliers .
