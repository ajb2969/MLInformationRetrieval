Lupeol synthase

Lupeol synthase (, LUPI, BPW, RcLUS) is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, lupeol-forming).1234567 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene 
 
 
lupeol


Also forms some beta-amyrin.
