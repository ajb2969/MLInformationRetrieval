   Wahlquist fluid      Wahlquist fluid   In general relativity , the Wahlquist fluid is an exact rotating perfect fluid solution to Einstein's equation with equation of state corresponding to constant gravitational mass density.  Introduction  The Wahlquist fluid was first discovered by Hugo D. Wahlquist in 1968. 1 It is one of few known exact rotating perfect fluid solutions in general relativity. The solution reduces to the static Whittaker metric in the limit of zero rotation.
