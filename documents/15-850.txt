Uracil dehydrogenase

Uracil dehydrogenase (, uracil oxidase) is an enzyme with system name uracil:(acceptor) oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

uracil + acceptor 
 
 
barbiturate + reduced acceptor
 

Also oxidizes thymine. The enzyme acts on the hydrated derivative of the substrate.
