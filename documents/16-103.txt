1,8-cineole synthase

1,8-cineole synthase (, 1,8-cineole cyclase, geranyl pyrophoshate:1,8-cineole cyclase, 1,8-cineole synthetase) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, 1,8-cineole-forming).12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
 
  1,8-cineole + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mn2+ or Zn2+.
