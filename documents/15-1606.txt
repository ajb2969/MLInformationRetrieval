Propionate kinase

Propionate kinase (, PduW, TdcD, propionate/acetate kinase) is an enzyme with system name ATP:propanoate phosphotransferase.123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + propanoate

 
  ADP + propanoyl phosphate
 

This enzyme requires Mg2+.
