6'''-Hydroxyneomycin C oxidase

6'''-hydroxyneomycin C oxidase (, neoG (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 6'''-deamino-6'''-hydroxyneomycin C:oxygen 6'''-oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

6'''-deamino-6'''-hydroxyneomycin C + O2 $\rightleftharpoons$ 6'''-deamino-6'''-oxoneomycin C + H2O2


This enzyme participates in biosynthesis of aminoglycoside antibiotics of the neomycin family.
