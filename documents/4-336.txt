   Resistance distance      Resistance distance   In graph theory , the resistance distance between two vertices of a simple connected graph , G , is equal to the resistance between two equivalent points on an electrical network , constructed so as to correspond to G , with each edge being replaced by a 1 ohm  resistance . It is a metric on graphs .
