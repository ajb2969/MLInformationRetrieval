   HD 143436      HD 143436   HD 143436 is a star in the constellation  Serpens . The temperature, rotation, mass, and abundance of elements in this star are indistinguishable from the same properties in the Sun , and for this reason it is a solar twin candidate. The only notable difference is an approximately six times higher abundance of lithium compared to the Sun.
