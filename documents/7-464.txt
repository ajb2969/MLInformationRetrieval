   Van der Waerden notation      Van der Waerden notation   In theoretical physics , van der Waerden notation 1 2 refers to the usage of two-component spinors ( Weyl spinors ) in four spacetime dimensions. This is standard in twistor theory and supersymmetry . It is named after Bartel Leendert van der Waerden .
