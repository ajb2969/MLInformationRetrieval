   16-hydroxysteroid epimerase      16-hydroxysteroid epimerase   In enzymology , a 16-hydroxysteroid epimerase () is an enzyme that catalyzes the chemical reaction   16alpha-hydroxysteroid   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   16beta-hydroxysteroid   Hence, this enzyme has one substrate , 16alpha-hydroxysteroid , and one product , 16beta-hydroxysteroid .
