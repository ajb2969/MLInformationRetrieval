Usage

This template calculates mass M of a spherical body of the density ρ and diameter d:

M = π * ρ * d 3 6 . M π ρ superscript d 3 6 M=\frac{\pi*\rho*d^{3}}{6}.

The template should be used in the following way:

{{MEst|d|ρ}},

where ρ is body's density in g/cm3, and d is diameter in km. The result is expressed in 1021 kg.

Example: ρ=2.0 g/cm3, d=100 km:

M=·1021 kg.
