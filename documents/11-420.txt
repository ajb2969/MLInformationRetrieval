   4-pyridoxolactonase      4-pyridoxolactonase   In enzymology , a 4-pyridoxolactonase () is an enzyme that catalyzes the chemical reaction   4-pyridoxolactone + H 2 O   ⇌   normal-⇌   \rightleftharpoons   4-pyridoxate   Thus, the two substrates of this enzyme are 4-pyridoxolactone and H 2 O , whereas its product is 4-pyridoxate .  This enzyme belongs to the family of hydrolases , specifically those acting on carboxylic ester bonds. The systematic name of this enzyme class is 4-pyridoxolactone lactonohydrolase .
