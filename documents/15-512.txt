   Distorted Schwarzschild metric      Distorted Schwarzschild metric   The distorted Schwarzschild metric refers to the metric of a standard/isolated Schwarzschild spacetime exposed in external fields. In numerical simulation, the Schwarzschild metric can be distorted by almost arbitrary kinds of external energy–momentum distribution . However, in exact analysis, the mature method to distort the standard Schwarzschild metric is restricted to the framework of Weyl metrics .
