   Unit dummy force method      Unit dummy force method   The Unit dummy force method provides a convenient means for computing displacements in structural systems. It is applicable for both linear and non-linear material behaviours as well as for systems subject to environmental effects, and hence more general than Castigliano's second theorem .  Discrete systems  Consider a discrete system such as trusses, beams or frames having members interconnected at the nodes.
