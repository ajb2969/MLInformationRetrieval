Sphingomyelin phosphodiesterase D

Sphingomyelin phosphodiesterase D (, sphingomyelinase D) is an enzyme with system name sphingomyelin ceramide-phosphohydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

sphingomyelin + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ceramide phosphate + choline

This enzyme hydrolyses 2-lysophosphatidylcholine to choline and 2-lysophosphatidate.
