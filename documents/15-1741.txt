(1) [protein]-3-O-(N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl)-L-serine + H2O 
 
 
 
  [protein]-L-serine + N-acetyl-D-glucosamine
 
(2) [protein]-3-O-(N-acetyl-beta-D-glucosaminyl)-L-threonine + H2O 
 
 
 
  [protein]-L-threonine + N-acetyl-D-glucosamine
