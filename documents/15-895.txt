Acrylyl-CoA reductase (NADH)

Acrylyl-CoA reductase (NADH) () is an enzyme with system name propanoyl-CoA:NAD+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

propanoyl-CoA + NAD+

acryloyl-CoA + NADH + H+


The reaction is catalysed in the opposite direction.
