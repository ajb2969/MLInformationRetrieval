Pimelyl-(acyl-carrier protein) methyl ester esterase

Pimelyl-(acyl-carrier protein) methyl ester esterase (, BioH) is an enzyme with system name pimelyl-(acyl-carrier protein) methyl ester hydrolase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

pimelyl-[acyl-carrier protein] methyl ester + H2O 
 
 
 
  pimelyl-[acyl-carrier protein] + methanol


This enzyme takes part in biotin biosynthesis in Gram-negative bacteria.
