Molybdopterin-synthase adenylyltransferase

Molybdopterin-synthase adenylyltransferase (, MoeB, adenylyltransferase and sulfurtransferase MOCS3) is an enzyme with system name ATP:molybdopterin-synthase adenylyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + [molybdopterin-synthase sulfur-carrier protein]-Gly-Gly ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons diphosphate + [molybdopterin-synthase sulfur-carrier protein]-Gly-Gly-AMP

This enzyme adenylates the C-terminus of the small subunit of the molybdopterin synthase.
