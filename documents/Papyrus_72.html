Text

The Greek text of this codex is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type. According to Aland in 1-2 Peter it has normal text, in Jude free text, both with certain peculiarities. Aland placed it into I Category.6 It is close to the Codex Vaticanus and Codex Alexandrinus.7 ğ”“ ğ”“ \mathfrak{P} 72 is currently housed at the Vatican Library (P. Bodmer VIII) at Rome.89
