   Vector algebra relations      Vector algebra relations   The relations below apply to vectors in a three-dimensional Euclidean space . 1 Some, but not all of them, extend to vectors of higher dimensions. In particular, the cross product of two vectors is not available in all dimensions. See Seven-dimensional cross product .
