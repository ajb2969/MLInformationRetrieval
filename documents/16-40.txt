Alpha-farnesene synthase

Alpha-farnesene synthase (, (E,E)-alpha-farnesene synthase, AFS1, MdAFS1) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate lyase ((3E,6E)-alpha-farnesene-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  (3E,6E)-alpha-farnesene + diphosphate
