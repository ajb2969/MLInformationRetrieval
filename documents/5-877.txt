   Kruskal–Katona theorem      Kruskal–Katona theorem   In algebraic combinatorics , the Kruskal–Katona theorem gives a complete characterization of the f -vectors of abstract simplicial complexes . It includes as a special case the Erdős–Ko–Rado theorem and can be restated in terms of uniform hypergraphs . The theorem is named after Joseph Kruskal and Gyula O. H. Katona . It was independently proved by Marcel-Paul Schützenberger , but his contribution escaped notice for several years.
