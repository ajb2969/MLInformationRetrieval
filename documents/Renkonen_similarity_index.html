Renkonen similarity index

The Renkonen similarity index (P), is a measure of dissimilarity between two communities (sites), based on relative (proportional) abundances p i = n i / ∑ n i subscript p i subscript n i subscript n i p_{i}=n_{i}/\sum{n_{i}} of individuals of composite species. It was developed by the botanist Olavi Renkonen and published in 1938.1

The codomain of this distance function ranges from 1 (identical proportional abundances) to 0 (no taxa shared).
