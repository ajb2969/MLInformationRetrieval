In the 1990s, Alex Wilkie showed that one has the same result if instead of adding every analytic function, one just adds the exponential function to R to get the ordered real field with exponentiation, R exp , a result known as Wilkie's theorem . 3 Wilkie then tackled the question of which finite sets of functions could be added to R to get this result.
