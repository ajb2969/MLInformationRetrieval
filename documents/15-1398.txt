(Z)-3-hexen-1-ol acetyltransferase

(Z)-3-hexen-1-ol acetyltransferase (, CHAT, At3g03480) is an enzyme with system name acetyl-CoA:(3Z)-hex-3-en-1-ol acetyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

acetyl-CoA + (3Z)-hex-3-en-1-ol 
 
 
 
  CoA + (3Z)-hex-3-en-1-yl acetate
 

The enzyme is responsible for the production of (3Z)-hex-3-en-1-yl acetate.
