   Kolmogorov continuity theorem      Kolmogorov continuity theorem   In mathematics , the Kolmogorov continuity theorem is a theorem that guarantees that a stochastic process that satisfies certain constraints on the moments of its increments will be continuous (or, more precisely, have a "continuous version"). It is credited to the Soviet  mathematician  Andrey Nikolaevich Kolmogorov .
