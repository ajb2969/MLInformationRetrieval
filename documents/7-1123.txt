   Q-difference polynomial      Q-difference polynomial   In combinatorial  mathematics , the q-difference polynomials or q-harmonic polynomials are a polynomial sequence defined in terms of the q-derivative . They are a generalized type of Brenke polynomial , and generalize the Appell polynomials . See also Sheffer sequence .
