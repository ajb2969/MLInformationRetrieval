Purpose

Convert light travel distance in terms of Mpc (megaparsecs) to redshift in values of km/s (ie kps) (kilometres per second)

Usage

  • (MANDATORY) value of light travel distance in terms of megaparsecs (Mpc)

  • (OPTIONAL) adds km/s to the display

  • (OPTIONAL) define the Hubble Constant (H0) in terms of kps/Mpc if you do not wish to use the default of 71kps/Mpc
