Cysteate synthase

Cysteate synthase () is an enzyme with system name sulfite:O-phospho-L-serine sulfotransferase (phosphate-hydrolysing, L-cysteate-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

O-phospho-L-serine + sulfite

 
  L-cysteate + phosphate


This enzyme is a pyridoxal-phosphate protein.
