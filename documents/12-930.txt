   Three-dimensional rotation operator      Three-dimensional rotation operator   This article derives the main properties of rotations in 3-dimensional space .  The three Euler rotations are one way to bring a rigid body to any desired orientation by sequentially making rotations about axis' fixed relative to the object. However, this can also be achieved with one single rotation ( Euler's rotation theorem ). Using the concepts of linear algebra it is shown how this single rotation can be performed.
