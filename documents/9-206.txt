   List of area moments of inertia      List of area moments of inertia   The following is a list of area moments of inertia . The Area Moment of Inertia or second moment of area has a unit of dimension length 4 , and should not be confused with the mass moment of inertia . If the piece is thin, however, the mass moment of inertia equals the area density times the area moment of inertia. Each is with respect to a horizontal axis through the centroid of the given shape, unless otherwise specified.
