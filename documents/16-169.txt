Prolycopene isomerase

Prolycopene isomerase (, CRTISO, carotene cis-trans isomerase, ZEBRA2 (gene), carotene isomerase, carotenoid isomerase) is an enzyme with system name 7,9,7',9'-tetracis-lycopene cis-trans-isomerase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

7,9,7',9'-tetracis-lycopene 
 
 
 
  all-trans-lycopene
 

This enzyme is involved in carotenoid biosynthesis.
