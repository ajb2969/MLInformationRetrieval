Implementation in the Mizar system  The Mizar language, underlying the implementation of TG and providing its logical syntax, is typed and the types are assumed to be non-empty. Hence, the theory is implicitly taken to be non-empty . The existence axioms, e.g. the existence of the unordered pair, is also implemented indirectly by the definition of term constructors.
