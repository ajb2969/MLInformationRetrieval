Beta-sesquiphellandrene synthase

Beta-sesquiphellandrene synthase (, Tps1, Os08g07100 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name ''(2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, beta-sesquiphellandrene-forming) ''.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
 
  beta-sesquiphellandrene + diphosphate
