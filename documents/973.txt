   Excisive triad      Excisive triad   In topology , a branch of mathematics, an excisive triad is a triple    (  X  ;  A  ,  B  )     X  A  B    (X;A,B)   of topological spaces such that A , B are subspaces of X and X is the union of the interior of A and the interior of B . 1 Note B is not required to be a subspace of A .
