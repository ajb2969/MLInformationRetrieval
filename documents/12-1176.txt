   Lebedev–Milin inequality      Lebedev–Milin inequality   In mathematics, the Lebedev–Milin inequality is any of several inequalities for the coefficients of the exponential of a power series, found by  and . It was used in the proof of the Bieberbach conjecture , as it shows that the Milin conjecture implies the Robertson conjecture .
