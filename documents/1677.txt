   Glass batch calculation      Glass batch calculation   Glass batch calculation or glass batching is used to determine the correct mix of raw materials (batch) for a glass melt.  Principle  The raw materials mixture for glass melting is termed "batch". The batch must be measured properly to achieve a given, desired glass formulation.
