   Octic reciprocity      Octic reciprocity   In number theory , octic reciprocity is a reciprocity law relating the residues of 8th powers modulo  primes , analogous to the law of quadratic reciprocity .  There is a rational reciprocity law for 8th powers, due to Williams. Define the symbol ( x | p ) k to be +1 if x is a k -th power modulo the prime p and -1 otherwise.
