   Skorokhod's embedding theorem      Skorokhod's embedding theorem   In mathematics and probability theory , Skorokhod's embedding theorem is either or both of two theorems that allow one to regard any suitable collection of random variables as a Wiener process ( Brownian motion ) evaluated at a collection of stopping times . Both results are named for the Ukrainian  mathematician  A.V. Skorokhod .
