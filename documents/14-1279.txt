   Potassium hexafluoronickelate(IV)      Potassium hexafluoronickelate(IV)   Potassium hexafluoronickelate(IV) is an inorganic compound with the chemical formula . It can be produced through the reaction of potassium fluoride , nickel dichloride , and fluorine .  It reacts violently with water, releasing oxygen. It dissolves in anhydrous hydrogen fluoride to produce a light-red solution.
