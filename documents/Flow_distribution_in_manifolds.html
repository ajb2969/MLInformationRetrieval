Flow distribution in manifolds

The '''flow in manifolds''' is extensively encountered in many indus

trial processes when it is necessary to distribute a large fluid stream into several parallel streams and then to collect them into one discharge stream, such as fuel cells, plate heat exchanger, radial flow reactor, and irrigation. Manifolds can usually be categorized into one of the following types: dividing, combining, Z-type and U-type manifolds (Fig. 1).123 A key question is the uniformity of the flow distribution and pressure drop.
