Duct modes

The acoustic pressure in a cylindrical duct can be expressed as the superposition of duct modes:

p = ∑ α i ϕ i p subscript α i subscript ϕ i p=\sum\alpha_{i}\phi_{i}

where α i subscript α i \alpha_{i} is the participation factor or modal amplitude and ϕ i subscript ϕ i \phi_{i} is the mode shape. The duct mode shape are analytically determined from the solution of the Helmholtz equation.1
