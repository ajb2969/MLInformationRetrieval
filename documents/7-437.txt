   Upper-convected time derivative      Upper-convected time derivative   In continuum mechanics , including fluid dynamics , an upper-convected time derivative or Oldroyd derivative , named after James G. Oldroyd , is the rate of change of some tensor property of a small parcel of fluid that is written in the coordinate system rotating and stretching with the fluid.
