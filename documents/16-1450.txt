   Strang splitting      Strang splitting   Strang splitting is a numerical method of solving ordinary differential equations (ODEs). It is named after Gilbert Strang . The essential idea is that a complex ODE can be decomposed into multiple simpler ODEs. Each ODE can be advanced independently, and then the total change would be the sum of all individual changes.
