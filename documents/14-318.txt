It is also known as the logistic normal distribution , 1 which often refers to a multinomial logit version (e.g. 2 3 4 5 ).  A variable might be modeled as logit-normal if it is a proportion, which is bounded by zero and one, and where values of zero and one never occur. One is often interested in ratios rather than absolute component values.
