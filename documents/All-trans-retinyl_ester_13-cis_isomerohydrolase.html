All-trans-retinyl ester 13-cis isomerohydrolase

All-trans-retinyl ester 13-cis isomerohydrolase () is an enzyme with system name all-trans-retinyl ester acylhydrolase, 13-cis retinol forming.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction
