Soyasaponin III rhamnosyltransferase

Soyasaponin III rhamnosyltransferase (, UGT91H4, GmSGT3 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name UDP-rhamnose:soyasaponin III rhamnosyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

UDP-rhamnose + soyasaponin III 
 
 
 
  UDP + soyasaponin I
 

Part of the biosynthetic pathway for soyasaponins.
