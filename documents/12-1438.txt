It is a papyrus  manuscript of the Pauline epistles , it contains only 1 Thess 4:12-5:18. 25-28; 2 Thess 1:1-2; 2:1.9-11. The manuscript paleographically has been assigned to the 3rd century. 1  Description  The manuscript is written in large uncial letters. The nomina sacra are abbreviated. The number of the pages suggest that the manuscript was a collection of the Pauline epistles. 2 It is a carefully executed manuscript. 3  The Greek text of this codex is a representative of the Alexandrian text-type (rather proto-Alexandrian).
