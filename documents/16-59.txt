Zingiberene synthase

Zingiberene synthase (, alpha-zingiberene synthase, ZIS) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (zingiberene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate 
 
 
zingiberene + diphosphate
