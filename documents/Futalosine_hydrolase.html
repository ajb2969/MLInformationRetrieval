Futalosine hydrolase

Futalosine hydrolase (, futalosine nucleosidase, MqnB) is an enzyme with system name futalosine ribohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

futalosine + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons dehypoxanthine futalosine + hypoxanthine

This enzyme catalyses the second step of menaquinone biosynthesis.
