Shewanella algae

Shewanella algae is a rod-shaped Gram-negative marine bacterium. S. algae is also a facultative anaerobe with the ability to reduce iron, uranium and plutonium metabolically. When no oxygen is available, it can use metal cations as the terminal electron acceptor in the electron transport chain.
