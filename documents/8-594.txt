   Riesz mean      Riesz mean   In mathematics , the Riesz mean is a certain mean of the terms in a series . They were introduced by Marcel Riesz in 1911 as an improvement over the Cesàro mean . The Riesz mean should not be confused with the Bochner–Riesz mean or the Strong–Riesz mean .
