UDP-4-amino-4,6-dideoxy-N-acetyl-beta-L-altrosamine N-acetyltransferase

'''UDP-4-amino-4,6-dideoxy-N-acetyl-beta-L-altrosamine N-acetyltransferase ''' (, PseH) is an enzyme with system name acetyl-CoA:UDP-4-amino-4,6-dideoxy-N-acetyl-beta-L-altrosamine N-acetyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

acetyl-CoA + UDP-4-amino-4,6-dideoxy-N-acetyl-beta-L-altrosamine 
 
 
 
  CoA + UDP-2,4-bis(acetamido)-2,4,6-trideoxy-beta-L-altropyranose
 

This enzyme is isolated from Helicobacter pylori.
