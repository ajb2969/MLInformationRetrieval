Semicircular potential well

Boundary conditions

As with all quantum mechanics problems, if the boundary conditions are changed so does the wave function. If a particle is confined to the motion of an entire ring ranging from 0 to 2 π 2 π 2\pi , the particle is subject only to a periodic boundary condition (see particle in a ring).
