Presilphiperfolanol synthase

Presilphiperfolanol synthase (, BcBOT2, CND15) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphohydrolase (presilphiperfolan-8beta-ol-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate + H2O 
 
 
 
  presilphiperfolan-8beta-ol + diphosphate


This enzyme requires Mg2+.
