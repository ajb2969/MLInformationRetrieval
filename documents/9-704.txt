   Bonnesen's inequality      Bonnesen's inequality   Bonnesen's inequality is an inequality relating the length, the area, the radius of the incircle and the radius of the circumcircle of a Jordan curve . It is a strengthening of the classical isoperimetric inequality .  More precisely, consider a planar simple closed curve of length   L   L   L   bounding a domain of area   A   A   A   .
