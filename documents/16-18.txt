Sporulenol synthase

Sporulenol synthase (, sqhC (gene)) is an enzyme with system name tetraprenyl-beta-curcumene—sporulenol cyclase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

sporulenol 
 
 
 
 
  tetraprenyl-beta-curcumene + H2O
 

The reaction occurs in the reverse direction.
