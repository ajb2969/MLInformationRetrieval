   Moffat distribution      Moffat distribution   The Moffat distribution , named after the physicist  Anthony Moffat , is a continuous probability distribution based upon the Lorentzian distribution . Its particular importance in astrophysics is due to its ability to accurately reconstruct point spread functions , whose wings cannot be accurately portrayed by either a Gaussian or Lorentzian function.  Characterisation  Probability density function  The Moffat distribution can be described in two ways.
