2-hydroxy-dATP diphosphatase

2-hydroxy-dATP diphosphatase (, NUDT1, MTH1, MTH2, oxidized purine nucleoside triphosphatase, (2'-deoxy) ribonucleoside 5'-triphosphate pyrophosphohydrolase) is an enzyme with system name 2-hydroxy-dATP diphosphohydrolase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-hydroxy-dATP + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2-hydroxy-dAMP + diphosphate

The enzyme hydrolyses oxidizes purine nucleoside triphosphates.
