 , [3,4,3,3] |- |Properties||Vertex transitive |} In four-dimensional Euclidean geometry, the steritruncated 16-cell honeycomb is a uniform space-filling honeycomb, with runcinated 24-cell, truncated 16-cell, octahedral prism, 3-6 duoprism, and truncated tetrahedral prism cells.
