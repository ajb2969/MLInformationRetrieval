2-nitroimidazole nitrohydrolase

2-nitroimidazole nitrohydrolase (, NnhA, 2NI nitrohydrolase, 2NI denitrase) is an enzyme with system name 2-nitroimidazole nitrohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-nitroimidazole + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons imidazol-2-one + nitrite

This enzyme is present in the soil bacterium Mycobacterium sp. JS330
