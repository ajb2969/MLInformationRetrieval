Haynsworth inertia additivity formula

In mathematics, the Haynsworth inertia additivity formula, discovered by Emilie Virginia Haynsworth1 (1916–1985), concerns the number of positive, negative, and zero eigenvalues of a Hermitian matrix and of block matrices into which it is partitioned.
