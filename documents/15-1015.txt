Bromide peroxidase

Bromide peroxidase (, bromoperoxidase, haloperoxidase (ambiguous), eosinophil peroxidase) is an enzyme with system name bromide:hydrogen-peroxide oxidoreductase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

RH + HBr + H2O2

 
  RBr + 2 H2O
 

Bromo peroxidases of red and brown marine algae (Rhodophyta and Phaeophyta) contain vanadate.
