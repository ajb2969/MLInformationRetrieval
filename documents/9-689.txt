Myosin ATPase

Myosin ATPase () is an enzyme with system name ATP phosphohydrolase (actin-translocating).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + H2O 
 
 
 
  ADP + phosphate


ATP hydrolysis provides energy for actomyosin contraction.
