(+)-Larreatricin hydroxylase

(+)-Larreatricin hydroxylase () is an enzyme with system name (+)-larreatricin:oxygen 3'-hydroxylase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(+)-larreatricin + O2 + AH2

 
  (+)-3'-hydroxylarreatricin + A + H2O
 

This enzyme is isolated from the plant Larrea tridentata (creosote bush).
