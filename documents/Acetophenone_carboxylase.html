Acetophenone carboxylase

Acetophenone carboxylase () is an enzyme with system name acetophenone:carbon-dioxide ligase (ADP-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 ATP + acetophenone + HCO3− + H2O + H+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2 ADP + 2 phosphate + 3-oxo-3-phenylpropanoate

The enzyme is involved in anaerobic degradation of ethylbenzene.
