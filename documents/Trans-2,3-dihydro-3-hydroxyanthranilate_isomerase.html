Trans-2,3-dihydro-3-hydroxyanthranilate isomerase

Trans-2,3-dihydro-3-hydroxyanthranilate isomerase (, phzF (gene)) is an enzyme with system name (5S,6S)-6-amino-5-hydroxycyclohexane-1,3-diene-1-carboxyate isomerase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(5S,6S)-6-amino-5-hydroxycyclohexane-1,3-diene-1-carboxyate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (1R,6S)-6-amino-5-oxocyclohex-2-ene-1-carboxylate

The enzyme is involved in phenazine biosynthesis.
