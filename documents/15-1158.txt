Nitrilotriacetate monooxygenase

Nitrilotriacetate monooxygenase () is an enzyme with system name nitrilotriacetate,FMNH2:oxygen oxidoreductase (glyoxylate-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

nitrilotriacetate + FMNH2 + H+ + O2

iminodiacetate + glyoxylate + FMN + H2O
 

Nitrilotriacetate monooxygenase requires Mg2+.
