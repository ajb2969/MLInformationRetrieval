6-oxocamphor hydrolase

6-oxocamphor hydrolase (, OCH, camK (gene)) is an enzyme with system name bornane-2,6-dione hydrolase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

bornane-2,6-dione + H2O 
 
 
 
  [(1S)-4-hydroxy-2,2,3-trimethylcyclopent-3-enyl]acetate
 

This enzyme is isolated from Rhodococcus sp.
