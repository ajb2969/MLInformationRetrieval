D ~ 4 subscript normal-~ D 4 {\tilde{D}}_{4} = [31,1,1,1] |- |Dual|| |- |Properties||vertex-transitive |} In four-dimensional Euclidean geometry, the bitruncated tesseractic honeycomb is a uniform space-filling tessellation (or honeycomb) in Euclidean 4-space. It is constructed by a bitruncation of a tesseractic honeycomb. It is also called a cantic quarter tesseractic honeycomb from its q2{4,3,3,4} construction.
