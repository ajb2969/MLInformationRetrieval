Symmetry constructions

There are 3 different symmetry constructions, all with 3-3 duopyramid vertex figures. The B ~ 4 subscript normal-~ B 4 {\tilde{B}}_{4} symmetry doubles on D ~ 4 subscript normal-~ D 4 {\tilde{D}}_{4} in three possible ways, while F ~ 4 subscript normal-~ F 4 {\tilde{F}}_{4} contains the highest symmetry.
