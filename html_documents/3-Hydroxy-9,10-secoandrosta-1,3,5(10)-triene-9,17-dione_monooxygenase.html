3-Hydroxy-9,10-secoandrosta-1,3,5(10)-triene-9,17-dione monooxygenase

3-hydroxy-9,10-secoandrosta-1,3,5(10)-triene-9,17-dione monooxygenase (, HsaA) is an enzyme with system name 3-hydroxy-9,10-secoandrosta-1,3,5(10)-triene-9,17-dione,FMNH2:oxygen oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

3-hydroxy-9,10-secoandrosta-1,3,5(10)-triene-9,17-dione + FMNH2 + O2 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 3,4-dihydroxy-9,10-secoandrosta-1,3,5(10)-triene-9,17-dione + FMN + H2O

This bacterial enzyme participates in the degradation of several steroids, including cholesterol and testosterone.
