Sulfoacetaldehyde dehydrogenase (acylating)

Sulfoacetaldehyde dehydrogenase (acylating) (, SauS) is an enzyme with system name 2-sulfoacetaldehyde:NADP+ oxidoreductase (CoA-acetylating).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-sulfoacetaldehyde + CoA + NADP+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons sulfoacetyl-CoA + NADPH + H+

The enzyme is involved in degradation of sulfoacetate.
