Phosphonoacetaldehyde reductase (NADH)

Phosphonoacetaldehyde reductase (NADH) (, PhpC) is an enzyme with system name 2-hydroxyethylphosphonate:NAD+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-hydroxyethylphosphonate + NAD+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons phosphonoacetaldehyde + NADH + H+

The enzyme from Streptomyces viridochromogenes catalyses a step in the biosynthesis of phosphinothricin tripeptide, the reduction of phosphonoacetaldehyde to 2-hydroxyethylphosphonate.
