Fluoroacetyl-CoA thioesterase

Fluoroacetyl-CoA thioesterase () is an enzyme with system name fluoroacetyl-CoA hydrolase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

fluoroacetyl-CoA + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons fluoroacetate + CoA

Fluoroacetate is extremely toxic.
