Cocaine esterase

Cocaine esterase (, CocE, hCE2, hCE-2, human carboxylesterase 2) is an enzyme with system name cocaine benzoylhydrolase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

cocaine + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ecgonine methyl ester + benzoate

Rhodococcus sp. strain MB1 and Pseudomonas maltophilia strain MB11L can utilize cocaine as sole source of carbon and energy.
