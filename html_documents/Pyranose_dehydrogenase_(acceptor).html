Pyranose dehydrogenase (acceptor)

Pyranose dehydrogenase (acceptor) (, pyranose dehydrogenase, pyranose-quinone oxidoreductase, quinone-dependent pyranose dehydrogenase, PDH) is an enzyme with system name pyranose:acceptor oxidoreductase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) a pyranose + acceptor ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons a pyranos-2-ulose (or a pyranos-3-ulose or a pyranos-2,3-diulose) + reduced acceptor
(2) a pyranoside + acceptor ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons a pyranosid-3-ulose (or a pyranosid-3,4-diulose) + reduced acceptor

This enzyme requires FAD. A number of aldoses and ketoses in pyranose form, as well as glycosides, gluco-oligosaccharides, sucrose and lactose can act as a donor.
