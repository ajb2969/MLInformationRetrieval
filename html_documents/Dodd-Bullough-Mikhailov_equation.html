Dodd-Bullough-Mikhailov equation

'''Dodd-Bullough-Mikhailov equation is a nonlinear partial differential equation introduced by Roger Dodd, Robin Bullough, and Alexander Mikhailov1。

u x t + α * e u + γ * e - 2 * u = 0 subscript u x t α superscript e u γ superscript e 2 u 0 u_{xt}+\alpha*e^{u}+\gamma*e^{-2*u}=0

In 2005 Mathematician Wazwar combined the Tzitzeica equation with Dodd-Bullough-Mikhailov equation into Tzitz´eica-Dodd-Bullough-Mikhailov equation.2

Dodd-Bullough-Mikhailov equation has traveling wave solutions.
