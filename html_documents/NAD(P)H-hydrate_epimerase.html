NAD(P)H-hydrate epimerase

NAD(P)H-hydrate epimerase (, NAD(P)HX epimerase) is an enzyme with system name (6R)-6beta-hydroxy-1,4,5,6-tetrahydronicotinamide-adenine dinucleotide 6-epimerase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) (6R)-6beta-hydroxy-1,4,5,6-tetrahydronicotinamide-adenine dinucleotide ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (6S)-6beta-hydroxy-1,4,5,6-tetrahydronicotinamide-adenine dinucleotide
(2) (6R)-6beta-hydroxy-1,4,5,6-tetrahydronicotinamide-adenine dinucleotide phosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (6S)-6beta-hydroxy-1,4,5,6-tetrahydronicotinamide-adenine dinucleotide phosphate

The enzyme can use either (R)-NADH-hydrate or (R)-NADPH-hydrate as a substrate.
