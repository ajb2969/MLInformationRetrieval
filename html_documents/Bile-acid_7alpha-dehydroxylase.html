Bile-acid 7alpha-dehydroxylase

Bile-acid 7alpha-dehydroxylase (, cholate 7alpha-dehydroxylase, 7alpha-dehydroxylase, bile acid 7-dehydroxylase) is an enzyme with system name deoxycholate:NAD+ oxidoreductase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) deoxycholate + NAD+ + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons cholate + NADH + H+
(2) lithocholate + NAD+ + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons chenodeoxycholate + NADH + H+

Bile-acid 7alpha-dehydroxylase is highly specific for bile acid substrates.
