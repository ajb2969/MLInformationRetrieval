Gamma-terpinene synthase

Gamma-terpinene synthase (, OvTPS2, ClcTS) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, gamma-terpinene-forming).12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons gamma-terpinene + diphosphate

This enzyme is isolated from Thymus vulgaris (thyme), Citrus limon (lemon), Citrus unshiu (satsuma) and Origanum vulgare (oregano).
