Bresler Pister yield criterion

The Bresler-Pister yield criterion1 is a function that was originally devised to predict the strength of concrete under multiaxial stress states. This yield criterion is an extension of the Drucker-Prager yield criterion and can be expressed on terms of the stress invariants as
