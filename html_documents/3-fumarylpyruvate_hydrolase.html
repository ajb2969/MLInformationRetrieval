3-fumarylpyruvate hydrolase

3-Fumarylpyruvate hydrolase (, nagK (gene), naaD (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 3-fumarylpyruvate hydrolyase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

3-fumarylpyruvate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons fumarate + pyruvate

The enzyme is involved in bacterial degradation of 5-substituted salicylates.
