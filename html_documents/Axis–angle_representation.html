Uses

The axis‚Äìangle representation is convenient when dealing with rigid body dynamics. It is useful to both characterize rotations, and also for converting between different representations of rigid body motion, such as homogeneous transformations and twists.

When a rigid body rotates around a fixed axis, its axis‚Äìangle data are a constant rotation axis and the rotation angle continuously dependent on time.
