Singular/Delivery and Singular/On Time

Singular case definition

To fit to the needs of the environment, the granularity of a singular case ( D P * S D superscript subscript P S DP_{*}^{S} ) has to be defined. In general a singular case is described by a n-Tuple consisting of a set of the following order and delivery details:

  • order number
  • customer identifier
  • product identifier
  • wish date of customer
  • confirmed date of supplier
  • ship to information
  • delivery date
  • delivery note number
