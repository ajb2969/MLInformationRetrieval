(13E)-labda-7,13-dien-15-ol synthase

(13E)-labda-7,13-dien-15-ol synthase (, labda-7,13E-dien-15-ol synthase) is an enzyme with system name geranylgeranyl-diphosphate diphosphohydrolase ((13E)-labda-7,13-dien-15-ol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranylgeranyl diphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (13E)-labda-7,13-dien-15-ol + thumbnail

The enzyme from the lycophyte Selaginella moellendorffii is bifunctional
