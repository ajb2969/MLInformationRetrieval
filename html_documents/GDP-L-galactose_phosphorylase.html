GDP-L-galactose phosphorylase

GDP-L-galactose phosphorylase (, VTC2, VTC5) is an enzyme with system name GDP:alpha-L-galactose 1-phosphate guanylyltransferase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

GDP-L-galactose + phosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons alpha-L-galactose 1-phosphate + GDP

The enzyme catalyses a reaction of the Smirnoff-Wheeler pathway.
