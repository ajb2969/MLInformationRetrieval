L-glutamyl-(BtrI acyl-carrier protein) decarboxylase

L-glutamyl-(BtrI acyl-carrier protein) decarboxylase (, btrK (gene)) is an enzyme with system name L-glutamyl-(BtrI acyl-carrier protein) carboxy-lyase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

L-glutamyl-[BtrI acyl-carrier protein] ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 4-amino butanoyl-[BtrI acyl-carrier protein] + CO2

This enzyme binds pyridoxal 5'-phosphate.
