Nicotinate dehydrogenase (cytochrome)

Nicotinate dehydrogenase (cytochrome) (, nicotinic acid hydroxylase, nicotinate hydroxylase) is an enzyme with system name nicotinate:cytochrome 6-oxidoreductase (hydroxylating).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

nicotinate + a ferricytochrome + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 6-hydroxynicotinate + a ferrocytochrome + 2 H+

This two-component enzyme from Pseudomonas belongs to the family of xanthine dehydrogenases.
