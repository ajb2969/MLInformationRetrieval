Lebesgue's lemma

For Lebesgue's lemma for open covers of compact spaces in topology see Lebesgue's number lemma

In mathematics, Lebesgue's lemma is an important statement in approximation theory. It provides a bound for the projection error.

Statement

Let (V, ||·||) be a normed vector space, U be a subspace of V and let P P P be a linear projector on U U U . Then, for each v in V:

∥ v - P v ∥ ≤ ( 1 + ∥ P ∥ ) inf u ∈ U ∥ v - u ∥ . norm v P v 1 norm P subscript infimum u U norm v u \|v-Pv\|\leq(1+\|P\|)\inf_{u\in U}\|v-u\|.
