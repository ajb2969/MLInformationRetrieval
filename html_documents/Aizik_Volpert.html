Aizik Volpert

Life and academic career

Vol'pert graduated from Lviv University in 1951, earning the candidate of science degree and the docent title respectively in 1954 and 1956 from the same university:6 from 1951 on he worked at the Lviv Industrial Forestry Institute.7 In 1961 he become senior research fellow8 while 1962 he earned the "doktor nauk"9 degree from Moscow State University. Work

Index theory and elliptic boundary problems

Vol'pert developed an effective algorithm for calculating the index of an elliptic problem before the Atiyah-Singer index theorem appeared:13 He was also the first to show that the index of a singular matrix operator can be different from zero.14
