(3S,6E)-nerolidol synthase

(3S,6E)-nerolidol synthase (, (E)-nerolidol synthase, nerolidol synthase, (3S)-(E)-nerolidol synthase, FaNES1) is an enzyme with system name ''(2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((3S,6E)-nerolidol-forming) ''.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (3S,6E)-nerolidol + diphosphate

The enzyme catalyses a step in the formation of (3E)-4,8-dimethylnona-1,3,7-triene.
