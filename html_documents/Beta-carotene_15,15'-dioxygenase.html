Beta-carotene 15,15'-dioxygenase

Beta-carotene 15,15'-dioxygenase (, blh (gene)) is an enzyme with system name beta-carotene:oxygen 15,15'-dioxygenase (bond-cleaving).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

beta-carotene + O2 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2 all-trans-retinal

Beta-carotene 15,15'-dioxygenase contains Fe2+.
