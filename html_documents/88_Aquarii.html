88 Aquarii

88 Aquarii is the Flamsteed designation for a star in the equatorial constellation of Aquarius. The Bayer designation of this star is c2 Aquarii. In dark conditions it is visible to the naked eye with an apparent visual magnitude of +3.68.1 Based upon parallax measurements, this star is at a distance of around from Earth.2

The spectrum of 88 Aquarii matches an evolved giant star with a classification of K1 III.3 Its measured angular diameter is ,4 which, at the estimated distance of Delta Ophiuchi,5 yields a physical size of about 29 times the radius of the Sun.6 The cool, orange hued glow of this star comes from the outer atmosphere's effective temperature of 4,430 K.7
