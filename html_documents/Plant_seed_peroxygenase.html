Plant seed peroxygenase

Plant seed peroxygenase (, plant peroxygenase, soybean peroxygenase) is an enzyme with system name substrate:hydroperoxide oxidoreductase (RH-hydroxylating or epoxidising).12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

R1H + R2OOH ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons R1OH + R2OH

This enzyme is a heme protein that contains calcium binding motif.
