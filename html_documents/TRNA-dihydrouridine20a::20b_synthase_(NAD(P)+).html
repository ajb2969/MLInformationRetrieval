TRNA-dihydrouridine20a/20b synthase (NAD(P)+)

TRNA-dihydrouridine20a/20b synthase (NAD(P)+) (, Dus4p) is an enzyme with system name tRNA-5,6-dihydrouracil20a/20b:NAD(P)+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) 5,6-dihydrouracil20a in tRNA + NAD(P)+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons uracil20a in tRNA + NAD(P)H + H+
(2) 5,6-dihydrouracil20b in tRNA + NAD(P)+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons uracil20b in tRNA + NAD(P)H + H+

A flavoenzyme. The enzyme specifically modifies uracil20a and uracil20b in tRNA.
