12 squares |- |Edges||120 |- |Vertices||48 |- |Symmetry group||octahedral (Oh) |- |Subgroup restricting to one constituent||chiral octahedral (O) |} This uniform polyhedron compound is a composition of the 2 enantiomers of the snub cube. As a holosnub, it is represented by Schläfli symbol βr{4,3} and Coxeter diagram .

The vertex arrangement of this compound is shared by a convex nonuniform truncated cuboctahedron, having rectangular faces, alongside irregular hexagons and octagons, each alternating with two edge lengths.
