Geranylgeranyl diphosphate diphosphatase

Geranylgeranyl diphosphate diphosphatase (, geranylgeranyl diphosphate phosphatase) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranylgeranyl diphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons geranylgeraniol + diphosphate

This enzyme is involved in the biosynthesis of plaunotol.
