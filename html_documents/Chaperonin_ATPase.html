Chaperonin ATPase

Chaperonin ATPase (, chaperonin) is an enzyme with system name ATP phosphohydrolase (polypeptide-unfolding).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + phosphate

These enzymes are a subclass of molecular chaperones.
