A-1,4-glucan-protein synthase (UDP-forming)

Alpha-1,4-glucan-protein synthase (UDP-forming) (, UDP-glucose:protein glucosyltransferase, glycogen initiator synthase, UDPGlc:protein transglucosylase, UPTG, uridine diphosphoglucose protein transglucosylase I, proglycogen synthase, uridine diphosphoglucose-protein 4-alpha-glucosyltransferase, uridine diphosphoglucose-protein glucosyltransferase, UDP-glucose protein transglucosylase, UDP-glucose-protein glucosyltransferase, uridine diphosphate glucose-protein transglucosylase I) is an enzyme with system name UDP-glucose:protein 4-alpha-glucosyltransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

UDP-glucose + protein ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons UDP + alpha-D-glucosyl-protein

The enzyme builds up alpha-1,4-glucan chains covalently bound to protein.
