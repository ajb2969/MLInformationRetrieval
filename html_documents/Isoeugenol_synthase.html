Isoeugenol synthase

Isoeugenol synthase (, IGS1, t-anol/isoeugenol synthase 1) is an enzyme with system name eugenol:NADP+ oxidoreductase (coniferyl acetate reducing).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction.4

isoeugenol + acetate + NADP+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons coniferyl acetate + NADPH + H+

The enzyme acts in the reverse direction.
