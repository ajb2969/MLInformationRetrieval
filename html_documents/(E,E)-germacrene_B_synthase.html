(E,E)-germacrene B synthase

(E,E)-germacrene B synthase () is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((E,E)-germacrene-B-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (E,E)-germacrene B + diphosphate
