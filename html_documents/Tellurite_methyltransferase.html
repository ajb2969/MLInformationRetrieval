Tellurite methyltransferase

Tellurite methyltransferase (, TehB) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:tellurite methyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-adenosyl-L-methionine + tellurite ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + methanetelluronate

The enzyme is involved in the detoxification of tellurite.
