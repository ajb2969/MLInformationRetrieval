Conchospiral

In mathematics, a conchospiral is a three-dimensional spiral. In cylindrical coordinates, it is described by the parametric equations:

r = μ t a r superscript μ t a r=\mu^{t}a

θ = t θ t \theta=t

z = μ t c . z superscript μ t c z=\mu^{t}c. The projection of a conchospiral on the (r, θ)-plane is a logarithmic spiral.
