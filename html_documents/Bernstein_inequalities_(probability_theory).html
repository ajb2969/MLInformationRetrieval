Bernstein inequalities (probability theory)

Bernstein inequalities were proved and published by Sergei Bernstein in the 1920s and 1930s.1234 Later, these inequalities were rediscovered several times in various forms. Thus, special cases of the Bernstein inequalities are also known as the Chernoff bound, Hoeffding's inequality and Azuma's inequality.
