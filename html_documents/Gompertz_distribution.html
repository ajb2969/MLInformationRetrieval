Gompertz distribution

In probability and statistics, the Gompertz distribution is a continuous probability distribution. The Gompertz distribution is often applied to describe the distribution of adult lifespans by demographers12 and actuaries.34 Related fields of science such as biology5 and gerontology6 also considered the Gompertz distribution for the analysis of survival. More recently, computer scientists have also started to model the failure rates of computer codes by the Gompertz distribution.7 In Marketing Science, it has been used as an individual-level simulation for customer lifetime value modeling.8 In an independent effort, early users in the 1990s for the Gompertz distribution in CLV models included Edge Consulting and BrandScience

Properties

The Gompertz distribution is a flexible distribution that can be skewed to the right and to the left.
