Protoporphyrinogen IX dehydrogenase (menaquinone)

Protoporphyrinogen IX dehydrogenase (menaquinone) (, HemG) is an enzyme with system name protoporphyrinogen IX:menaquinone oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

protoporphyrinogen IX + 3 menaquinone ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons protoporphyrin IX + 3 menaquinol

This enzyme enables Escherichia coli to synthesize heme in both aerobic and anaerobic environments.
