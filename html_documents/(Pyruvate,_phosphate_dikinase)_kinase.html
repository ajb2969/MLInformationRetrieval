(Pyruvate, phosphate dikinase) kinase

(Pyruvate, phosphate dikinase) kinase (, PPDK regulatory protein, pyruvate, phosphate dikinase regulatory protein, bifunctional dikinase regulatory protein) is an enzyme with system name ADP:(pyruvate, phosphate dikinase) phosphotransferase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ADP + [pyruvate, phosphate dikinase] ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons AMP + [pyruvate, phosphate dikinase] phosphate

This enzyme is isolated from the plants Zea mays (maize) and arabidopsis.
