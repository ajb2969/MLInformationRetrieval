CDP-abequose synthase

CDP-abequose synthase (, rfbJ (gene)) is an enzyme with system name CDP-alpha-D-abequose:NADP+ 4-oxidoreductase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

CDP-alpha-D-abequose + NADP+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons CDP-4-dehydro-3,6-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucose + NADPH + H+

This enzyme iz from Yersinia pseudotuberculosis and Salmonella enterica.
