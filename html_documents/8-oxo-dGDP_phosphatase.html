8-oxo-dGDP phosphatase

8-oxo-dGDP phosphatase (, NUDT5) is an enzyme with system name 8-oxo-dGDP phosphohydrolase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

8-oxo-dGDP + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 8-oxo-dGMP + phosphate

The enzyme catalyses the hydrolysis of both 8-oxo-dGDP and 8-oxo-GDP.
