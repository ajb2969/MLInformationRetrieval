Fusicocca-2,10(14)-diene synthase

Fusicocca-2,10(14)-diene synthase (, fusicoccadiene synthase, PaFS, PaDC4) is an enzyme with system name geranylgeranyl diphosphate-lyase (fusicocca-2,10(14)-diene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranylgeranyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons fusicocca-2,10(14)-diene + diphosphate

This multifunctional enzyme also has EC 2.5.1.29, farnesyltranstransferase, activity.
