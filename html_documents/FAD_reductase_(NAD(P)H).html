FAD reductase (NAD(P)H)

FAD reductase (NAD(P)H) (, GTNG_3158 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name FADH2:NAD(P)+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

FADH2 + NAD(P)+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons FAD + NAD(P)H + H+

This enzyme is isolated from the bacterium Geobacillus thermodenitrificans. It takes part in degradation tryptophan.
