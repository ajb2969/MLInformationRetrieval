Benzil reductase ((R)-benzoin forming)

Benzil reductase ((R)-benzoin forming) () is an enzyme with system name (R)-benzoin:NADP+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(R)-benzoin + NADP+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons benzil + NADPH + H+

The bacterial enzyme (Xanthomonas oryzae) enantioselectively reduces one of the two carbonyl groups of benzil to form optically active (R)-benzoin.
