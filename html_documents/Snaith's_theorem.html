Snaith's theorem

In algebraic topology, a branch of mathematics, Snaith's theorem, introduced by Victor Snaith, identifies the complex K-theory spectrum with the localization of the suspension spectrum of ℂ P ∞ ℂ superscript P \mathbb{C}P^{\infty} away from the Bott element.
