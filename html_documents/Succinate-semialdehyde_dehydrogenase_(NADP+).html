Succinate-semialdehyde dehydrogenase (NADP+)

Succinate-semialdehyde dehydrogenase (NADP+) (, succinic semialdehyde dehydrogenase (NADP+), succinyl semialdehyde dehydrogenase (NADP+), succinate semialdehyde:NADP+ oxidoreductase, NADP-dependent succinate-semialdehyde dehydrogenase, GabD) is an enzyme with system name succinate-semialdehyde:NADP+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

succinate semialdehyde + NADP+ + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons succinate + NADPH + 2 H+

This enzyme participates in the degradation of glutamate and 4-aminobutyrate.
