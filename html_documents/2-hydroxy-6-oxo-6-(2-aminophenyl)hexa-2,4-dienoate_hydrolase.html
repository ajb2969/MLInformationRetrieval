2-hydroxy-6-oxo-6-(2-aminophenyl)hexa-2,4-dienoate hydrolase

2-hydroxy-6-oxo-6-(2-aminophenyl)hexa-2,4-dienoate hydrolase (, CarC) is an enzyme with system name (2E,4E)-6-(2-aminophenyl)-2-hydroxy-6-oxohexa-2,4-dienoate acylhydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,4E)-6-(2-aminophenyl)-2-hydroxy-6-oxohexa-2,4-dienoate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons anthranilate + (2E)-2-hydroxypenta-2,4-dienoate

This enzyme catalyses the third step in the aerobic degradation pathway of carbazole.
