(+)-Thujan-3-ol dehydrogenase

(+)-Thujan-3-ol dehydrogenase (, d-3-thujanol dehydrogenase, TDH) is an enzyme with system name (+)-thujan-3-ol:NAD(P)+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(+)-thujan-3-ol + NAD(P)+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (+)-thujan-3-one + NAD(P)H + H+

This enzyme is isolated from the plant Tanacetum vulgare.
