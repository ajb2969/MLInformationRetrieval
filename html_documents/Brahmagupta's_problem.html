Brahmagupta's problem

This problem was given in India by the mathematician Brahmagupta in 628 in his treatise Brahma Sputa Siddhanta:

solve the Pell equation

x 2 - 92 y 2 = 1. superscript x 2 92 superscript y 2 1. x^{2}-92y^{2}=1.

Brahmagupta gave the smallest solution as

( x , y ) = ( 1151 , 120 ) . x y 1151 120 (x,y)=(1151,120).
