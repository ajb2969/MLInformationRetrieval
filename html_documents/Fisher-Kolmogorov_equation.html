Fisher-Kolmogorov equation

Fisher-Kolmogorov equation named after Ronald Fisher and Andrey Kolmogorov, is a nonlinear partial differential equation of the form12

∂ u ∂ t = α k u ( 1 - u q ) + ∂ 2 u ∂ x 2 . u t α k u 1 superscript u q superscript 2 u superscript x 2 \frac{\partial u}{\partial t}=\frac{\alpha}{k}u(1-u^{q})+\frac{\partial^{2}u}{% \partial x^{2}}.\,

F-K equation has application in biology.
