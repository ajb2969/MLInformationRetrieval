Littlewood's rule

The earliest Revenue Management model is known as Littlewood’s rule, developed by Ken Littlewood while working at British Overseas Airways Corporation.

The two class model

Littlewood proposed the first static single resource quantity based RM model.1 It was a solution method for the seat inventory problem for a single leg flight with two fare classes.
