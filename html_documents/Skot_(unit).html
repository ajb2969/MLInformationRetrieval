Skot (unit)

Skot (symbol: sk) is an old and deprecated measurement unit of luminance, used for self-luminous objects (dark luminance).

Unit conversions

One skot is equal to:

  • 1 10 3 π 1 superscript 10 3 π \frac{1}{10^{3}\pi} candela per square metre
  • 10−7 lamberts
  • 10−3 apostilbs
  • 104 brils
  • 1 10 7 π 1 superscript 10 7 π \frac{1}{10^{7}\pi} stilbs (candela per square centimetre)
  • about 0.0000929 foot-lamberts
