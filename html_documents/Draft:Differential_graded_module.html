Draft:Differential graded module

A ℤ d superscript ℤ d \mathbb{Z}^{d} -graded module together with a differential; i.e., a square-zero endomorphism of the module.

Example: spectral sequence

The category of differential modules over a ring R coincides with the category of modules over the ring R [ ϵ ] R delimited-[] ϵ R[\epsilon] of dual numbers over R.(Cartan-Eilenberg)
