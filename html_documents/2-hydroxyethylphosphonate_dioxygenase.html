2-hydroxyethylphosphonate dioxygenase

2-hydroxyethylphosphonate dioxygenase (, HEPD, phpD (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 2-hydroxyethylphosphonate:O2 1,2-oxidoreductase (hydroxymethylphosphonate forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-hydroxyethylphosphonate + O2 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons hydroxymethylphosphonate + formate

2-hydroxyethylphosphonate dioxygenase contains non-heme-Fe(II).
