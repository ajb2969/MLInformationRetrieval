Heme ligase

Heme ligase (, heme detoxification protein, HDP, hemozoin synthase) is an enzyme with system name Fe3+:ferriprotoporphyrin IX ligase (beta-hematin-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 ferriprotoporphyrin IX ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons beta-hematin

This heme detoxifying enzyme is found in Plasmodium parasites.
