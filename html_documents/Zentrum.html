Zentrum

Zentrum is German for centre. It may refer to:

  • Zentrumspartei or Centre Party, a political party in Germany.
  • ETH Zentrum, a central campus of ETH Zurich
  • BMW Zentrum, a BMW museum in Spartanburg, South Carolina
  • Zentrum, a brand name for the amino acid Arginine 1200 mg.
