Undecaprenyl-phosphate glucose phosphotransferase

Undecaprenyl-phosphate glucose phosphotransferase (, GumD, undecaprenylphosphate glucosylphosphate transferase) is an enzyme with system name UDP-glucose:ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl-phosphate glucose phosphotransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

UDP-glucose + ditrans,octacis-undecaprenyl phosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons UMP + alpha-D-glucopyranosyl-diphospho-ditrans,octacis-undecaprenol

The enzyme is involved in biosynthesis of xanthan.
