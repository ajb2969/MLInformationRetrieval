Nitronate monooxygenase

Nitronate monooxygenase (, NMO) is an enzyme with system name nitronate:oxygen 2-oxidoreductase (nitrite-forming).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ethylnitronate + O2 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons acetaldehyde + nitrite + other products

The enzymes from the fungus Neurospora crassa and the yeast Williopsis saturnus var. mrakii contain non-covalently bound FMN as the cofactor.
