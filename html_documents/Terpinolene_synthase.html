Terpinolene synthase

Terpinolene synthase (, ag9, PmeTPS2, LaLIMS_RR) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, terpinolene-forming).12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons terpinolene + diphosphate

This enzyme requires Mg2+.
