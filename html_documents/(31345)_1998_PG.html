(31345) 1998 PG

(31345) 1998 PG is a near-earth object discovered by the LONEOS program on August 3, 1998. It is known to have a moon, S/2001 (31345) 1.1

Status as a Near-Earth Object

1998 PG is classified as an Amor asteroid. It has made multiple close approaches to Earth, with the closest being on 15 October 1978.2

Moon

1998 PG has a single moon, S/2001 (31345) 1. The moon was discovered at the Table Mountain Observatory by analyzing observations from 29 August to 25 October 1998.
