It is a papyrus manuscript of the Gospel of Matthew, it contains only Matthew 12:24-26.32-33. The manuscript paleographically had been assigned to the early 3rd century.1

Description

The manuscript is written in large upright uncial letters.2

The Greek text of this codex probably is a mixture of text-types. Aland placed it in Category III.3
