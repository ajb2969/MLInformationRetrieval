7-cyano-7-deazaguanine synthase

7-cyano-7-deazaguanine synthase (, preQ0 synthase, 7-cyano-7-carbaguanine synthase, queC (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 7-carboxy-7-carbaguanine:ammonia ligase (ADP-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

7-carboxy-7-carbaguanine + NH3 + ATP ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 7-cyano-7-carbaguanine + ADP + phosphate + H2O

This enzyme binds Zn2+.
