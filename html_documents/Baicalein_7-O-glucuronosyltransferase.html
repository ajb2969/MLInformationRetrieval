Baicalein 7-O-glucuronosyltransferase

Baicalein 7-O-glucuronosyltransferase (, UBGAT) is an enzyme with system name ''UDP-D-glucuronate:5,6,7-trihydroxyflavone 7-O-glucuronosyltransferase ''.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

UDP-D-glucuronate + baicalein ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons UDP + baicalin

The enzyme is specific for UDP-D-glucuronate as a sugar donor.
