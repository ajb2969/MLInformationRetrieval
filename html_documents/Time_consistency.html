Time consistency

Time consistency is a property in financial risk related to dynamic risk measures. The purpose of the time consistent property is to categorize the risk measures which satisfy the condition that if portfolio (A) is more risky than portfolio (B) at some time in the future, then it is guaranteed to be more risky at any time prior to that point. This is an important property since if it were not to hold then there is an event (with probability of occurring greater than 0) such that B is riskier than A at time t t t although it is certain that A is riskier than B at time t + 1 t 1 t+1 . As the name suggests a time inconsistent risk measure can lead to inconsistent behavior in financial risk management.

Dynamic superhedging price

The dynamic superhedging price is a time consistent risk measure.6

Dynamic entropic risk

The dynamic entropic risk measure is a time consistent risk measure if the risk aversion parameter is constant.7
