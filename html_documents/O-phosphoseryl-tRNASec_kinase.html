O-phosphoseryl-tRNASec kinase

O-phosphoseryl-tRNASec kinase (, PSTK, phosphoseryl-tRNA[Ser]Sec kinase, phosphoseryl-tRNASec kinase) is an enzyme with system name ATP:L-seryl-tRNASec O-phosphotransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + L-seryl-tRNASec ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + O-phospho-L-seryl-tRNASec

In archaea and eukarya selenocysteine formation is achieved by a two-step process.
