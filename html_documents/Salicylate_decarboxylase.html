Salicylate decarboxylase

Salicylate decarboxylase (, salicylic acid decarboxylase, Scd) is an enzyme with system name salicylate carboxy-lyase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

salicylate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons phenol + CO2

In the reverse direction the enzyme catalyses the regioselective carboxylation of phenol into salicylate.
