Peroxyureidoacrylate/ureidoacrylate amidohydrolase

Peroxyureidoacrylate/ureidoacrylate amidohydrolase (, RutB) is an enzyme with system name (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid amidohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (Z)-3-peroxyaminoacrylate + CO2 + NH3 (overall reaction)
(1a) (Z)-3-ureidoacrylate peracid + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (Z)-3-peroxyaminoacrylate + carbamate
(1b) carbamate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons CO2 + NH3 (spontaneous)
(2) (Z)-2-methylureidoacrylate peracid + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (Z)-2-methylperoxyaminoacrylate + CO2 + NH3 (overall reaction)
(2a) (Z)-2-methylureidoacrylate peracid + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (Z)-2-methylperoxyaminoacrylate + carbamate
(2b) carbamate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons CO2 + NH3 (spontaneous)

The enzyme also shows activity towards ureidoacrylate.
