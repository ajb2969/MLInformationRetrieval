1-Hydroxycarotenoid 3,4-desaturase

1-Hydroxycarotenoid 3,4-desaturase (, CrtD, hydroxyneurosporene desaturase, carotenoid 3,4-dehydrogenase, 1-hydroxy-carotenoid 3,4-dehydrogenase) is an enzyme with system name 1-hydroxy-1,2-dihydrolycopene:acceptor oxidoreductase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

1-hydroxy-1,2-dihydrolycopene + acceptor ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 1-hydroxy-3,4-didehydro-1,2-dihydrolycopene + reduced acceptor

The enzymes from Rubrivivax gelatinosus and Rhodobacter sphaeroides acts primarily on acyclic carotenoids.
