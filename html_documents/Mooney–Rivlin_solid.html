Rubber

Elastic response of rubber-like materials are often modeled based on the Mooney—Rivlin model. The constants C 1 , C 2 subscript C 1 subscript C 2 C_{1},C_{2} are determined by the fitting predicted stress from the above equations to experimental data. The recommended tests are uniaxial tension, equibiaxial compression, equibiaxial tension, uniaxial compression, and for shear, planar tension and planar compression. The two parameter Mooney–Rivlin model is usually valid for strains less than 100%.
