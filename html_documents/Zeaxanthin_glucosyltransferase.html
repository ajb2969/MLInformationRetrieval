Zeaxanthin glucosyltransferase

Zeaxanthin glucosyltransferase (, crtX (gene)) is an enzyme with system name UDP-glucose:zeaxanthin beta-D-glucosyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 UDP-glucose + zeaxanthin ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2 UDP + zeaxanthin bis(beta-D-glucoside)

The reaction proceeds in two steps with the monoglucoside as an intermediate.
