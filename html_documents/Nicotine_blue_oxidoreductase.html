Nicotine blue oxidoreductase

Nicotine blue oxidoreductase (, nboR (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 3,3'-bipyridine-2,2',5,5',6,6'-hexol:NADP+ 11-oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

3,3'-bipyridine-2,2',5,5',6,6'-hexol + NAD(P)+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (E)-2,2',5,5'-tetrahydroxy-6H,6'H-[3,3'-bipyridinylidene]-6,6'-dione + NAD(P)H + H+

This enzyme is extracted from bacterium Arthrobacter nicotinovorans.
