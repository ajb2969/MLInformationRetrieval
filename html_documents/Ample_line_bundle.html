Ample line bundle

In algebraic geometry, a very ample line bundle is one with enough global sections to set up an embedding of its base variety or manifold M M M into projective space. An ample line bundle is one such that some positive power is very ample. Globally generated sheaves are those with enough sections to define a morphism to projective space.
