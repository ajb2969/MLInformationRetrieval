Beta-carotene isomerase

Beta-carotene isomerase (, DWARF27 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name beta-carotene 9-cis-all-trans isomerase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

all-trans-beta-carotene ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 9-cis-beta-carotene

The enzyme participates in a pathway leading to biosynthesis of strigolactones.
