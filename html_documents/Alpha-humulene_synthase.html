Alpha-humulene synthase

Alpha-humulene synthase (, ZSS1) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (alpha-humulene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons alpha-humulene + diphosphate

The enzyme from Zingiber zerumbet, shampoo ginger, also gives traces of beta-caryophyllene.
