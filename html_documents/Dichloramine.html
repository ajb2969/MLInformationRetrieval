Dichloramine

Dichloramine is a reactive inorganic compound. It has the formula . The yellow gas is unstable and reacts with many materials.1 It is formed by a reaction between ammonia and chlorine or sodium hypochlorite. It is a byproduct formed during the synthesis of chloramine and nitrogen trichloride.
