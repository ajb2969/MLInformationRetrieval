This is a list of some vector calculus formulae for working with common curvilinear coordinate systems.

Notes

  • This article uses the standard notation ISO 80000-2, which supersedes ISO 31-11, for spherical coordinates (other sources may reverse the definitions of Œ∏ and œï):
    • The polar angle is denoted by Œ∏: it is the angle between the z-axis and the radial vector connecting the origin to the point in question.
    • The azimuthal angle is denoted by œï: it is the angle between the x-axis and the projection of the radial vector onto the xy-plane.
