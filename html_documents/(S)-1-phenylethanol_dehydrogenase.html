(S)-1-phenylethanol dehydrogenase

(S)-1-phenylethanol dehydrogenase (, PED) is an enzyme with system name (S)-1-phenylethanol:NAD+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(S)-1-phenylethanol + NAD+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons acetophenone + NADH + H+

The enzyme is involved in degradation of ethylbenzene.
