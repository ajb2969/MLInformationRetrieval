Last geometric statement of Jacobi

In differential geometry and algebraic geometry, the last geometric statement of Jacobi is a conjecture named after Carl Gustav Jacob Jacobi. According to this conjecture, every caustic from any point p p p on an ellipsoid other than umbilical points has exactly four cusps.
