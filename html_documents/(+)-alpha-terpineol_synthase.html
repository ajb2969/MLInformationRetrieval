(+)-alpha-terpineol synthase

(+)-alpha-terpineol synthase () is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, (+)-alpha-terpineol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (+)-alpha-terpineol + diphosphate

The enzyme has been characterized from Santalum album (sandalwood).
