Synchysite

Synchysite is a group of carbonate minerals.1 There are three isostructural chemical end members are Synchysite-(Ce), Synchysite-(Nd) and Synchysite-(Y). Huanghoite-(Ce) belongs to the group, but has a different symmetry and calcium is replaced by barium.23
