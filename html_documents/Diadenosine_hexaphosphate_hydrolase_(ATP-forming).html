Diadenosine hexaphosphate hydrolase (ATP-forming)

Diadenosine hexaphosphate hydrolase (ATP-forming) (, Ndx1) is an enzyme with system name P1,P6-bis(5'-adenosyl)hexaphosphate nucleotidohydrolase (ATP-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) P1,P6-bis(5'-adenosyl)hexaphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2 ATP
(2) P1,P5-bis(5'-adenosyl)pentaphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ATP + ADP
(3) P1,P4-bis(5'-adenosyl)tetraphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ATP + AMP

The enzyme requires the presence of the divalent cations (Mn2+, Mg2+, Zn2+, and Co2+).
