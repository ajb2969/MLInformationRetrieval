Sulfoacetaldehyde reductase

Sulfoacetaldehyde reductase (, ISFD) is an enzyme with system name isethionate:NADP+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

isethionate + NADP+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2-sulfoacetaldehyde + NADPH + H+

This enzyme catalyses the reaction in the reverse direction.
