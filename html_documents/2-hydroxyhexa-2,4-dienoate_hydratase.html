2-hydroxyhexa-2,4-dienoate hydratase

2-hydroxyhexa-2,4-dienoate hydratase (, tesE (gene), hsaE (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 4-hydroxy-2-oxohexanoate hydro-lyase ((2Z,4Z)-2-hydroxyhexa-2,4-dienoate-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

4-hydroxy-2-oxohexanoate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (2Z,4Z)-2-hydroxyhexa-2,4-dienoate + H2O

This enzyme catalyses a late step in the bacterial steroid degradation pathway.
