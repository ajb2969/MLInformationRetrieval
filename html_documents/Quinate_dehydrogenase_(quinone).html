Quinate dehydrogenase (quinone)

Quinate dehydrogenase (quinone) (, NAD(P)+-independent quinate dehydrogenase, quinate:pyrroloquinoline-quinone 5-oxidoreductase) is an enzyme with system name quinate:quinol 3-oxidoreductase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

quinate + quinone ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 3-dehydroquinate + quinol

This enzyme is membrane-bound.
