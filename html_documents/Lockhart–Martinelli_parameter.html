Lockhart–Martinelli parameter

The Lockhart–Martinelli parameter ( χ χ \chi ) is a dimensionless number used in internal two-phase flow calculations.1 It expresses the liquid fraction of a flowing fluid. Its main application is in two-phase pressure drop and boiling/condensing heat transfer calculations.
