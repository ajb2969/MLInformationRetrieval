DTDP-6-deoxy-L-talose 4-dehydrogenase (NAD+)

DTDP-6-deoxy-L-talose 4-dehydrogenase (NAD+) (, tll (gene name)) is an enzyme with system name dTDP-6-deoxy-beta-L-talose:NAD+ 4-oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

dTDP-6-deoxy-beta-L-talose + NAD+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons dTDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-beta-L-mannose + NADH + H+

The enzyme from bacterium Aggregatibacter actinomycetemcomitans participates in the biosynthesis of the serotype c-specific polysaccharide antigen.
