Coenzyme F420-0:L-glutamate ligase

Coenzyme F420-0:L-glutamate ligase (, CofE-AF, MJ0768, CofE) is an enzyme with system name L-glutamate:coenzyme F420-0 ligase (GDP-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

GTP + coenzyme F420-0 + L-glutamate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons GDP + phosphate + coenzyme F420-1

This protein catalyses the successive addition of two glutamate residues to cofactor F420 by two distinct and independent reactions.
