TDP-4-oxo-6-deoxy-alpha-D-glucose-3,4-oxoisomerase (dTDP-3-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-galactopyranose-forming)

TDP-4-oxo-6-deoxy-alpha-D-glucose-3,4-oxoisomerase (dTDP-3-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-galactopyranose-forming) (, dTDP-6-deoxy-hex-4-ulose isomerase, TDP-6-deoxy-hex-4-ulose isomerase, FdtA) is an enzyme with system name dTDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranose:dTDP-3-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-galactopyranose isomerase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

dTDP-4-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranose ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons dTDP-3-dehydro-6-deoxy-alpha-D-galactopyranose

The enzyme is involved in the biosynthesis of dTDP-3-acetamido-3,6-dideoxy-alpha-D-galactose.
