145 (number)

145 (one hundred [and] forty-five) is the natural number following 144 and preceding 146.

In mathematics

  • Although composite, 145 is a pseudoprime.
  • Given 145, the Mertens function returns 0.
  • 145 is a pentagonal number and a centered square number.
  • 145 = 12 2 + 1 2 = 8 2 + 9 2 145 superscript 12 2 superscript 1 2 superscript 8 2 superscript 9 2 145=12^{2}+1^{2}=8^{2}+9^{2} . 145 is the fourth number that is the sum of two different pairs of squares. Also, 145 is the result of 34 + 43, making it a Leyland number.
  • 145 = 1 ! + 4 ! + 5 ! 145 1 4 5 145=1!+4!+5! , making it a factorion. The only other numbers that have the property that they are the sum of the factorials of their digits are 1, 2 and 40585.
