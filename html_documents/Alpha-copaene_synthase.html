Alpha-copaene synthase

Alpha-copaene synthase () is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, alpha-copaene-forming).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (-)-alpha-copaene + diphosphate

This enzyme is isolated from Helianthus annuus (sunflower).
