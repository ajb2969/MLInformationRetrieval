4-Methylaminobutanoate oxidase (methylamine-forming)

4-methylaminobutanoate oxidase (methylamine-forming) (, mao (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 4-methylaminobutanoate methylamidohydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

4-methylaminobutanoate + O2 + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons succinate semialdehyde + methylamine + H2O2

The enzyme participates in the nicotine degradation in soil bacterium Arthrobacter nicotinovorans.
