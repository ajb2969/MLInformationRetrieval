(1-4)-a-D-glucan 1-a-D-glucosylmutase

(1->4)-alpha-D-glucan 1-alpha-D-glucosylmutase (, malto-oligosyltrehalose synthase, maltodextrin alpha-D-glucosyltransferase) is an enzyme with system name (1->4)-alpha-D-glucan 1-alpha-D-glucosylmutase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

4-[(1->4)-alpha-D-glucosyl]n-1-D-glucose ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 1-alpha-D-[(1->4)-alpha-D-glucosyl]n-1-alpha-D-glucopyranoside

The enzyme from Arthrobacter sp., Sulfolobus acidocaldarius acts on (1->4)-alpha-D-glucans containing three or more (1->4)-alpha-linked D-glucose units.
