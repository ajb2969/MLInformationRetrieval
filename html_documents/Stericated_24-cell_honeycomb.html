It can be seen as a sterication of the regular 24-cell honeycomb, containing 24-cell, 16-cell, octahedral prism, tetrahedral prism, and 3-3 duoprism cells.

Alternate names

  • Stericated icositetrachoric/hexadecaachoric tetracomb/honeycomb
