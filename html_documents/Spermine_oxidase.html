Spermine oxidase

Spermine oxidase (, PAOh1/SMO, AtPAO1, AtPAO4, SMO) is an enzyme with system name spermidine:oxygen oxidoreductase (spermidine-forming).1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

spermine + O2 + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons spermidine + 3-aminopropanal + H2O2

The enzyme from Arabidopsis thaliana oxidizes norspermine to norspermidine.
