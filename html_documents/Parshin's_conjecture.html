Points and curves

The conjecture holds for a finite field by Quillen's computations of the K-groups in this case. Secondly, for a smooth proper curve, Quillen1 has shown that the K-groups are finitely generated, while Harder's computations2 show that the groups are torsion. The two results together thus show Parshin's conjecture for curves.
