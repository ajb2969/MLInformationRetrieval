Hygromycin B 4-O-kinase

Hygromycin B 4-O-kinase (, hygromycin-B kinase) is an enzyme with system name ATP:hygromycin-B 4-O-phosphotransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + hygromycin B ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + 4-O-phosphohygromycin B

Phosphorylates the antibiotic hygromycin B.
