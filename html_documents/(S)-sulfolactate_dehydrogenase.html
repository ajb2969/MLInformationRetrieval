(S)-sulfolactate dehydrogenase

(S)-sulfolactate dehydrogenase (, (2S)-3-sulfolactate dehydrogenase, SlcC) is an enzyme with system name (2S)-sulfolactate:NAD+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2S)-3-sulfolactate + NAD+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 3-sulfopyruvate + NADH + H+

This bacterial (Chromohalobacter salexigens) enzyme acts on the 3-sulfolactate.
