Beta-phellandrene synthase (neryl-diphosphate-cyclizing)

Beta-phellandrene synthase (neryl-diphosphate-cyclizing) (, phellandrene synthase 1, PHS1, monoterpene synthase PHS1) is an enzyme with system name neryl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing; beta-phellandrene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

neryl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons beta-phellandrene + diphosphate

The enzyme from Solanum lycopersicum has very poor affinity with geranyl diphosphate.
