Mandelate racemase

Mandelate racemase () is a bacterial enzyme which catalyzes the interconversion of the enantiomers of mandelate via an enol intermediate.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(S)-mandelate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (R)-mandelate

It is a member of the enolase superfamily of enzymes, along with muconate lactonizing enzyme and enolase.
