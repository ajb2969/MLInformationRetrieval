Mn2+-dependent ADP-ribose/CDP-alcohol diphosphatase

Mn2+-dependent ADP-ribose/CDP-alcohol diphosphatase (, Mn2+-dependent ADP-ribose/CDP-alcohol pyrophosphatase, ADPRibase-Mn) is an enzyme with system name CDP-choline phosphohydrolase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) CDP-choline + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons CMP + phosphocholine
(2) ADP-ribose + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons AMP + D-ribose 5-phosphate

This enzyme requires Mn2+, which cannot be replaced by Mg2+.
