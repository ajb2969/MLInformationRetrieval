In mathematics, a Preradical is a subfunctor of the identity functor in the category of left modules over a ring with identity. The class of all preradicals over R-mod is denoted by R-pr. Theres is a natural order in R-pr given by, for any two preradicals σ σ \sigma and τ τ \tau , σ ≤ τ σ τ \sigma\leq\tau , if for any left R-module M, σ M ≤ τ M σ M τ M \sigma M\leq\tau M . With this order R-pr becomes a big lattice.
