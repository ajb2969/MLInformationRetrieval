Antiplane shear

Antiplane shear or antiplane strain1 is a special state of strain in a body. This state of strain is achieved when the displacements in the body are zero in the plane of interest but nonzero in the direction perpendicular to the plane. Applications

The antiplane shear assumption is used to determine the stresses and displacements due to a screw dislocation.
