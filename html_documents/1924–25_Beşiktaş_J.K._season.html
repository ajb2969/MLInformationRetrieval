1925-26 |} The 1924-25 season was the club's 5th official football season and their 22nd year in existence. They qualified for the playoffs, but lost to Galatasaray in the semi finals 1-6.

İstanbul Football League

Beşiktaş finished in the top 4, qualifying for the playoffs, along with Galatasaray, Vefa S.K. and Anadolu Üsküdar 1908.
