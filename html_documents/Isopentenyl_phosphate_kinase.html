Isopentenyl phosphate kinase

Isopentenyl phosphate kinase () is an enzyme with system name ATP:isopentenyl phosphate phosphotransferase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + isopentenyl phosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + isopentenyl diphosphate

This enzyme takes part in the mevalonate pathway in Archaea.
