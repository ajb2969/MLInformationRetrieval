Molybdopterin adenylyltransferase

Molybdopterin adenylyltransferase (, MogA, Cnx1) is an enzyme with system name ATP:molybdopterin adenylyltransferase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + molybdopterin ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons diphosphate + adenylyl-molybdopterin

This enzyme catalyses the activation of molybdopterin for molybdenum insertion.
