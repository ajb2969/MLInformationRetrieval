Cyclohexane-1,2-dione hydrolase

Cyclohexane-1,2-dione hydrolase () is an enzyme with system name cyclohexane-1,2-dione acylhydrolase (decyclizing).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

cyclohexane-1,2-dione + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 6-oxohexanoate

Thia enzyme is highly specific.
