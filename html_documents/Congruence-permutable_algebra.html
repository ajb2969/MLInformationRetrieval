Congruence-permutable algebra

In universal algebra, two congruences α , β α β \alpha,\beta are said to be permutable if α ∘ β = β ∘ α α β β α \alpha\circ\beta=\beta\circ\alpha . A congruence-permutable algebra is one in which all congruences are permutable. A variety of algebras is congruence-permutable if every algebra in the variety is congruence-permutable.
