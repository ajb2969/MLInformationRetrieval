Verma module

Verma modules, named after Daya-Nand Verma, are objects in the representation theory of Lie algebras, a branch of mathematics.

Verma modules can be used to prove that an irreducible highest weight module with highest weight λ λ \lambda is finite-dimensional, if and only if the weight λ λ \lambda is dominant and integral. Their homomorphisms correspond to invariant differential operators over flag manifolds.
