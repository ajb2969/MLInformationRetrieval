DATP(dGTP)—DNA purinetransferase

DATP(dGTP)---DNA purinetransferase () is an enzyme with system name dATP(dGTP):depurinated-DNA purine transferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) dATP + depurinated DNA ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons deoxyribose triphosphate + DNA
(2) dGTP + depurinated DNA ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons deoxyribose triphosphate + DNA

The purine residue is transferred on to the apurinic site forming a normal glycosylic bond.
