Beta-farnesene synthase

Beta-farnesene synthase (, farnesene synthase, terpene synthase 10, terpene synthase 10-B73, TPS10) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((E)-beta-farnesene-forming).12345678 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (E)-beta-farnesene + diphosphate
