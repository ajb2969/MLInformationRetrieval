(+)-alpha-santalene synthase ((2Z,6Z)-farnesyl diphosphate cyclizing)

(+)-alpha-santalene synthase ((2Z,6Z)-farnesyl diphosphate cyclizing) (, SBS) is an enzyme with system name (2Z,6Z)-farnesyl diphosphate lyase (cyclizing; (+)-alpha-santalene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2Z,6Z)-farnesyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (+)-alpha-santalene + diphosphate

The enzyme synthesizes a mixture of sesquiterpenoids from (2Z,6Z)-farnesyl diphosphate.
