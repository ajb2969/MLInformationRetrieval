3',5'-cyclic-AMP phosphodiesterase

3',5'-cyclic-AMP phosphodiesterase (, cAMP-specific phosphodiesterase, cAMP-specific PDE, PDE1, PDE2A, PDE2B, PDE4, PDE7, PDE8, PDEB1, PDEB2) is an enzyme with system name 3',5'-cyclic-AMP 5'-nucleotidohydrolase.123456 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

adenosine 3',5'-cyclic phosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons AMP

This enzyme requires Mg2+ or Mn2+ for activity.
