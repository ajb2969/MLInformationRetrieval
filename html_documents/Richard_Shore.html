Richard Shore

Richard A. Shore is a professor of mathematics at Cornell University who works in recursion theory. He is particularly known for his work on 𝒟 𝒟 \mathcal{D} , the partial order of the Turing degrees.

  • In joint work with Theodore Slaman, Shore showed that the Turing jump is definable in 𝒟 𝒟 \mathcal{D} .2

In 2012 he became a fellow of the American Mathematical Society.3
