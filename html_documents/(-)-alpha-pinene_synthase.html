(-)-alpha-pinene synthase

(-)-alpha-pinene synthase (, (-)-alpha-pinene/(-)-camphene synthase, (-)-alpha-pinene cyclase) is an enzyme with system name geranyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, (-)-alpha-pinene-forming).1234567891011121314 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (-)-alpha-pinene + diphosphate

Cyclase II of Salvia officinalis (sage) gives about equal parts (-)-alpha-pinene, (-)-beta-pinene and (-)-camphene.
