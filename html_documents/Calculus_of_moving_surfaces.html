Calculus of moving surfaces

The calculus of moving surfaces (CMS) 1 is an extension of the classical tensor calculus to deforming manifolds. Central to the CMS is the δ / δ t δ δ t \delta/\delta t -derivative whose original definition 2 was put forth by Jacques Hadamard. In particular, it has the property that it produces a tensor when applied to a tensor.
