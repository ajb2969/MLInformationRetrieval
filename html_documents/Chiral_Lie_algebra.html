Chiral Lie algebra

In algebra, a chiral Lie algebra is a D-module on a curve with a certain structure of Lie algebra. It is related to an ℰ 2 subscript ℰ 2 \mathcal{E}_{2} -algebra via the Riemann–Hilbert correspondence.
