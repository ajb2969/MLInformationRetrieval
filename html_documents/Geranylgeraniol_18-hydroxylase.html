Geranylgeraniol 18-hydroxylase

Geranylgeraniol 18-hydroxylase (, GGOH-18-hydroxylase) is an enzyme with system name geranylgeraniol,NADPH:oxygen oxidoreductase (18-hydroxylating).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

geranylgeraniol + NADPH + H+ + O2 ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 18-hydroxygeranylgeraniol + NADP+ + H2O

Geranylgeraniol 18-hydroxylase is a heme-thiolate protein (P-450).
