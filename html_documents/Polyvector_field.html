Polyvector field

In mathematics, a multivector field, polyvector field of '''degree k ''', or ''' k-vector field''', on a manifold M, is a section of the kth exterior power of the tangent bundle, Λ k T M superscript normal-Λ k T M \Lambda^{k}TM . Polyvector fields of degree k are dual to k-forms.
