This is for a producing inner products of quantum states in bra–ket notation, using wikicode, ideally with , as an alternative to LaTeX in mode.

Application

There are two parameters, which is the text to be displayed as the bra and ket labels.

Examples

The inner product of the kets $| ξ \rangle$ and $| ψ \rangle$ can be written: $\langle ψ ξ \rangle$ = $\langle ξ ψ \rangle$†, that is $\langle ψ ξ \rangle$ = $\langle ξ ψ \rangle$†.

Using , the inner product can be written: $\langle ψ ξ \rangle$, that is $\langle ψ ξ \rangle$.
