8-oxoguanine deaminase

8-oxoguanine deaminase (, 8-OGD) is an enzyme with system name 8-oxoguanine aminohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

8-oxoguanine + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons urate + NH3

Zn2+ is bound in the active site.
