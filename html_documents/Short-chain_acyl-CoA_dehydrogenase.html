Short-chain acyl-CoA dehydrogenase

a short-chain acyl-CoA + electron-transfer flavoprotein ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons a short-chain trans-2,3-dehydroacyl-CoA + reduced electron-transfer flavoprotein
