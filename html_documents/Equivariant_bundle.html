Equivariant bundle

In differential geometry, given a compact Lie group G, an equivariant bundle is a fiber bundle such that the total space and the base spaces are both G-spaces and the projection map π π \pi between them is equivariant π ∘ g = g ∘ π π g g π \pi\circ g=g\circ\pi with some extra requirement depending on a typical fiber.

For example, an equivariant vector bundle is an equivariant bundle.
