Fermat quintic threefold

In mathematics, a Fermat quintic threefold is a special quintic threefold, in other words a degree 5 dimension 3 hypersurface in 4-dimensional projective space, given by

V 5 + W 5 + X 5 + Y 5 + Z 5 = 0. superscript V 5 superscript W 5 superscript X 5 superscript Y 5 superscript Z 5 0. V^{5}+W^{5}+X^{5}+Y^{5}+Z^{5}=0.\,

It is a Calabi–Yau manifold.

The Fermat quintic threefold is not generic in this sense, and showed that its lines are contained in 50 1-dimensional families of the form (x : −ζx : ay : by : cy) for ζ 5 = 1 and a5 + b5 + c5 = 0.
