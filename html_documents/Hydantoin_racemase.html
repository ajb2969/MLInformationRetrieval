Hydantoin racemase

Hydantoin racemase (, 5'-monosubstituted-hydantoin racemase, HyuA, HyuE) is an enzyme with system name D-5-monosubstituted-hydantoin racemase.1234567 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

D-5-monosubstituted hydantoin ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons L-5-monosubstituted hydantoin

This enzyme is a part of the reaction cascade known as the "hydantoinase process".
