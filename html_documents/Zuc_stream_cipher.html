Zuc stream cipher

ZUC is a new stream cipher due for possible inclusion in the Long Term Evolution standards for mobile devices.1

It uses a 16-stage Linear Feedback Shift Register (LFSR) with each stage in GF( 2 31 - 1 superscript 2 31 1 2^{31}-1 ) and produces a 32-bit word on each tick.
