2-oxoglutaramate amidase

2-oxoglutaramate amidase (, omega-amidase) is an enzyme with system name 5-amino-2,5-dioxopentanoate amidohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-oxoglutaramate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2-oxoglutarate + ammonia

The enzyme participates in the nicotine degradation pathway of several Gram-positive bacteria.
