Draft:Krein space

In mathematics, a Krein space is a type of indefinite inner product space. The inner product of a Krein space is the difference of two positive definite inner products. Krein spaces are named in honor of the Ukrainian mathematician Mark Grigorievich Krein (3 April 1907 – 17 October 1989).
