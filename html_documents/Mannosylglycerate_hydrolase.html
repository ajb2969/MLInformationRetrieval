Mannosylglycerate hydrolase

Mannosylglycerate hydrolase (, 2-O-(6-phospho-mannosyl)-D-glycerate hydrolase, alpha-mannosidase, mngB (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 2-O-(6-phospho-alpha-D-mannosyl)-D-glycerate acylhydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-O-(6-phospho-alpha-D-mannosyl)-D-glycerate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons D-mannose 6-phosphate + D-glycerate

The enzyme participates in the mannosylglycerate degradation pathway of some bacteria.
