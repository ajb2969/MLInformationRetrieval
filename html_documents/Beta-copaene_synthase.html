Beta-copaene synthase

Beta-copaene synthase (, cop4) is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, beta-copaene-forming).123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons beta-copaene + diphosphate

This enzyme is isolated from the fungus Coprinus cinereus.
