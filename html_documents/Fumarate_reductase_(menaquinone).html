Fumarate reductase (menaquinone)

Fumarate reductase (menaquinone) (, FRD, menaquinol-fumarate oxidoreductase, succinate dehydrogenase (menaquinone)) is an enzyme with system name succinate:menaquinone oxidoreductase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

succinate + a menaquinone ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons fumarate + a menaquinol

The reaction is catalysed in the opposite direction.
