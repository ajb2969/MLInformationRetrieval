Valine dehydrogenase (NAD+)

Valine dehydrogenase (NAD+) () is an enzyme with system name L-valine:NAD+ oxidoreductase (deaminating).12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

L-valine + H2O + NAD+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 3-methyl-2-oxobutanoate + NH3 + NADH + H+

The enzyme from Streptomyces spp. has no activity with NADP+.
