Vertical vector field

In differential geometry, a vertical vector field is a vector field on a principal G-bundle π : P → M normal-: π normal-→ P M \pi:P\to M that is in the kernel of d π d π d\pi at each point of P.1 More generally, the projection π : P → M normal-: π normal-→ P M \pi:P\to M needs only to be a fibered manifold, i.e., a surjective submersion.
