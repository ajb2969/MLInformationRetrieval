DTDP-3-amino-3,6-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranose N,N-dimethyltransferase

DTDP-3-amino-3,6-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranose N,N-dimethyltransferase (, TylM1) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:dTDP-3-amino-3,6-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranose 3-N,N-dimethyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 S-adenosyl-L-methionine + dTDP-3-amino-3,6-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranose ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2 S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + dTDP-3-dimethylamino-3,6-dideoxy-alpha-D-glucopyranose

The enzyme is involved in the biosynthesis of mycaminose.
