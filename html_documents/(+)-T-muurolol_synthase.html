(+)-T-muurolol synthase

(+)-T-muurolol synthase () is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase (cyclizing, (+)-T-muurolol-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (+)-T-muurolol + diphosphate

The cyclization mechanism involves an intermediate nerolidyl diphosphate.
