Musical isomorphism

In mathematics, the musical isomorphism (or canonical isomorphism) is an isomorphism between the tangent bundle T M T M TM and the cotangent bundle of a Riemannian manifold given by its metric. There are similar isomorphisms on symplectic manifolds. The term musical refers to the use of the symbols ♭ and ♯.1

It is also known as raising and lowering indices.
