TRNA (cytidine56-2'-O)-methyltransferase

tRNA (cytidine56-2'-O)-methyltransferase (, aTrm56, tRNA ribose 2'-O-methyltransferase aTrm56, PAB1040 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:tRNA (cytidine56-2'-O)-methyltransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-adenosyl-L-methionine + cytidine56 in tRNA ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + 2'-O-methylcytidine56 in tRNA

The archaeal enzyme specifically catalyses the S-adenosyl-L-methionine dependent 2'-O-ribose methylation of cytidine at position 56 in tRNA transcripts.
