Geranylgeranyl diphosphate reductase

Geranylgeranyl diphosphate reductase (, geranylgeranyl reductase, CHL P) is an enzyme with system name geranylgeranyl-diphosphate:NADP+ oxidoreductase.123 This enzyme catalises the following chemical reaction

phytyl diphosphate + 3 NADP+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons geranylgeranyl diphosphate + 3 NADPH + 3 H+

This enzyme also acts on geranylgeranyl-chlorophyll a.
