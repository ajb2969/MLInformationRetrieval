Isopimara-7,15-diene synthase

Isopimara-7,15-diene synthase (, PaTPS-Iso, copalyl diphosphate-lyase (isopimara-7,15-diene-forming)) is an enzyme with system name (+)-copalyl diphosphate-lyase (isopimara-7,15-diene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(+)-copalyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons isopimara-7,15-diene + diphosphate

The enzyme only forms isopimara-7,15-diene.
