Each vertex is shared by 12 5-simplexes, 30 rectified 5-simplexes, and 20 birectified 5-simplexes. These facet types occur in proportions of 2:2:1 respectively in the whole honeycomb.

A5 lattice

This vertex arrangement is called the A5 lattice or 5-simplex lattice. The 30 vertices of the stericated 5-simplex vertex figure represent the 30 roots of the A ~ 5 subscript normal-~ A 5 {\tilde{A}}_{5} Coxeter group.1 It is the 5-dimensional case of a simplectic honeycomb.
