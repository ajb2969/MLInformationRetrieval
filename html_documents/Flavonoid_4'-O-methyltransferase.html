Flavonoid 4'-O-methyltransferase

Flavonoid 4'-O-methyltransferase (, SOMT-2, 4'-hydroxyisoflavone methyltransferase) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:flavonoid 4'-O-methyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-adenosyl-L-methionine + 4'-hydroxyflavanone ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + 4'-methoxyflavanone

The enzyme catalyses the 4'-methylation of naringenin.
