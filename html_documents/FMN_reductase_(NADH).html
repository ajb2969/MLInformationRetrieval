FMN reductase (NADH)

FMN reductase (NADH) (, NADH-FMN reductase) is an enzyme with system name FMNH2:NAD+ oxidoreductase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

FMNH2 + NAD+ $\rightleftharpoons$ FMN + NADH + H+

The enzyme often forms a complex with monooxygenases.
