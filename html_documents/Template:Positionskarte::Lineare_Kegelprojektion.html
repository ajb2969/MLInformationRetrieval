This generic template allows the creation of position maps in linear conical projection.

Kopiervorlage

{{Positionskarte/Lineare Kegelprojektion
|{{{1}}}|{{{2}}}|{{{3}}}
|latitude=
|longitude=
|top=
|bottom=
|n=
|latitudeG=
|dx=
|px0=
|image=
|name=
}}

Parameters

Requested parameter
Latitude (between -90° and +90°)
Longitude (between -180° and +180°)
longitude
Longitude of the centre of the projection (central meridian)
top
Latitude of the upper edge of the map at the central meridian
bottom
Latitude of the lower edge of the map at the central meridian
n
Coefficient, that corresponds to the splay of the meridians. The standard value is n = sin ( latitude × π / 180 ) n latitude π 180 n=\sin(\rm{latitude}\times\pi/180) .
latitudeG
Latitude at which the meridians intersect. If latitudeG=90, this may be omitted.
dx
Horizontal scaling factor. If dx=1, this may be omitted.
px0
Horizontal position of the central meridian as a percentage of map width. If px0=50, this may be omitted.
