Lin–Tsien equation

The Lin–Tsien equation (named after C. C. Lin and H. S. Tsien) is the partial differential equation

2 u t x + u x u x x - u y y = 0. 2 subscript u t x subscript u x subscript u x x subscript u y y 0. 2u_{tx}+u_{x}u_{xx}-u_{yy}=0.\,
