251 (number)

251 (two hundred [and] fifty-one) is the natural number between 250 and 252. It is also a prime number.

In mathematics

251 is a Sophie Germain prime.1 Every 5 × 5 matrix has exactly 251 square submatrices.2 251 is a de Polignac number, meaning that it is odd and cannot be formed by adding a power of two to a prime number.34 It is the smallest number that can be formed in more than one way by summing three positive cubes:56
