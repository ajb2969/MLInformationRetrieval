L-threonine kinase

L-threonine kinase (, PduX) is an enzyme with system name ATP:L-threonine O3-phosphotransferase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

ATP + L-threonine ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons ADP + O-phospho-L-threonine

The enzyme takes part in the synthesis of adenosylcobalamin.
