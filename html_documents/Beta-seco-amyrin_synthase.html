Beta-seco-amyrin synthase

Beta-seco-amyrin synthase () is an enzyme with system name (3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene mutase (cyclizing, beta-seco-amyrin-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(3S)-2,3-epoxy-2,3-dihydrosqualene ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons beta-seco-amyrin

The enzyme from Arabidopsis thaliana is multifunctional.
