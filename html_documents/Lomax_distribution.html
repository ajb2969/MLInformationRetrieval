The Lomax distribution, conditionally also called the Pareto Type II distribution, is a heavy-tail probability distribution often used in business, economics, and actuarial modeling.12 It is named after K. S. Lomax. It is essentially a Pareto distribution that has been shifted so that its support begins at zero.3
