4-Sulfomuconolactone hydrolase

4-sulfomuconolactone hydrolase () is an enzyme with system name 4-sulfomuconolactone sulfohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

4-sulfomuconolactone + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons maleylacetate + sulfite

The enzyme was isolated from the bacteria Hydrogenophaga intermedia and Agrobacterium radiobacter S2.
