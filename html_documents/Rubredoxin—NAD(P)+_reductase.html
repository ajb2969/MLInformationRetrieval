Rubredoxin—NAD(P)+ reductase

Rubredoxin—NAD(P)+ reductase (, rubredoxin-nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide (phosphate) reductase, rubredoxin-nicotinamide adenine, dinucleotide phosphate reductase, NAD(P)+-rubredoxin oxidoreductase, NAD(P)H-rubredoxin oxidoreductase) is an enzyme with system name rubredoxin:NAD(P)+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2 reduced rubredoxin + NAD(P)+ + H+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 2 oxidized rubredoxin + NAD(P)H

The enzyme from Pyrococcus furiosus requires FAD.
