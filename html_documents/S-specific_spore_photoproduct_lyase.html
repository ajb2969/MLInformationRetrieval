S-specific spore photoproduct lyase

S-specific spore photoproduct lyase (, SAM, SP lyase, SPL, SplB, SplG) is an enzyme with system name S-specific spore photoproduct pyrimidine-lyase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(5S)-5,6-dihydro-5-(thymidin-7-yl)thymidine (in DNA) + S-adenosyl-L-methionine ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons thymidylyl-(3'->5')-thymidylate (in DNA) + 5'-deoxyadenosine + L-methionine

This enzyme is an iron-sulfur protein.
