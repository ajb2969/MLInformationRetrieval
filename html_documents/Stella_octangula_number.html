Stella octangula number

In mathematics, a stella octangula number is a figurate number based on the stella octangula, of the form n(2n2 − 1).12

The sequence of stella octangula numbers begins

0, 1, 14, 51, 124, 245, 426, 679, 1016, 1449, 1990, ....3
