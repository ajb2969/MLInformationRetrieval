Site-specific DNA-methyltransferase (cytosine-N4-specific)

Site-specific DNA-methyltransferase (cytosine-N4-specific) (, modification methylase, restriction-modification system, DNA[cytosine-N4]methyltransferase, m4C-forming MTase, S-adenosyl-L-methionine:DNA-cytosine 4-N-methyltransferase) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:DNA-cytosine N4-methyltransferase.1234 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-adenosyl-L-methionine + DNA cytosine ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + DNA N4-methylcytosine
