Saturation (graph theory)

Let G ( V , E ) G V E G(V,E) be a graph and M M M a matching in G G G . A vertex v ∈ V ( G ) v V G v\in V(G) is said to be saturated by M M M if there is an edge in M M M incident to v v v . A vertex v ∈ V ( G ) v V G v\in V(G) with no such edge is said to be unsaturated by M M M . We also say that M M M saturates v v v .1
