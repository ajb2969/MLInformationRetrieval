Botryococcus squalene synthase

Botryococcus squalene synthase (, SSL-2 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name squalene:NADP+ oxidoreductase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

squalene + diphosphate + NADP+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons presqualene diphosphate + NADPH + H+

This enzyme is solated from the green alga Botryococcus braunii BOT22.
