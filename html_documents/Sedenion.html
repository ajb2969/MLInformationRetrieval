Arithmetic

Like octonions, multiplication of sedenions is neither commutative nor associative. But in contrast to the octonions, the sedenions do not even have the property of being alternative. They do, however, have the property of power associativity, which can be stated as for any element x of ùïä ùïä \mathbb{S} , the power x n superscript x n x^{n} is well-defined. They are also flexible.
