TRNA-dihydrouridine16/17 synthase (NAD(P)+)

TRNA-dihydrouridine16/17 synthase (NAD(P)+) (, Dus1p, tRNA-dihydrouridine synthase 1) is an enzyme with system name tRNA-5,6-dihydrouracil16/17:NAD(P)+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(1) 5,6-dihydrouracil16 in tRNA + NAD(P)+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons uracil16 in tRNA + NAD(P)H + H+
(2) 5,6-dihydrouracil17 in tRNA + NAD(P)+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons uracil17 in tRNA + NAD(P)H + H+

This enzyme specifically modifies uracil16 and uracil17 in tRNA.
