(1) 4-aminobutanoate + pyruvate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons succinate semialdehyde + L-alanine
(2) 4-aminobutanoate + glyoxylate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons succinate semialdehyde + glycine
