Brocard's conjecture

In number theory, Brocard's conjecture is a conjecture that there are at least four prime numbers between (pn)2 and (pn+1)2, for n > 1, where pn is the nth prime number.1 It is widely believed that this conjecture is true. However, it remains unproven as of March 2015.
