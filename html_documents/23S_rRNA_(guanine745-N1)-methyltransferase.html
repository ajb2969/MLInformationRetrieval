23S rRNA (guanine745-N1)-methyltransferase

23S rRNA (guanine745-N1)-methyltransferase (, Rlma(I), Rlma1, 23S rRNA m1G745 methyltransferase, YebH, RlmAI methyltransferase, ribosomal RNA(m1G)-methylase, rRNA(m1G)methylase, RrmA, 23S rRNA:m1G745 methyltransferase) is an enzyme with system name S-adenosyl-L-methionine:23S rRNA (guanine745-N1)-methyltransferase.12345 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-adenosyl-L-methionine + guanine745 in 23S rRNA ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons S-adenosyl-L-homocysteine + N1-methylguanine745 in 23S rRNA

The enzyme specifically methylates guanine745 at N1 in 23S rRNA.
