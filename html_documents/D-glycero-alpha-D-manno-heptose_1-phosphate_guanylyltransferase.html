D-glycero-alpha-D-manno-heptose 1-phosphate guanylyltransferase

D-glycero-alpha-D-manno-heptose 1-phosphate guanylyltransferase (, hddC (gene), gmhD (gene)) is an enzyme with system name GTP:D-glycero-alpha-D-manno-heptose 1-phosphate guanylyltransferase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

D-glycero-alpha-D-manno-heptose 1-phosphate + GTP ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons GDP-D-glycero-alpha-D-manno-heptose + diphosphate

The enzyme is involved in biosynthesis of GDP-D-glycero-alpha-D-manno-heptose.
