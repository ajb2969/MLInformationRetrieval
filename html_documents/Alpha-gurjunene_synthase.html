Alpha-gurjunene synthase

Alpha-gurjunene synthase () is an enzyme with system name (2E,6E)-farnesyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((-)-alpha-gurjunene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(2E,6E)-farnesyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (-)-alpha-gurjunene + diphosphate

Initial cyclization probably gives biyclogermacrene in an enzyme bound form.
