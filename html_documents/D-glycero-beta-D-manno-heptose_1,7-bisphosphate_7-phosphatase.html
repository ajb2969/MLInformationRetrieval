D-glycero-beta-D-manno-heptose 1,7-bisphosphate 7-phosphatase

D-glycero-beta-D-manno-heptose 1,7-bisphosphate 7-phosphatase (, gmhB (gene), yaeD (gene)) is an enzyme with system name D-glycero-beta-D-manno-heptose 1,7-bisphosphate 7-phosphohydrolase.123 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

D-glycero-beta-D-manno-heptose 1,7-bisphosphate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons D-glycero-beta-D-manno-heptose 1-phosphate + phosphate

The enzyme is involved in biosynthesis of ADP-L-glycero-beta-D-manno-heptose.
