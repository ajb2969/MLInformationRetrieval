S-methyl-5'-thioadenosine deaminase

S-methyl-5'-thioadenosine deaminase (, MTA deaminase, 5-methylthioadenosine deaminase) is an enzyme with system name S-methyl-5'-thioadenosine amidohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

S-methyl-5'-thioadenosine + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons 5'-S-methyl-5'-thioinosine + NH3

The enzyme from Thermotoga maritima also functions as S-adenosylhomocysteine deaminase (EC 3.5.4.28).
