2-oxo-3-(5-oxofuran-2-ylidene)propanoate lactonase

2-oxo-3-(5-oxofuran-2-ylidene)propanoate lactonase (, naaC (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 2-oxo-3-(5-oxofuran-2-ylidene)propanoate lactonohydrolase.1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

2-oxo-3-(5-oxofuran-2-ylidene)propanoate + H2O ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons maleylpyruvate

This enzyme is isolated from the soil bacterium Bradyrhizobium sp. JS329.
