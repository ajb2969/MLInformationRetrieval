Bril (unit)

The bril is an old, non-SI, unit of luminance. The SI unit of luminance is the candela per square metre.

Unit conversions

One bril is equal to:

  • 1 10 7 π 1 superscript 10 7 π \frac{1}{10^{7}\pi} candela per square metre
  • 10−4 skots
  • 10−11 lamberts
  • 10−7 apostilbs
  • 1 10 11 π 1 superscript 10 11 π \frac{1}{10^{11}\pi} stilbs (candela per square centimetre)
  • about 9.29×10−9 foot-lamberts
