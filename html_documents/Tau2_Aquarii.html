Tau2 Aquarii

For other star systems with this Bayer designation, see Tau Aquarii.

Tau2 Aquarii (τ2 Aqr, τ2 Aquarii) is the Bayer designation for a star in the equatorial constellation of Aquarius. It is visible to the naked eye with an apparent visual magnitude of +4.0.1 This is an orange-hued2 giant star with a stellar classification of K5 III.3 The measured angular diameter, after correction for limb darkening, is .4 At an estimated distance of based on parallax measurements,5 this yields a physical size of about 53 times the radius of the Sun.6
