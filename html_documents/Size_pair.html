Size pair

In size theory, a size pair is a pair ( M , φ ) M φ (M,\varphi) , where M M M is a compact topological space and φ : M → ℝ k normal-: φ normal-→ M superscript ℝ k \varphi:M\to\mathbb{R}^{k} is a continuous function.123 The function φ φ \varphi is called a measuring function.
