Labdatriene synthase

Labdatriene synthase (, OsKSL10 (gene)) is an enzyme with system name 9alpha-copalyl-diphosphate diphosphate-lyase ((12E)-9alpha-labda-8(17),12,14-triene-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

9alpha-copalyl diphosphate ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons (12E)-9alpha-labda-8(17),12,14-triene + diphosphate

The enzyme from rice (Oryza sativa) also produces ent-sandaracopimara-8(14),15-diene from ent-copalyl diphosphate.
