Benzil reductase ((S)-benzoin forming)

Benzil reductase ((S)-benzoin forming) (, YueD) is an enzyme with system name (S)-benzoin:NADP+ oxidoreductase.12 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(S)-benzoin + NADP+ ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons benzil + NADPH + H+

The enzyme also reduces 1-phenylpropane-1,2-dione.
