Ethylmalonyl-CoA decarboxylase

Ethylmalonyl-CoA decarboxylase () is an enzyme with system name (S)-ethylmalonyl-CoA carboxy-lyase (butanoyl-CoA-forming).1 This enzyme catalyses the following chemical reaction

(S)-ethylmalonyl-CoA ⇌ normal-⇌ \rightleftharpoons butanoyl-CoA + CO2

The vertebrate enzyme decarboxylates ethylmalonyl-CoA.
