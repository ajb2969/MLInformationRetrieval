Evanston, Indiana

Evanston is an unincorporated community in Huff Township, Spencer County, Indiana, United States.

The town's population is 715 and its land area is 64.68 km².

Geography

Evanston is located at at an elevation of 413 feet.

The nearest city is Tell City.
