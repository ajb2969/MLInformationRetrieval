Leo III

Leo III, Leon III, or Levon III may refer to:

Armenian kings
  • Leo III, King of Armenia (1287–1307), ruled from 1303–1307, with his uncle Hetum II as Regent
  • Leo II, King of Armenia, sometimes also referred to as Leo III. Ruled from 1269–1289, was the son of Hetum I.
People
  • Leo III the Isaurian (685–741), Byzantine emperor
  • Pope Leo III (d. 816), contemporary of Charlemagne
  • Leon III of Abkhazia, king of the Kingdom of Abkhazia (960–969), of the House of the Anosids (Anchabadze)
Other uses
  • Leo A, an irregular galaxy in the constellation of Leo
  • The Oath of Leo III, 16th-century painting about the 9th century Pope Leo III
  • LEO III, the early computer used for commercial business applications.
