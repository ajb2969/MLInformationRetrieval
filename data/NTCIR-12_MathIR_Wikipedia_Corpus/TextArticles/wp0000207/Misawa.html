Misawa

Misawa (written: 三沢 or 三澤) is a Japanese name and may refer to:

Surname

  • Mitsuharu Misawa (1962–2009), Japanese professional wrestler
  • Bastion Misawa, a fictional character also known by the given name Daichi
  • Junichi Misawa (born 1985), Japanese football player. He plays for F.C. Ryukyu
  • Keiichi Misawa (born 1988), Japanese football player
  • Koichi Misawa (born 1974), Japanese baseball pitcher who won a silver medal in the 1996 Summer Olympics. He also played for the Yomiuri Giants, Osaka Kintetsu Buffaloes, and the Tokyo Yakult Swallows in Nippon Professional Baseball from 1997 to 2005
  • Sachika Misawa, Japanese voice actress and singer from Yamanashi Prefecture who is affiliated with Style Cube. As a singer, she is signed to Warner Home Video
  • Shin Misawa, Japanese anime director and storyboard artist. He is on general staff at Studio Comet, and handles production and directing jobs

Fictional characters:

  • Bastion Misawa (Daichi Misawa) in Yu-Gi-Oh! GX

Places

  • Misawa, Aomori, a city located along the far North Pacific coastline of mainland Japan
  • Misawa Station, a railway station of Misawa, Aomori
  • Misawa Air Base, an American as well as a Japanese airbase
  • Misawa Airport
