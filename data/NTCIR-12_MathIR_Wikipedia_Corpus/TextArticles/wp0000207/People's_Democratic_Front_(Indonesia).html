People's Democratic Front (Indonesia)

People's Democratic Front (, FDR) was a short-lived united front of leftists in Indonesia, founded in February 1948.12 FDR included the Communist Party of Indonesia, the Socialist Party, Labour Party of Indonesia, SOBSI and Pesindo.34 The leader of FDR was Amir Sjarifuddin.5
