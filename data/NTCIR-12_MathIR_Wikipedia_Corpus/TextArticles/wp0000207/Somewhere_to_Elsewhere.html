Somewhere to Elsewhere

Somewhere to Elsewhere is the 14th studio album by American rock band Kansas, released in 2000. It is the band's last studio album to date. This album marks the reunion of the band's original 1970s lineup since 1980's Audio-Visions, along with Billy Greer, who joined Kansas with Power. Kerry Livgren composed all of the album's tracks, and he sings (which he does not normally do) on the hidden track "Geodesic Dome".
