Career

Born in Parma, Guatelli has played for Parma, Portsmouth, Oxford United, FC Zürich and FC Chiasso.234

He signed a two-year contract with Portsmouth in August 2004 following a trial with the club.5 He moved on loan to Oxford United in March 2006, along with fellow Portsmouth player Liam Horsted.6 He was one of 7 player released by Portsmouth in May 2006 following the expiry of their contracts.7
