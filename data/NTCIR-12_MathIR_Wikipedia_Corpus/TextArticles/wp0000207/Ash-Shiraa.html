Ash-Shiraa

Ash-Shiraa (or Al-Shiraa) (Arabic: الشراع| The Sail in English) is an Arabic weekly magazine published in Lebanon.1 The magazine is one of the oldest publications in the country.

History and profile

Ash-Shiraa was launched in 1948.2 It is described as an anti-Syrian publication3 and is based in Beirut.4

Content

The magazine was the first to report that the United States had been selling weapons to Iran, a scandal that was later revealed to have been part of an arms-for-hostages deal (see Iran-Contra Affair).5 The report was published in the magazine on 3 November 1986.6
