Sony Entertainment Television

Sony Entertainment Television (SET) is a series of general entertainment television channels, owned by Sony Pictures Television. At least 10 International versions of this channel exist:

  • Sony Entertainment Television Asia
  • Sony Entertainment Television (Baltic)
  • Sony Entertainment Television (India)
  • Sony Entertainment Television (Latin America)
  • Sony Entertainment Television (Portugal)
  • Sony Entertainment Television (Russia)
  • Sony Channel (South Africa)
  • Sony Channel (Asia)
  • Sony Entertainment Television (UK & Ireland)
  • Sony Entertainment Television (Germany)
