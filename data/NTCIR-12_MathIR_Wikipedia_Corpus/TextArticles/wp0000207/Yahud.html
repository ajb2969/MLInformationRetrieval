Yahud

Yahud may refer to:

  • Yehud (יהוד) is a city in Israel. It is located near the Ben Gurion Airport.
  • Yahud is a classical Arabic term which comes from a term used 11 times in the Quran to refer to descendants of Hud's community who had strayed into the worship 'Uzayr as the son of Allah. It is now ubiquitously nothing but the plural for Jew.
