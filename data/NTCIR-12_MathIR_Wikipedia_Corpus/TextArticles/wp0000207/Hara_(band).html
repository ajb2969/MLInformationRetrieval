Hara (band)

Hara is a Romanian pop-rock band from Cluj-Napoca. Its members during 2000-2002 were: Flavius Buzilă, Mihai Pop, Nicu Lichirie, Tudor Runcanu and Doru Dejeu. Since 2003 the members are: Flavius Buzilă, Mihai Pop, Nicu Lichirie, Marius Astilean and Vasi Nani.

History

Hara was founded in May 2000 in Cluj-Napoca, at the initiative of the English professor Flavius Buzila who proposed to bring a new breath, fresh sound in Romanian music. The group was noticed immediately because of its original creations, very inspired, combining with much naturalness elements of alternative rock, electro-pop, Celtic and Romanian folk sounds, with meaningful lyrics, deep messages played in a unique personal, interpretation.

Hara's current members are:

  • Flavius Buzila - vocals, guitar
  • Marius Astilean - violin
  • Radu Anghel - bass
  • Iulian Udrea - programming, vocals
  • Valentin Musat - guitar
  • Nunu Racris - drums
