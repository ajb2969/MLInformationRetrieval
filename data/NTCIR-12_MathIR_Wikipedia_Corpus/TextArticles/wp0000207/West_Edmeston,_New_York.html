West Edmeston, New York

West Edmeston is a hamlet on the Unadilla River on the border the Town of Brookfield in Madison County, New York and the Town of Edmeston in Otsego County, New York. On August 24, 1898, the new First Day Baptist Church building was opened in West Edmeston. First conceived in January 1897, the church was formally organized in February 1898 and admitted to the Otsego County Baptist Association.
