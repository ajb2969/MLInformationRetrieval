Jordon Zadorozny

Jordon Zadorozny (born c.1974 Pembroke, Ontario)12 is a Canadian rock musician.3 He is the singer and songwriter for indie rock band Blinker the Star.45 He is best known for cowriting songs with Courtney Love in the late 1990s. Zadorozny has also appeared on recordings by Melissa Auf der Maur,6 Sam Roberts,7 Tara Slone8 and Mandy Moore.9 He produced countless bands in the Ontario area in his French Kiss Studio. As of 2007, Jordon is a member of Canadian indie band "Abbey" with Sofia Silva10 and transatlantic pop act SheLoom with Filippo Gaetani and Eric Matthews.
