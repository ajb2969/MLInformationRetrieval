Ivone Ramos

Ivone Aida Lopes Fernandes Ramos (born July 9, 1926 in Santa Catarina on the island of Santiago, Cape Verde) is a Cape Verdean writer who lives in Mindelo.12

Her father was Armando Napoleão Rodrigues Fernandes. She re-edited her father's book Léxico do dialecto crioulo do arquipélago de Cabo Verde in 1990 in Mindelo.3

Bibliography

  • Vidas Vividas (Short-stories in Portuguese, 1990)
  • Futcera ta cendê na Rotcha (Short-stories in Portuguese, 2000)
  • A exilada (2005)4
  • Mambia tita contá história na criol (Children's story book, 2009)56
  • Short-story Capotóna (Crioulo of São Vicente) published in the book Futcera ta cendê na Rotcha
